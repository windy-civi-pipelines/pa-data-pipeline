2025-2026 Regular Session SR 115 PN 0884 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 884 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.115 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY VOGEL, BROOKS, BARTOLOTTA, SCHWANK, COMITTA, J. WARD, PHILLIPS-HILL, MASTRIANO, PENNYCUICK, FONTANA, BROWN, SANTARSIERO, CULVER, ARGALL, MARTIN, COSTA, LANGERHOLC, CAPPELLETTI, DUSH, STEFANO AND TARTAGLIONE, JUNE 3, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, JUNE 3, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of June 2025 as "Dairy Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The dairy industry is a vital sector of Pennsylvania's agricultural industry; andWHEREAS, Dairy farmers are established contributors to our communities; andWHEREAS, Dairy farming is an integral part of Pennsylvania's agriculture and overall economy, generating $11.8 billion of economic activity annually; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania ranks second in the nation in terms of the number of dairy farms; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania ranks eighth nationally in milk production, and this Commonwealth's 468,000 cows produce nearly 10 billion pounds of milk annually; andWHEREAS, The dairy industry supports more than 47,000 jobs across this Commonwealth; and12345678910111213141516 WHEREAS, Ninety-nine percent of the dairy farms in this Commonwealth are family-owned; andWHEREAS, Dairy farmers, their families and other dairy industry employees should be recognized and celebrated; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate designate the month of June 2025 as "Dairy Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate bring awareness to the current hardships facing the dairy industry in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the immense value that Pennsylvania's dairy industry injects into our State and local economies; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate applaud the hard work and resiliency of our dairy farmers and their families; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate promote the purchase and consumption of the high-quality dairy products produced here at home in Pennsylvania.20250SR0115PN0884 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819
