2025-2026 Regular Session HR 277 PN 2060 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2060 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.277 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY TWARDZIK, GREINER, STENDER, STAMBAUGH, BRENNAN, PICKETT, JAMES, BARTON, ROWE, STEHR AND NEILSON, JUNE 30, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS, JUNE 30, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating August 16, 2025, as "Pottsville Maroons Championship Day" in Pennsylvania in honor of the team's 1925 championship victory.WHEREAS, The Pottsville Maroons, a professional football team from Pottsville, Pennsylvania, achieved remarkable success during the 1925 National Football League (NFL) season, finishing with an impressive record of 10 wins, 2 losses and 0 ties; andWHEREAS, On December 6, 1925, the Pottsville Maroons defeated the Chicago Cardinals in a pivotal game widely regarded as the de facto NFL championship, securing the team's place as one of the premier teams in the early years of professional football; andWHEREAS, The Maroons further solidified their legacy by defeating the Notre Dame All-Stars, a team composed of celebrated college football players featuring the famous Four Horsemen, in an exhibition game that showcased the growing prowess of the NFL and its ability to compete with and surpass the more popular college football teams of the era; and123456789101112131415161718 WHEREAS, The 1925 Pottsville Maroons, under the leadership of players such as Tony Latone, Herb Stein, Charlie Berry and Walter French, and coached by Dick Rauch, exemplified teamwork, resilience and athletic excellence, bringing pride to the City of Pottsville and Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, The Maroons' achievements in 1925 played a significant role in elevating the visibility and credibility of professional football, contributing to the growth of the NFL as a major American sports institution; andWHEREAS, The legacy of the 1925 Pottsville Maroons continues to inspire athletes, fans and communities across Pennsylvania, serving as a testament to the enduring spirit of determination and the importance of recognizing the contributions of early professional football teams; andWHEREAS, This year marks the 100th anniversary of the team's championship victory; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania designate August 16, 2025, as "Pottsville Maroons Championship Day" in Pennsylvania in honor of the team's 1925 championship victory; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania recognize the Pottsville Maroons' contributions to the development of professional football and the team's role in showcasing Pennsylvania's proud sports heritage; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the Pottsville Area Historical Society and the Pro Football Hall of Fame to commemorate the enduring legacy of the 1925 Pottsville Maroons.20250HR0277PN2060 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526272829
