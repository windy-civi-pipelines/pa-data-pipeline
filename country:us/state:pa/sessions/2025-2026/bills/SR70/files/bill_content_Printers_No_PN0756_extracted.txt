WARD, STEFANO, BAKER AND MILLER, MAY 6, 2025 INTRODUCED AND ADOPTED, MAY 6, 2025 A RESOLUTIONHonoring the life and extending condolences to the family of Officer Andrew William Duarte.WHEREAS, Officer Andrew William Duarte gave his life in the line of duty on February 22, 2025, at the age of 30; andWHEREAS, Born in Oakland, California, on February 17, 1995, Officer Duarte was the son of Gary Duarte and Thelma Valdez and Nancy Underhill Duarte Matarese and David Matarese; andWHEREAS, A 2013 graduate of Berean Christian High School, Officer Duarte earned a bachelor's degree in criminal justice/police science from the California University of Pennsylvania in 2016; andWHEREAS, Officer Duarte began his career in law enforcement as a seasonal officer with the Ocean City Police Department in Maryland during the summer of 2016; andWHEREAS, Officer Duarte then worked for the Denver Police Department in Colorado from 2017 to 2022, during which time he 12345678910111213141516 graduated as a Drug Recognition Expert, received the highest test score recorded in the nationally recognized class and was lauded as the youngest police officer to achieve the certification in the State of Colorado at the time; andWHEREAS, In 2021, Officer Duarte was recognized as the Mothers Against Drunk Driving Colorado Hero of the Year for his dedication and enforcement of impaired driving; andWHEREAS, For the past three years, Officer Duarte was a member of the West York Borough Police Department and in his free time, he enjoyed taking photographs, playing guitar, hiking, traveling and spending time with his family; andWHEREAS, Since his enlistment, Officer Duarte faithfully endeavored to preserve and protect the health, safety and welfare of his fellow citizens; andWHEREAS, In the finest hour of public service, Officer Duarte demonstrated exemplary knowledge, ability and integrity in carrying out his many responsibilities and earned deep respect and gratitude for his tireless devotion to duty; andWHEREAS, Officer Andrew William Duarte's life greatly benefited all those who lived, served and worked with him; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate honor the life and extend condolences to the family of Officer Andrew William Duarte.20250SR0070PN0756 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
