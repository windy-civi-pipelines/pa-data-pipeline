2025-2026 Regular Session SR 47 PN 0393 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 393 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.47 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MARTIN, PHILLIPS-HILL, HUTCHINSON, FONTANA, ROBINSON AND LAUGHLIN, MARCH 17, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, MARCH 17, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating March 8, 2025, as "PIAA Wrestling Day of Champions" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Wrestling instills at a young age and continues to teach athletes at all levels key life skills such as self-reliance, self-discipline and teamwork, all of which apply to one's growth in professional development; andWHEREAS, Wrestling promotes both a physically healthy lifestyle and mental growth; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania has a rich history of and unrivaled passion for wrestling as a sport; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania is home to elite wrestling programs that exist at all age and experience levels of the sport; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania's wrestlers participate at all stages of the sport from professional, international competitors to collegiate-level competitors under the guidance of the Pennsylvania Interscholastic Athletic Association (PIAA) and even younger with local clubs and associations; andWHEREAS, Participation in wrestling has grown in all age 123456789101112131415161718 groups and levels of the sport; andWHEREAS, According to the National Federation of State High School Associations, participation in wrestling reached a record 356,131 participants nationwide during the 2023-2024 season; andWHEREAS, Boys' wrestling participation grew to 259,431 and girls' wrestling participation grew to 64,257 during the 2023-2024 season; andWHEREAS, The record increase in high school wrestling represents an approximately 16.5% growth in participation year over year; andWHEREAS, The growth in participation can also be attributed to the increase in female participation at all levels of the sport; andWHEREAS, Girls' wrestling has been the fastest-growing high school sport in the country over the past five years; andWHEREAS, The second PIAA Championship Playoff with both boys and girls wrestling in two distinct championship systems is held March 6 through 8, 2025; andWHEREAS, Participation in wrestling continues to grow and leads to invaluable opportunities to compete and succeed in life; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate designate March 8, 2025, as "PIAA Wrestling Day of Champions" in Pennsylvania.20250SR0047PN0393 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
