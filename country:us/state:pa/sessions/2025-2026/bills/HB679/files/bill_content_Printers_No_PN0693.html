AN ACT
Amending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An
act relating to the public school system, including certain
provisions applicable as well to private and parochial
schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the
laws relating thereto," in certification of teachers, further
providing for continuing professional development.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 1205.1(c.2) of the act of March 10, 1949
(P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is
amended to read:
Section 1205.1. Continuing Professional Development.--* * *
(c.2) (1) A professional education plan may include joint
or cooperative professional education activities with another
school entity or an institution of higher education.
(2) The department shall provide professional education
opportunities, resources and guidance for school entities to
work collaboratively under this subsection. The professional
education opportunities under this paragraph shall address the
most frequent concerns from school employes across this
