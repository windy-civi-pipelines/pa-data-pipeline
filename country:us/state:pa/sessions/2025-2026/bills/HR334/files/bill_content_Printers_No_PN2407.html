A RESOLUTION
Remembering the distinguished life and service of the Honorable
Ralph D. Pratt, former six-term member of the House of
Representatives, who died August 18, 2025, in New Castle,
Lawrence County, at 85 years of age.
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives joins in commending
Ralph D. Pratt's talents as a research chemist and chemical
engineer, including his work on behalf of the Gemini space
program; and
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives honors Mr. Pratt's
contributions to State government as an assistant attorney
general, labor relations coordinator and legislator; and
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives notes Mr. Pratt's
dedication to our legal and judicial systems as an attorney and
judge; and
WHEREAS, Elected to the House of Representatives from the
10th District, Mr. Pratt represented Lawrence and Mercer
Counties from 1975 through 1982 and Beaver, Lawrence and Mercer
Counties from 1983 through January 6, 1986; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Pratt was appointed to the Joint Legislative Air

WHEREAS, Mr. Pratt's early study of statutory law and legal
