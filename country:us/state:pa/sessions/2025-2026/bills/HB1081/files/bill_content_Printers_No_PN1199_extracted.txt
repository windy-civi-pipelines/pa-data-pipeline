2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1081 PN 1199 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1199 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1081 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY PROKOPIAK, BOROWSKI, BRENNAN, SMITH-WADE-EL, PIELLI, MADDEN, PROBST, SANCHEZ, SCHLOSSBERG, ABNEY, NEILSON, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, RIVERA, D. WILLIAMS AND GREEN, APRIL 1, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, APRIL 1, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in charter schools, providing for moratorium on buildings.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 1750.1-A. Moratorium on buildings. (a) Cyber charter school.--Beginning on the effective date of this subsection, a cyber charter school may not own, purchase or lease property.(b) Educational management service provider.--Beginning on the effective date of this subsection, an educational management service provider may not own, purchase or lease property on behalf of a cyber charter school.12345678910111213141516171819 (c) Applicability.--Subsections (a) and (b) shall not apply to property owned, purchased or leased on or before the effective date of this subsection.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1081PN1199 - 2 - 1234
