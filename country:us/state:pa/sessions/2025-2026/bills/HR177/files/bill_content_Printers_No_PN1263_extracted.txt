2025-2026 Regular Session HR 177 PN 1263 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1263 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.177 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MIHALEK, STENDER, HADDOCK AND ARMANINI, APRIL 4, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON ENVIRONMENTAL AND NATURAL RESOURCE PROTECTION, APRIL 4, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of April 2025 as "Low Head Dam Public Safety Awareness Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The spring is a time when Pennsylvanians want to be outdoors, and they often enjoy the plentiful recreation opportunities on our waterways; andWHEREAS, Across our waterways can be found low head dams, which are built in a river or stream channel, extend fully across the banks, and are designed and built so that water flows continuously over the crest from bank-to-bank; andWHEREAS, Low head dams are referred to as drowning machines because of the dangerous, lethal current they can create; andWHEREAS, April has been recognized nationally as "Low Head Dam Public Safety Awareness Month" by the American Society of Civil Engineers; andWHEREAS, To protect anyone who is using our waterways, dedicating a month will help remind people of the risks of low head dams; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Spreading awareness and education of low head dam dangers will help save lives; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of April 2025 as "Low Head Dam Public Safety Awareness Month" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0177PN1263 - 2 - 12345
