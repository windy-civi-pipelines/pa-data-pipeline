AN ACT
Amending the act of April 6, 1951 (P.L.69, No.20), entitled "An
act relating to the rights, obligations and liabilities of
landlord and tenant and of parties dealing with them and
amending, revising, changing and consolidating the law
relating thereto," in recovery of possession, further
providing for escrow funds limited.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 511.1 of the act of April 6, 1951
(P.L.69, No.20), known as The Landlord and Tenant Act of 1951,
is amended by adding a subsection to read:
Section 511.1. Escrow Funds Limited.--* * *
(d.1) A lease or rental agreement may not contain a
provision which characterizes a security deposit, including a
deposit or fee for a pet, as nonrefundable.
* * *
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
