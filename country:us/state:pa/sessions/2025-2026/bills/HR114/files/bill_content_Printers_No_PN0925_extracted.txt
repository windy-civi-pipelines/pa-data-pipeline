2025-2026 Regular Session HR 114 PN 0925 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 925 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.114 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY PARKER, BRENNAN, GIRAL, PROBST, VENKAT, FREEMAN, ISAACSON, HOHENSTEIN, ABNEY, HILL-EVANS, HOWARD AND STEELE, MARCH 11, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, MARCH 11, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the week of March 10 through 14, 2025, as "Civic Learning Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, A well-informed and engaged citizenry are essential to sustaining a healthy democracy; andWHEREAS, After local observances in Massachusetts, Rhode Island and New York in 2022, "Civic Learning Week" became a national observance in 2023; andWHEREAS, "Civic Learning Week" is observed across the nation annually as a week designed to highlight the knowledge, skills and dispositions that provide the foundation for an informed and engaged citizenry, and to call for individual, local, state and national action to prioritize civic education; andWHEREAS, It is essential that each new generation is equipped to engage in our democracy for our civic strength, our economy, our national security and more; andWHEREAS, Civic education is vital to the preservation and improvement of the constitutional government of the United 1234567891011121314151617 States; andWHEREAS, This year's "Civic Learning Week" theme, "Making civic learning a nationwide priority for a stronger democracy," is a worthwhile goal for the people and learning institutions of this Commonwealth; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the week of March 10 through 14, 2025, as "Civic Learning Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the vital role civic education plays in preparing the next generation for democratic engagement.20250HR0114PN0925 - 2 - 1234567891011
