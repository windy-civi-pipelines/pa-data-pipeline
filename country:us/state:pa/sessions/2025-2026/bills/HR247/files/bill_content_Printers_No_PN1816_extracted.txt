2025-2026 Regular Session HR 247 PN 1816 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1816 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.247 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY McANDREW, VENKAT, GIRAL, KHAN, COOPER, PROBST, MALAGARI, DONAHUE, SANCHEZ AND CERRATO, JUNE 2, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, JUNE 3, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of June 2025 as "Golf Tourism Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, This Commonwealth is home to more than 500 golf courses, ranging from historic clubs to modern public courses, offering diverse experiences for golfers of all skill levels; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth's golf industry significantly contributes to the State's economy, supporting local businesses, creating jobs and attracting visitors from across the nation and around the world; andWHEREAS, Golf tourism promotes outdoor recreation, encourages healthy lifestyles and showcases this Commonwealth's scenic landscapes and hospitality; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth has produced many distinguished professional golfers who have elevated the game and brought pride to this Commonwealth, including Arnold Palmer of Latrobe, a legendary figure known as "The King," whose impact on golf 1234567891011121314151617 remains unmatched; Jim Furyk of West Chester, a United States Open Champion and Ryder Cup captain; and Betsy King of Reading, a Hall of Fame Ladies Professional Golf Association golfer with 34 tour wins, whose achievements have inspired generations of athletes; andWHEREAS, Designating the month of June 2025 as "Golf Tourism Month" highlights the importance of golf to this Commonwealth's tourism sector and encourages residents and visitors alike to explore the State's golfing opportunities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of June 2025 as "Golf Tourism Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage Pennsylvanians and visitors to participate in golf-related activities and support local golf courses and related businesses throughout the month.20250HR0247PN1816 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516
