WILLIAMS, JUNE 2, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, JUNE 2, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of June 2025 as "Caribbean-American Heritage Month."WHEREAS, In 2006, the President of the United States signed the first proclamation recognizing June as "Caribbean-American Heritage Month" in the United States; andWHEREAS, Our nation thrives from the contributions of Caribbean Americans, who enrich our society and add to the strength of our nation; andWHEREAS, Caribbean Americans have been leaders in government, sports, entertainment, the arts and many other fields; andWHEREAS, Our great Commonwealth thrives from the contributions of Caribbean Americans; andWHEREAS, During the month of June and through the remainder of the year, the residents of Pennsylvania should honor the friendship between our Commonwealth, our nation and the Caribbean countries; andWHEREAS, With common values and history, we celebrate the 1234567891011121314151617 rich Caribbean heritage and the many contributions of Caribbean Americans; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of June 2025 as "Caribbean-American Heritage Month."20250HR0246PN1803 - 2 - 1234
