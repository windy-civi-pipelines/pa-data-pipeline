WILLIAMS, PISCIOTTANO, KANE, TARTAGLIONE, MALONE, COMITTA, BROWN, HAYWOOD, COSTA, FONTANA, VOGEL AND HUTCHINSON, SEPTEMBER 17, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, SEPTEMBER 17, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of September 2025 as "National Preparedness Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, September has been designated by the Federal Emergency Management Agency as "National Preparedness Month" to remind all Americans to prepare themselves and their families now and throughout the year for disasters; andWHEREAS, Disasters and emergencies can strike without warning, posing significant threats to life, property and community well-being; andWHEREAS, Residents can take simple steps to decrease their risk of illness and injury when faced with an emergency, be it manmade or natural; andWHEREAS, Preparedness measures include establishing family evacuation and communication plans, creating financial and medical readiness strategies, determining how to shelter in place and considering the unique needs of children, seniors, individuals with disabilities and pets; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Residents are encouraged to review insurance coverage, maintain essential supplies and safeguard financial and medical records in case of crisis; andWHEREAS, A well-prepared citizenry strengthens the resilience of this Commonwealth, reduces risk of injury and loss and supports faster recovery from disasters; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the month of September 2025 as "National Preparedness Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That residents of this Commonwealth be encouraged to take proactive steps to prepare themselves, their families and their communities and to recognize the work of emergency managers, first responders and organizations dedicated to protecting lives and property.20250SR0154PN1171 - 2 - 1234567891011121314
