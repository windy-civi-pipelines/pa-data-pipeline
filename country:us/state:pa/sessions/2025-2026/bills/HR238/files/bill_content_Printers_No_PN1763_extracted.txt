2025-2026 Regular Session HR 238 PN 1763 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1763 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.238 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CIRESI, MUSTELLO, ARMANINI, T. DAVIS, VENKAT, HILL-EVANS, RABB, PICKETT, FREEMAN, BRENNAN, SANCHEZ, NEILSON AND SCHMITT, MAY 29, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, MAY 29, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of June 2025 as "Italian-American Heritage Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Italians were among the first explorers to discover the American continents and map their geography; andWHEREAS, The term "America" is derived from the name of Italian explorer and navigator Amerigo Vespucci; andWHEREAS, Italian Americans have defended the liberty and integrity of the United States since the Revolutionary War; andWHEREAS, The phrase in the Declaration of Independence, "all men are created equal," was paraphrased from the writings of Italian immigrant and American patriot Filippo Mazzei; andWHEREAS, An estimated 5.5 million Italians immigrated to the United States between 1820 and 2004; andWHEREAS, Today there are approximately 18 million Americans of Italian descent in the United States, making them the fourth-largest ethnic group in this country; andWHEREAS, The Italian-American community has strong roots in 1234567891011121314151617 Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, The City of Philadelphia remains the second-largest Italian-American community in this country; andWHEREAS, The recent 2023 American Community Survey reported that approximately 1.4 million Pennsylvanians have Italian ancestry; andWHEREAS, Italian Americans have made and continue to make important contributions to this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, On June 2 of each year, Italian Americans, Italians and individuals of Italian descent around the world celebrate Festa della Repubblica, or Republic Day, Italy's National Day that commemorates the 1946 referendum ending the monarchy in Italy and establishing a republic; andWHEREAS, The month of June also marks the anniversaries of the founding of two notable Italian-American organizations, the Order Sons and Daughters of Italy in America on June 22 and the Italian American Heritage Foundation on June 29; andWHEREAS, These observances have previously led the Congress of the United States and state and local governments to commemorate Italian-American heritage by declaring "National Italian-American Heritage Week," "National Italian-American Day," "Italian Heritage Day," "Italian-American Day" and other days in June; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of June 2025 as "Italian-American Heritage Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the significant contributions that Italian Americans have made to this Commonwealth and the United States.20250HR0238PN1763 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526272829
