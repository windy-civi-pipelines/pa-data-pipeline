2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1808 PN 2223 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2223 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1808 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BURNS, GUZMAN, KUZMA AND HADDOCK, AUGUST 15, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, AUGUST 19, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in public assistance, further providing for workfare program.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 405.2 of the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), known as the Human Services Code, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 405.2. Workfare Program.--* * *(a.1) Within six months of the effective date of this subsection, the department shall establish a workfare pilot program in counties of the fourth and fifth class in accordance with the following:(1) An individual receiving cash assistance under Temporary Assistance for Needy Families may receive benefits while working for an employer for a forty-hour work week and receiving pay equivalent to twenty hours of work.12345678910111213141516171819 (2) After the individual has worked for the employer for six months, the individual may continue to receive up to seventy percent of their cash assistance and shall be paid for thirty hours for working a forty-hour work week.(3) After the individual has worked for the employer for twelve months, the individual shall be paid for a full forty hours of work for working a forty-hour work week, and the department shall terminate the cash assistance payments to the individual.(4) The applicable provisions of this section shall apply to the workfare pilot program.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1808PN2223 - 2 - 12345678910111213
