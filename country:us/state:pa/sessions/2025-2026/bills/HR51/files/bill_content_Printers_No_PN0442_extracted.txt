2025-2026 Regular Session HR 51 PN 0442 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 442 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.51 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BURGOS, GUZMAN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, KENYATTA, VENKAT, BRENNAN, MAYES, KHAN, GUENST, SCHLOSSBERG, A. BROWN, GIRAL, McNEILL, SANCHEZ, PROBST, HOHENSTEIN, PIELLI, HILL-EVANS, DONAHUE, HADDOCK, DELLOSO, MERSKI, FREEMAN, SAMUELSON, O'MARA, STEELE, PASHINSKI AND GREEN, FEBRUARY 3, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS, FEBRUARY 3, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing February 27, 2025, as "Dominican Republic Independence Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, On February 27, 2025, the Dominican Republic is celebrating the anniversary of its independence from Haiti in 1844; andWHEREAS, Before the Dominican Republic declared its independence, the shared island of Hispaniola had been under Haitian rule for more than two decades after centuries of colonialism, first by the Spanish and then by the French; andWHEREAS, Nationalist Juan Pablo Duarte helped organize the war against the Haitian regime and is considered one of the Dominican Republic's founding fathers; andWHEREAS, "Dominican Republic Independence Day" coincides with Carnaval, which is celebrated the entire month of February with parades, competitions and spirited events; andWHEREAS, The Dominican Republic is an important partner to 12345678910111213141516 the United States in hemispheric affairs due to its standing as the Caribbean's second-largest economy and third-largest country in terms of population and land mass, its large bilateral trade with the United States and its proximity to the United States and other Caribbean nations; andWHEREAS, The family unit within Dominican society is incredibly important and has formed the basis of stability throughout all of the Dominican Republic's political and economic upheaval; andWHEREAS, Whenever possible, the extended family, which usually encompasses three generations, lives within the same vicinity; andWHEREAS, Family loyalty is a deeply ingrained characteristic of the Dominican people; andWHEREAS, The Dominican Republic is well known as the homeland of much of Major League Baseball, but it is also known for fashion designer Oscar de la Renta, "Orange is The New Black" actress Dascha Polanco, Maryland State Delegate Joseline Peña-Melnyk, NASA aerospace engineer and inventor Dr. Victor Carreño, Pulitzer Prize-winning novelist Junot Díaz and countless other accomplished individuals; andWHEREAS, Dominicans are the fifth-largest population of Hispanic origin living in the United States; andWHEREAS, The Dominican-American community contributes to our nation's economic vitality and culture while maintaining a deep connection with its Dominican roots; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize February 27, 2025, as "Dominican Republic Independence Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0051PN0442 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526272829
