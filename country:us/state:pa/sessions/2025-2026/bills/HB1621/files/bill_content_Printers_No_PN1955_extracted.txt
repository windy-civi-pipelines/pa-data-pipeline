2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1621 PN 1955 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1955 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1621 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BRENNAN, GIRAL, HILL-EVANS, PIELLI, PROBST, KHAN, MAYES, RABB, GUZMAN, SANCHEZ, SAPPEY, WAXMAN, RIVERA, HARKINS AND D. WILLIAMS, JUNE 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LABOR AND INDUSTRY, JUNE 17, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 2, 1915 (P.L.736, No.338), entitled "An act defining the liability of an employer to pay damages for injuries received by an employe in the course of employment; establishing an elective schedule of compensation; providing procedure for the determination of liability and compensation thereunder; and prescribing penalties," in procedure, providing for multilingual informational addendum.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of June 2, 1915 (P.L.736, No.338), known as the Workers' Compensation Act, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 452. (a) The department shall develop and make available an informational addendum for each form issued, used, referenced or relied upon under this act for the suspension, modification or termination of compensation benefits. The addendum shall be made available in English, Spanish and any additional language determined by the department to be in significant use within this Commonwealth.(b) The informational addendum shall include, at a minimum, 1234567891011121314151617181920 the following:(1) A statement advising that important rights under this act, including the right to wage loss benefits and medical benefits, may be affected by inaction and that interpretation and translation services are available.(2) Contact information for accessing language assistance services, including a toll-free telephone number, email address, mailing address and any other means of communication deemed appropriate by the department.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1621PN1955 - 2 - 12345678910
