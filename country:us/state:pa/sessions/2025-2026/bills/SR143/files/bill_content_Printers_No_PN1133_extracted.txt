2025-2026 Regular Session SR 143 PN 1133 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1133 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.143 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY VOGEL, CAPPELLETTI, MALONE, MARTIN, SCHWANK, CULVER, KANE, FONTANA, BARTOLOTTA, TARTAGLIONE, COMITTA, HUGHES AND HAYWOOD, SEPTEMBER 4, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, SEPTEMBER 4, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of September 2025 as "Hunger Action Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, September 2025 has been designated nationwide as "Hunger Action Month" to raise awareness of the issue of hunger and mobilize the public to help end hunger in America; andWHEREAS, According to Feeding America, one in eight Pennsylvanians and one in six children are food insecure; andWHEREAS, Food insecurity, the condition of limited or uncertain access to adequate food, is associated with numerous adverse social and health outcomes and is a critical public health issue; andWHEREAS, Children who experience hunger have higher levels of chronic illness, anxiety, depression and behavioral problems than children receiving a well-balanced diet; andWHEREAS, Food-insecure older adults are at increased risk of a range of physical and mental health conditions and hospitalization; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Food insecurity is not discerning of whether Pennsylvanians reside in urban or rural communities; andWHEREAS, Current inflation rates for essentials are compounding the economic challenges for many Pennsylvanians; andWHEREAS, Hunger relief organizations, food banks and food pantries throughout this Commonwealth are uniquely positioned to help struggling individuals and families make ends meet and are critical lifelines for more than 1.5 million Pennsylvanians annually but are increasingly stretched thin; andWHEREAS, The fight against hunger requires engaging a wide range of public and private entities, including policymakers, business leaders, community activists, concerned citizens and food-serving organizations; andWHEREAS, Food banks, food rescue organizations, soup kitchens, food pantries, faith-based organizations, businesses and individuals are donating time, raising funds and giving food to help their neighbors; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the month of September 2025 as "Hunger Action Month" in Pennsylvania.20250SR0143PN1133 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819
