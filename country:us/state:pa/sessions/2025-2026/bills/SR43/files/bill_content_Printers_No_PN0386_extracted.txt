2025-2026 Regular Session SR 43 PN 0386 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 386 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.43 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SCHWANK, COMITTA, KANE, FONTANA, HAYWOOD, HUGHES, COSTA, VOGEL, STREET, CAPPELLETTI AND STEFANO, MARCH 17, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, MARCH 17, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating March 13, 2025, as "Nutrition and Dietetics Technician, Registered Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, are trained in food and nutrition and are an integral part of health care and food service management teams; andWHEREAS, Nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, have earned an associate's degree or a bachelor's degree, completed an extensive accredited nutrition and dietetics program and passed a national examination; andWHEREAS, Nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, receive training in nutrition and diet therapy, medical nutrition therapy, food safety and sanitation, food management systems and meal management and preparation; andWHEREAS, The specialized education and experience that they receive make nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, greatly valuable to the nutrition and dietetics profession; andWHEREAS, Nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, work throughout our communities in a variety of employment 123456789101112131415161718 settings, including hospitals, nursing homes, schools, restaurants, public health agencies, Meals on Wheels and community health programs and weight management clinics, among others; andWHEREAS, In these various settings, nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, educate clients on the connection between food, fitness and health, oversee food service sanitation and safety, develop menus and work with registered dietitian nutritionists in providing medical nutrition therapy; andWHEREAS, The Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics recognizes the month of March 2025 as "National Nutrition Month®" and celebrates March 13, 2025, as "Nutrition and Dietetics Technician, Registered Day"; andWHEREAS, There are 216 nutrition dietetics technicians, registered, in this Commonwealth as of 2021 and more than 3,700 members of the Pennsylvania Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, which is an affiliate of the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, and advocates for the promotion of optimal nutrition, health and well-being in this Commonwealth; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate designate March 13, 2025, as "Nutrition and Dietetics Technician, Registered Day" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate encourage all Pennsylvanians to recognize the contributions of nutrition and dietetics technicians, registered, and express appreciation for their commitment to promoting science-based nutrition in the hope of achieving optimal health for both today and tomorrow.20250SR0043PN0386 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122232425262728
