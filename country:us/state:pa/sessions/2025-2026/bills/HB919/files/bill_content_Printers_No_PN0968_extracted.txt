2025-2026 Regular Session HB 919 PN 0968 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 968 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.919 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MALAGARI, GIRAL, HILL-EVANS, PIELLI, HANBIDGE, SANCHEZ, WARREN, FREEMAN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, O'MARA, WEBSTER, DEASY, CERRATO AND GREEN, MARCH 17, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGING AND OLDER ADULT SERVICES, MARCH 17, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of May 26, 2017 (P.L.6, No.3), entitled "An act providing for participation in the REAL ID Act of 2005, for compliance by residents of this Commonwealth, for reports to General Assembly and for publication; and repealing the REAL ID Nonparticipation Act," providing for exemption from fees.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of May 26, 2017 (P.L.6, No.3), known as the Pennsylvania REAL ID Compliance Act, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 3.1. Exemption from fees.(a) Exemption.--An eligible applicant who is at least 65 years of age shall be exempt from all fees related to the administration of this act and may only be required to pay the costs of a standard-issued driver's license or photo identification card.(b) Fees.--The department shall establish reasonable and appropriate fees in order to administer this section.123456789101112131415161718 Section 2. Any regulations inconsistent with this act are abrogated to the extent of any inconsistency with this act.Section 3. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0919PN0968 - 2 - 123
