2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1821 PN 2241 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2241 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1821 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KEPHART, JAMES, VITALI, GUENST, BARGER, COOK, SMITH, CAUSER AND STAATS, AUGUST 25, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, AUGUST 25, 2025 AN ACTDesignating a bridge, identified as Bridge Key 69488, carrying State Route 3005 over North Witmer Run in Irvona Borough, Clearfield County, as the World War II Veteran Pvt. Wilbur Russell Rea Memorial Bridge.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. World War II Veteran Pvt. Wilbur Russell Rea Memorial Bridge.(a) Findings.--The General Assembly finds and declares as follows:(1) Wilbur Russell Rea, born in Irvona on May 1, 1924, was the son of Herbert Bloom Rea and Mary Elizabeth Bone Rea.(2) Pvt. Rea graduated from Coalport Irvona High School.(3) Pvt. Rea enjoyed working on cars and farm equipment.(4) Pvt. Rea entered the service under the United States Army's 96th Division, Company B, 381st Infantry.(5) Pvt. Rea was killed in action on April 16, 1945, in Okinawa, Japan.(b) Designation.--The bridge, identified as Bridge Key 12345678910111213141516171819 69488, carrying State Route 3005 over North Witmer Run in Irvona Borough, Clearfield County, is designated as the World War II Veteran Pvt. Wilbur Russell Rea Memorial Bridge.(c) Signs.--The Department of Transportation shall erect and maintain appropriate signs displaying the name of the bridge to traffic in both directions on the bridge.Section 2. Effective date.This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1821PN2241 - 2 - 12345678
