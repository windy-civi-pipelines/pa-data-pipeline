AN ACT
Designating a bridge, identified as Bridge Key 69488, carrying
State Route 3005 over North Witmer Run in Irvona Borough,
Clearfield County, as the World War II Veteran Pvt. Wilbur
Russell Rea Memorial Bridge.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. World War II Veteran Pvt. Wilbur Russell Rea
Memorial Bridge.
(a) Findings.--The General Assembly finds and declares as
follows:
(1) Wilbur Russell Rea, born in Irvona on May 1, 1924,
was the son of Herbert Bloom Rea and Mary Elizabeth Bone Rea.
(2) Pvt. Rea graduated from Coalport Irvona High School.
(3) Pvt. Rea enjoyed working on cars and farm equipment.
(4) Pvt. Rea entered the service under the United States
Army's 96th Division, Company B, 381st Infantry.
(5) Pvt. Rea was killed in action on April 16, 1945, in
Okinawa, Japan.
(b) Designation.--The bridge, identified as Bridge Key
