WILLIAMS AND DELLOSO, MAY 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGING AND OLDER ADULT SERVICES, MAY 14, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 7 (Banks and Banking) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in mortgage loan industry licensing and consumer protection, further providing for general requirements.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 6121 of Title 7 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a paragraph to read:ยง 6121. General requirements.A licensee shall do all of the following:* * *(16) If the licensee accepts applications for reverse mortgage loans:(i) Provide in-person , face-to-face housing counseling to an applicant from a housing counseling agency that is approved by the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development in this Commonwealth. The housing counseling agency shall provide advice on a 123456789101112131415161718 reverse mortgage loan and alternatives to a reverse mortgage loan.(ii) If an applicant is unable or chooses not to schedule an in-person, face-to-face visit with a housing counselor by either traveling to a housing counseling agency or having an in-home visit from a counselor, conduct the housing counseling over the telephone or through video teleconference.(iii) After completion of the housing counseling, issue to the applicant a certificate that contains the following information:(A) The signatures of the applicant and the housing counselor who provided the service.(B) The date on which the housing counseling services were provided.(C) The name, address and telephone number of both the applicant who received counseling and the organization that provided the counseling.(iv) Maintain the certificate issued under subparagraph (iii) for the duration of the term of the loan.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1466PN1717 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
