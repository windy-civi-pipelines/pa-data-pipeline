AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for
vehicles exempt from registration; and, in inspection of
vehicles, further providing for operation of vehicle without
official certificate of inspection.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Sections 1302 and 4703(b) of Title 75 of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes are amended by adding a
paragraph to read:
§ 1302. Vehicles exempt from registration.
The following types of vehicles are exempt from registration:
* * *
(2.3) A commercial implement of husbandry being operated
on a highway within 50 miles of a place of business owned by
the owner of the commercial implement of husbandry for the
purpose of transporting the commercial implement of husbandry
to a farm or farms located within 50 miles of the commercial
vehicle owner's place of business, regardless of whether the
