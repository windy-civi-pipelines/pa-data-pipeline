AN ACT
Amending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An
act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public
welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in departmental powers and
duties as to supervision, providing for Keystone STARS
Program; and, in departmental powers and duties as to
licensing, further providing for fees, providing for Keystone
STARS Program and further providing for definition.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), known
as the Human Services Code, is amended by adding a section to
read:
Section 923. Keystone STARS Program.--(a) Each certified
child-care provider in the Keystone STARS Program may:
(1) Denote on its publicly accessible Internet website, if
operational, its designated STAR-level rating, which shall
include how many STARS that the certified child-care provider
has received.
(2) Provide, in a linguistically appropriate manner, the
certified child-care provider's STAR-level rating and the

(3) Obtain a department-issued placard that denotes the

Section 3. The act is amended by adding a section to read:
Section 1019. Keystone STARS Program.--(a) Each certified
child-care provider in the Keystone STARS Program may:
(1) Denote on its publicly accessible Internet website, if
operational, its designated STAR-level rating, which shall
include how many STARS that the certified child-care provider
has received.

certified child-care provider's STAR-level rating and the

certified child-care provider's participation in the Keystone
STARS Program and designated STAR-level rating, which may be
displayed in a public-facing location.

to program participants in conformance with the following:
(1) The placards shall be clear, visible, readable and

(2) The placards shall include the following information:
(i) The name of the certified child-care provider.
(ii) The name of the department.
(iii) The date that the department issued the placard.
(iv) The number of STARS that the certified child-care
provider has received.

Internet website that includes information on the Keystone STARS
Program and e-mail address of the department.
