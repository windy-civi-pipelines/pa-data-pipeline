AN ACT
Amending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An
act relating to the public school system, including certain
provisions applicable as well to private and parochial
schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the
laws relating thereto," in higher education accountability
and transparency, providing for notice of tuition increase.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known
as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a
section to read:
Section 2007-H. Notice of tuition increase.
(a) Requirement.--If an institution of higher education
increases tuition from the prior academic year, the institution
of higher education shall send notice of the tuition increase
electronically and by mail to all enrolled students of the
institution of higher education at least 45 days prior to any
bill for tuition.
(b) Contents.--The notice under subsection (a) must include:
(1) T he tuition amount for the prior academic year.
