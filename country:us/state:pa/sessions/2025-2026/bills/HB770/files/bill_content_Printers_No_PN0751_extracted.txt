2025-2026 Regular Session HB 770 PN 0751 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 751 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.770 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY EMRICK, ROAE, PICKETT, M. MACKENZIE, STAATS AND ROWE, FEBRUARY 25, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, FEBRUARY 25, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in higher education accountability and transparency, providing for notice of tuition increase.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 2007-H. Notice of tuition increase.(a) Requirement.--If an institution of higher education increases tuition from the prior academic year, the institution of higher education shall send notice of the tuition increase electronically and by mail to all enrolled students of the institution of higher education at least 45 days prior to any bill for tuition.(b) Contents.--The notice under subsection (a) must include:(1) T he tuition amount for the prior academic year. 1234567891011121314151617181920 (2) The current tuition amount.(3) The dollar and percent amount of the tuition increase.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0770PN0751 - 2 - 1234
