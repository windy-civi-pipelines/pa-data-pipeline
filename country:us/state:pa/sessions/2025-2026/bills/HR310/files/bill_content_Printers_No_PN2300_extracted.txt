2025-2026 Regular Session HR 310 PN 2300 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2300 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.310 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY McNEILL, FREEMAN, HILL-EVANS, DOUGHERTY, HOHENSTEIN, SANCHEZ, M. MACKENZIE, BRENNAN, PIELLI, NEILSON, JAMES AND VENKAT, SEPTEMBER 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, SEPTEMBER 10, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating September 28, 2025, as "Gold Star Mother's and Family Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, When a military service member dies in the line of duty, the family receives The Gold Star to commemorate the military member's service and life, while recognizing the impact of loss on the family; andWHEREAS, During World War I, families of service members displayed the Service Flag, which featured a blue star for each immediate family member serving in the military; andWHEREAS, When a service member tragically died during combat, the blue star was replaced with a gold star; andWHEREAS, During World War I, Grace Darling Seibold lost her son, George Vaughn Seibold, who died while serving in France; andWHEREAS, Grace Darling Seibold founded American Gold Star Mothers, Inc., to provide comfort and support to mothers as they processed their grief; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, President Woodrow Wilson used the term "Gold Star Mother" following World War I, representing the importance and honor of those who served and the lives that were sacrificed; andWHEREAS, In 1936, Congress designated the last Sunday of September as "Gold Star Mother's Day" in order to honor grieving mothers; andWHEREAS, In 2011, President Barack Obama amended the title of "Gold Star Mother's Day" to "Gold Star Mother's and Family Day," which is recognized on the last Sunday in September; andWHEREAS, "Gold Star Mother's and Family Day" signifies the impact of the loss of a service member on Gold Star family members, including parents, siblings and children; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate September 28, 2025, as "Gold Star Mother's and Family Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0310PN2300 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617
