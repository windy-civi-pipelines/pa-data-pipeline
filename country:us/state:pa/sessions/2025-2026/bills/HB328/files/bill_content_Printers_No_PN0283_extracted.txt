2025-2026 Regular Session HB 328 PN 0283 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 283 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.328 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY OWLETT, PICKETT, HAMM, STENDER, JAMES, ZIMMERMAN, KUZMA AND COOK, JANUARY 27, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON ENVIRONMENTAL AND NATURAL RESOURCE PROTECTION, JANUARY 27, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of November 26, 1978 (P.L.1375, No.325), entitled "An act providing for the regulation and safety of dams and reservoirs, water obstructions and encroachments; consolidating and clarifying the programs of the Department of Environmental Resources and Navigation Commission for the Delaware River; establishing penalties and repealing certain acts," further providing for permit issuance and conditions.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 9 of the act of November 26, 1978 (P.L.1375, No.325), known as the Dam Safety and Encroachments Act, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 9. Permit issuance and conditions.* * *(c) If the department issues an emergency permit, approval or authorization for stream clearing or maintenance activities around a water obstruction under this act or any other State law, the department shall authorize clearing activities for not less than 250 feet from the water obstruction if the emergency permit, approval or authorization is requested by a Commonwealth 1234567891011121314151617181920 agency or political subdivision and there is a declaration of disaster emergency for the area at the Federal, State or county level.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0328PN0283 - 2 - 1234
