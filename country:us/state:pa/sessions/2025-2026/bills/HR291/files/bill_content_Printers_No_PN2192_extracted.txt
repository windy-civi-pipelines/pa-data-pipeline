2025-2026 Regular Session HR 291 PN 2192 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 2192 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.291 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY O'MARA, BENNINGHOFF, CONKLIN, FREEMAN, HILL-EVANS, HANBIDGE, REICHARD, SCHLOSSBERG, SAMUELSON, McNEILL, GREINER, VENKAT, RIVERA, BURGOS, STENDER, PIELLI, K.HARRIS, PROBST, HADDOCK, HOWARD, MERSKI, GUENST, SANCHEZ, SAPPEY, GALLAGHER, HOHENSTEIN, D. WILLIAMS, NEILSON, BELLMON, GILLEN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, COOPER AND CIRESI, AUGUST 1, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, AUGUST 4, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of September 2025 as "Suicide Prevention Awareness Month" and September 10, 2025, as "World Suicide Prevention Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, More than 720,000 people around the world die by suicide each year; andWHEREAS, The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention estimates that there were 1.5 million suicide attempts and 12.8 million individuals that seriously thought about suicide in the United States in 2023; andWHEREAS, After declining between 2018 and 2020, suicide rates in the United States increased to their peak rate of 14.1 per 100,000 individuals; andWHEREAS, According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, suicide accounted for 49,000 deaths in 2023, which is 1 death every 11 minutes; andWHEREAS, Suicide is the eleventh-leading cause of death in 12345678910111213141516 the United States and the second-leading cause of death for those 10 to 14 years of age; andWHEREAS, Stigma surrounding mental health and suicide prevents many individuals who are contemplating suicide from seeking and receiving appropriate treatment; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of September 2025 as "Suicide Prevention Awareness Month" and September 10, 2025, as "World Suicide Prevention Day" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage the residents of this Commonwealth to provide support for those in need.20250HR0291PN2192 - 2 - 123456789101112
