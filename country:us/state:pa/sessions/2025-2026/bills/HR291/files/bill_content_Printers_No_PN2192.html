A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the month of September 2025 as "Suicide Prevention
Awareness Month" and September 10, 2025, as "World Suicide
Prevention Day" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, More than 720,000 people around the world die by
suicide each year; and
WHEREAS, The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
estimates that there were 1.5 million suicide attempts and 12.8
million individuals that seriously thought about suicide in the
United States in 2023; and
WHEREAS, After declining between 2018 and 2020, suicide rates
in the United States increased to their peak rate of 14.1 per
100,000 individuals; and
WHEREAS, According to the Centers for Disease Control and
Prevention, suicide accounted for 49,000 deaths in 2023, which
is 1 death every 11 minutes; and
WHEREAS, Suicide is the eleventh-leading cause of death in
