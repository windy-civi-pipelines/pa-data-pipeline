2025-2026 Regular Session HR 13 PN 0168 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 168 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.13 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY FLOOD, GLEIM, HAMM, KENYATTA, OLSOMMER, PICKETT, RADER, REICHARD, SMITH AND WATRO, JANUARY 17, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON INTERGOVERNMENTAL AFFAIRS AND OPERATIONS, JANUARY 17, 2025 A RESOLUTIONUrging the Federal Government to provide State and local governments with the authority to respond swiftly and decisively to unidentified drone threats.WHEREAS, Since mid-November 2024, New Jersey has documented more than 3,000 sightings of unidentified drones operating near military bases, private residences and various sensitive locations; andWHEREAS, Current Federal regulations prevent State and local governments from taking immediate action when unidentified drones possibly threaten the safety and privacy of our communities; andWHEREAS, The Federal Government should empower State and local governments with the legal authority to respond when unidentified drones are a possible threat; andWHEREAS, The Federal Government should implement stronger response measures and public transparency measures when dealing with potential unidentified drone threats; andWHEREAS, The residents of this Commonwealth deserve the 123456789101112131415161718 safety and security of immediate and effective State and local government action when unidentified drones threaten our communities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives urge the Federal Government to provide State and local governments with the authority to respond swiftly and decisively to unidentified drone threats.20250HR0013PN0168 - 2 - 1234567
