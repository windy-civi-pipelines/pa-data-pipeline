A RESOLUTION
Urging the Federal Government to provide State and local
governments with the authority to respond swiftly and
decisively to unidentified drone threats.
WHEREAS, Since mid-November 2024, New Jersey has documented
more than 3,000 sightings of unidentified drones operating near
military bases, private residences and various sensitive
locations; and
WHEREAS, Current Federal regulations prevent State and local
governments from taking immediate action when unidentified
drones possibly threaten the safety and privacy of our
communities; and
WHEREAS, The Federal Government should empower State and
local governments with the legal authority to respond when
unidentified drones are a possible threat; and
WHEREAS, The Federal Government should implement stronger
response measures and public transparency measures when dealing
with potential unidentified drone threats; and
WHEREAS, The residents of this Commonwealth deserve the
