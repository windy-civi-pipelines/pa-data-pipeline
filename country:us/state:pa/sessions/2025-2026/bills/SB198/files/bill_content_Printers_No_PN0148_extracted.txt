The following amendment to the Constitution of Pennsylvania is proposed in accordance with Article XI:That Article VIII be amended by adding a section to read:ยง 18. General appropriation bill.If the general appropriation bill is not enacted by the constitutional deadline of June 30 in any year, the Commonwealth shall maintain State appropriations at 80% of the amounts specified in the general appropriation act for the most recent fiscal year.Section 2. The following procedure applies to the proposed constitutional amendment in this joint resolution:(1) Upon the first passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising 123456789101112131415161718 requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.(2) Upon the second passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.
