2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1590 PN 1852 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1852 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1590 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY D. WILLIAMS, HEFFLEY, GIRAL, SANCHEZ, HILL-EVANS, WAXMAN, GUENST, JAMES, HOHENSTEIN, PROBST AND MERSKI, JUNE 6, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, JUNE 6, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in public assistance, providing for abrogation of regulatory requirements for payment for clinic services within physical confines of clinic facilities.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), known as the Human Services Code, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 443.16. Abrogation of Regulatory Requirements for Payment for Clinic Services within Physical Confines of Clinic Facilities.--The following provisions of 55 Pa. Code (relating to human services) are abrogated to the extent they limit medical assistance payments only for clinic services provided within the physical confines of a clinic facility:(1) Section 1153.14(6) (relating to noncovered services).(2) Section 1223.14(11) (relating to noncovered services).12345678910111213141516171819 Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB1590PN1852 - 2 - 1
