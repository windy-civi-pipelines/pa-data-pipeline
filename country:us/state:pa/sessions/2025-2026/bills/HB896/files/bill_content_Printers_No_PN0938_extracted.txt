Sentencing procedure for murder of the first degree.(a) Procedure in jury trials.--Except as provided in subsection (a.1):* * *(a.1) Undocumented migrant status.--After a verdict of murder of the first degree and the status of the defendant as an undocumented migrant are recorded, the court shall sentence the defendant to death.* * *12345678910111213141516171819 (p) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Undocumented migrant."
