AN ACT
Amending Title 35 (Health and Safety) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in Commonwealth services, providing
for protection against PFAS chemicals AND FOR FIREFIGHTING
PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT; and imposing penalties.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 35 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section SECTIONS to read:
§ 7390. Protection against PFAS chemicals.

July 1, 2026, a manufacturer may not manufacture or knowingly
sell, offer for sale, distribute for sale or distribute for
use in this Commonwealth class B firefighting foam containing
an intentionally added PFAS chemical.

January 1, 2027, a person, including a firefighting entity,

§ 7391. FIREFIGHTING PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT.
(A) WARNING LABELS.--A MANUFACTURER OF PERSONAL PROTECTIVE
EQUIPMENT USED IN FIRE PREVENTION, FIREFIGHTING OR FIRE CONTROL
ACTIVITIES SHALL AFFIX A WARNING LABEL TO SUCH PRODUCT SOLD FOR
USE IN THIS COMMONWEALTH THAT INDICATES IF THE PRODUCT CONTAINS
A PFAS CHEMICAL. THE FOLLOWING SHALL APPLY:
(1) A MANUFACTURER OF PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT USED
IN FIRE PREVENTION, FIREFIGHTING OR FIRE CONTROL ACTIVITIES
SHALL PROVIDE A WRITTEN NOTICE TO THE PURCHASER AT THE TIME
OF THE SALE THAT INCLUDES:
(I) A STATEMENT OF WHETHER THE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE
EQUIPMENT CONTAINS PFAS CHEMICALS.
(II) THE REASON THAT THE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE
EQUIPMENT CONTAINS PFAS CHEMICALS , IF APPLICABLE.
(2) A MANUFACTURER OR INDIVIDUAL IS NOT PRECLUDED FROM
PROVIDING FURTHER GUIDANCE ON THE LIMITATIONS OR RESTRICTIONS
ON PFAS CHEMICALS IN PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT IN
ADDITION TO THE WRITTEN NOTICE REQUIRED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1).
(3) THE MANUFACTURER AND PURCHASER OF THE PERSONAL
PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT CONTAINING A PFAS CHEMICAL SHALL RETAIN
THE WRITTEN NOTICE REQUIRED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) FOR AT LEAST
THREE YEARS FROM THE DATE OF PURCHASE.
(B) ENFORCEMENT.--THE OFFICE OF THE STATE FIRE COMMISSIONER
SHALL ENFORCE THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION.
(C) VIOLATIONS.--
(1) A MANUFACTURER OR PERSON SHALL BE SUBJECT TO A CIVIL
PENALTY NOT TO EXCEED $5,000 FOR A FIRST VIOLATION AND
$10,000 FOR EACH SUBSEQUENT VIOLATION.
(2) MONEY COLLECTED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) SHALL BE

(D) DEFINITIONS.--AS USED IN THIS SECTION, THE FOLLOWING
WORDS AND PHRASES SHALL HAVE THE MEANINGS GIVEN TO THEM IN THIS
SUBSECTION UNLESS THE CONTEXT CLEARLY INDICATES OTHERWISE:
"FIREFIGHTING ENTITY." AN ENTITY THAT ENGAGES IN FIRE
PREVENTION, FIREFIGHTING OR FIRE CONTROL ACTIVITIES IN THIS
COMMONWEALTH. THE TERM SHALL INCLUDE MUNICIPAL FIRE DEPARTMENTS
AND VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANIES.
"MANUFACTURER." A PERSON THAT MANUFACTURES, IMPORTS OR
DISTRIBUTES DOMESTICALLY PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT FOR A
FIREFIGHTING ENTITY.

MARKETED TO BE WORN BY FIREFIGHTING ENTITY PERSONNEL IN THE
PERFORMANCE OF FIRE AND RESCUE ACTIVITIES, INCLUDING JACKETS,
PANTS, SHOES, GLOVES, HELMETS AND RESPIRATORY EQUIPMENT.
