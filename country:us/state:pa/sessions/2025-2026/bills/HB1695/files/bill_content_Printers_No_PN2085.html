commissions, committees, councils and representatives; and

authorities, boards, commissions, committees, councils and
representatives abolishment; providing for composition of
Pennsylvania Human Relations Commission, for transfer of
duties and for duty of Secretary of the Commonwealth relating
to Interstate Rail Passenger Advisory Council Compact;
further providing for repeals; and abrogating an executive
order.

Section 2. The act is amended by adding sections to read:
Section 1.1. Pennsylvania Human Relations Commission.
(a) Membership.--The Pennsylvania Human Relations Commission
established under section 6 of the act of October 27, 1955
(P.L.744, No.222), known as the Pennsylvania Human Relations
Act, shall consist of the following members:
(1) Two individuals appointed by the President pro
tempore of the Senate.
(2) Two individuals appointed by the Minority Leader of
the Senate.
(3) Two individuals appointed by the Speaker of the
House of Representatives.
(4) Two individuals appointed by the Minority Leader of
the House of Representatives.
(5) One individual appointed by the Governor.
(6) One individual appointed by the Attorney General of
the Commonwealth.
(7) One individual appointed by the Auditor General of
the Commonwealth.

(b) Terms, vacancies and absences.--
(1) A member appointed under subsection (a) shall hold
office for a term of five years or until the member's
successor is duly appointed.
(2) Vacancies occurring in an office of a member of the
commission by expiration of term, death, resignation, removal
or for any other reason shall be filled in the manner
provided in subsection (a) for the balance of the term.
(3) Commission members failing to attend meetings for
three consecutive months shall forfeit their seats unless the
chairperson receives written notification from the member
involved that the absence was due to personal illness or the
death or illness of an immediate family member.
(c) Chairperson.--The members of the Pennsylvania Human
Relations Commission shall elect a member to serve as
chairperson of the Pennsylvania Human Relations Commission.
Section 1.2. Transfer of duties.
The following duties are transferred:
(1) The Secretary of Agriculture shall carry out the
duties of the former Canine Health Board under section 207(h)
(7) and (8) and (i)(3) and (5) of the act of December 7, 1982
(P.L.784, No.225), known as the Dog Law.
(2) The Secretary of the Commonwealth shall carry out
the duties of the former Lobbying Disclosure Regulations
Committee under 65 Pa.C.S. § 13A10(d)(5) and (6) (relating to
registration fees; fund established; system; regulations).
Section 1.3. Duty of Secretary of the Commonwealth.
Within 10 days of the effective date of this section, the
Secretary of the Commonwealth shall, pursuant to Article IV of
the Interstate Rail Passenger Advisory Council Compact, give
