2025-2026 Regular Session HR 311 PN 2304 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2304 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.311 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. MACKENZIE, BERNSTINE, KUZMA, STAATS, GALLAGHER, MENTZER, WATRO, GILLEN AND ZIMMERMAN, SEPTEMBER 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, SEPTEMBER 11, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of January 2026 as "Law Enforcement Professionals Appreciation Month" in Pennsylvania, honoring and supporting law enforcement professionals of all levels of government who serve and protect this Commonwealth.WHEREAS, January 9 of every year is recognized as National Law Enforcement Appreciation Day, a day created in 2015 to show gratitude for law enforcement officers and recognize their service and sacrifices; andWHEREAS, Our many proud law enforcement professionals, whose service and sacrifice protect our communities and safeguard our democracy, provide a vital public service to this Commonwealth through the fulfillment of their mission to serve and protect; andWHEREAS, The men and women of law enforcement put their lives on the line on a daily basis to protect the safety and welfare of our great Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Increasingly, the very officers who have sworn to protect our residents have found themselves under attack; andWHEREAS, Our law enforcement professionals have been subject 12345678910111213141516171819 to anti-police rhetoric, defunding and targeted anti-police violence; andWHEREAS, Threats of violence toward law enforcement professionals are on the rise which have affected the morale of those who run toward the danger and risk their lives for the residents of this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, By recognizing the month of January 2026 as "Law Enforcement Professionals Appreciation Month," the House of Representatives commits itself to continue to recognize and support those who put themselves between residents and danger to keep our communities safe and play such an integral role in our society; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of January 2026 as "Law Enforcement Professionals Appreciation Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize and support law enforcement professionals of all levels of government in Pennsylvania.20250HR0311PN2304 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718
