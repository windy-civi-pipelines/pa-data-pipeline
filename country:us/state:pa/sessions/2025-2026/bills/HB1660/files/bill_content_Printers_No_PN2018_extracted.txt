2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1660 PN 2018 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2018 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1660 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ORTITAY, KAUFFMAN, HAMM, COOK, ROWE, DIAMOND, MOUL, BANTA, FINK, WARNER AND ZIMMERMAN, JUNE 24, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LOCAL GOVERNMENT, JUNE 25, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of May 1, 1984 (P.L.206, No.43), entitled "An act providing for safe drinking water; imposing powers and duties on the Department of Environmental Resources in relation thereto; and appropriating certain funds," further providing for definitions; and providing for prohibition of water fluoridation.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 3 of the act of May 1, 1984 (P.L.206, No.43), known as the Pennsylvania Safe Drinking Water Act, is amended by adding a definition to read:Section 3. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this act shall have the meanings given to them in this section unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:* * *"Fluoride-containing chemicals." A chemical or compound containing fluoride and used with the intent of raising the fluoride levels of water, including sodium fluoride, fluorosilicic acid and sodium fluorosilicate.1234567891011121314151617181920 * * *Section 2. The act is amended by adding a section to read:Section 12.1. Prohibition of water fluoridation.It shall be a violation of this act for any supplier of water to add fluoride-containing chemicals to water provided by a public water system that the supplier of water owns or operates.Section 3. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1660PN2018 - 2 - 1234567
