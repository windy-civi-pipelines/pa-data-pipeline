2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1877 PN 2334 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2334 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1877 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY D'ORSIE, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, RIVERA, VENKAT, KHAN, WAXMAN, CUTLER, HILL-EVANS, GUZMAN, ZIMMERMAN, ANDERSON AND GREEN, SEPTEMBER 23, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, SEPTEMBER 25, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in terms and courses of study, providing for Pennsylvania Seal of Biliteracy.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 1511.2. Pennsylvania Seal of Biliteracy.--(a) Notwithstanding section 2604-B(b)(2)(v), 22 Pa. Code ยง 4.24 (relating to high school graduation requirements), 4.51 (relating to State assessment system) or 4.51c (relating to project-based assessment) or any law to the contrary, the Department of Education, beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, shall establish criteria for awarding the Pennsylvania Seal of Biliteracy to any student who demonstrates proficiency 12345678910111213141516171819 in English and at least one other foreign language on approved diplomas. To qualify for the Pennsylvania Seal of Biliteracy, a student must:(1) Complete all requirements to earn a high school diploma.(2) Meet any English proficiency criteria established by the Department of Education.(3) Meet any foreign language proficiency criteria established by the Department of Education.(b) By August 31 of the year following the establishment of criteria for awarding the Pennsylvania Seal of Biliteracy under subsection (a) and each August 31 thereafter , the State Board of Education shall issue a report on the efficacy of the seal, rate of completion among graduating students and recommendations for improvement, including proposed legislation, to the chairperson and minority chairperson of the Education Committee of the Senate and the chairperson and minority chairperson of the Education Committee of the House of Representatives.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB1877PN2334 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718
