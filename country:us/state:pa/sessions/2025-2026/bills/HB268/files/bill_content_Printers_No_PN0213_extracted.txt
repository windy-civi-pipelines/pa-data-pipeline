2025-2026 Regular Session HB 268 PN 0213 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 213 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.268 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HANBIDGE, SCHLOSSBERG, SMITH-WADE-EL, KHAN, GIRAL, McNEILL, SANCHEZ, HOHENSTEIN, PROBST, GUENST, HOWARD, WARREN, SHUSTERMAN, FREEMAN, HILL-EVANS, OTTEN, CERRATO AND GREEN, JANUARY 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, JANUARY 22, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in public assistance, further providing for the medically needy and determination of eligibility.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 442.1 of the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), known as the Human Services Code, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 442.1. The Medically Needy; Determination of Eligibility.--* * *(d) No later than ninety days after eligibility for medical assistance is determined under this section, a person determined to be eligible for services under a home- and community-based waiver shall be offered those benefits.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.1234567891011121314151617
