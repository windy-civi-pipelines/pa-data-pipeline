AN ACT
Amending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in assault, providing for the offense
of assault of emergency responder.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
§ 2702.2. Assault of emergency responder.
(a) Offense defined.--
(1) A person commits a felony of the second degree if
the person intentionally or knowingly causes or attempts to
cause an emergency responder, while in the performance of
duty and with knowledge that the victim is an emergency
responder, to come into contact with fentanyl or fentanyl
derivatives, including fentanyl or fentanyl derivative salts,
isomers and salts of isomers, by throwing, tossing or
expelling that substance at the emergency responder.
(2) A person commits a felony of the third degree if the
