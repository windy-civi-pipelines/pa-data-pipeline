2025-2026 Regular Session HR 263 PN 1961 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1961 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.263 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY WARREN, MARCELL, HOGAN, VITALI, McNEILL, PIELLI, HILL-EVANS, COOK, T. DAVIS, CERRATO, HOWARD, HANBIDGE, STAATS, GREINER, BRENNAN, VENKAT, SANCHEZ, NEILSON, HADDOCK, GALLAGHER AND GILLEN, JUNE 17, 2025 INTRODUCED AS NONCONTROVERSIAL RESOLUTION UNDER RULE 35, JUNE 18, 2025 A RESOLUTIONExpressing condolences and honoring the life of Alec Campbell, a park resource ranger with the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources who lost his life in the line of duty.WHEREAS, Alec D. Campbell, a park resource ranger with the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, lost his life in the line of duty on May 23, 2025; andWHEREAS, While working to lace a buoy after heavy rainfall, Mr. Campbell's kayak capsized due to the increased flow of the Neshaminy Creek and he fell into the water and tragically drowned; andWHEREAS, In working at Tyler State Park in Newtown, Mr. Campbell worked to promote the safe utilization of the park's recreation facilities; andWHEREAS, During Mr. Campbell's tenure, he helped welcome visitors, prepared and delivered informational presentations to visitors and groups, guided many Eagle Scout and youth projects, assisted park managers and rangers and strove to protect this 1234567891011121314151617 Commonwealth's natural spaces and ensure the safety of those who visited the park; andWHEREAS, Mr. Campbell enjoyed being outdoors, playing disc golf, hiking, paddling and spending time with his family; andWHEREAS, Mr. Campbell was a beloved family member, dedicated worker and an avowed community steward; andWHEREAS, Mr. Campbell is survived by his wife Rena and their two young children, Calder who is eight and Miles who is five; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives express condolences and honor the life of Alec Campbell, a park resource ranger with the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources who lost his life in the line of duty; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to Rena Campbell.20250HR0263PN1961 - 2 - 123456789101112131415
