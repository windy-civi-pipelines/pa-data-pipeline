A RESOLUTION
Expressing condolences and honoring the life of Alec Campbell, a
park resource ranger with the Department of Conservation and
Natural Resources who lost his life in the line of duty.
WHEREAS, Alec D. Campbell, a park resource ranger with the
Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, lost his life
in the line of duty on May 23, 2025; and
WHEREAS, While working to lace a buoy after heavy rainfall,
Mr. Campbell's kayak capsized due to the increased flow of the
Neshaminy Creek and he fell into the water and tragically
drowned; and
WHEREAS, In working at Tyler State Park in Newtown, Mr.
Campbell worked to promote the safe utilization of the park's
recreation facilities; and
WHEREAS, During Mr. Campbell's tenure, he helped welcome
visitors, prepared and delivered informational presentations to
visitors and groups, guided many Eagle Scout and youth projects,
assisted park managers and rangers and strove to protect this
