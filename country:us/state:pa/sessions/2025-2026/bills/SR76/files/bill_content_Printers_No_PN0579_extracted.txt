2025-2026 Regular Session SR 76 PN 0579 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 579 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.76 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY FARRY, BROOKS, BOSCOLA, MARTIN, COMITTA, KANE, HUGHES, HAYWOOD, FONTANA, BROWN AND PHILLIPS-HILL, APRIL 9, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, APRIL 9, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the week of April 13 through 19, 2025, as "National Public Safety Telecommunications Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, "National Public Safety Telecommunications Week" began in 1994 when President Clinton signed Presidential Proclamation 6667; andWHEREAS, "National Public Safety Telecommunications Week" is celebrated annually during the second week of April to recognize the invaluable work of 911 dispatchers and workers, emergency communications centers, public safety answering points, public safety communications centers, emergency operations centers and other public safety command centers; andWHEREAS, Their work includes dispatching emergency responses, assisting first responders with communication at incidents, instructing first aid care over the phone to 911 callers and comforting someone during the most stressful time of their life; andWHEREAS, This work requires 24/7, year-round service and can 1234567891011121314151617 be a difficult burden on the workers and their families; andWHEREAS, Their work often involves dealing with the worst moments of the lives of this Commonwealth's residents and providing them with the help to deal with these crises; andWHEREAS, The demand of emergency calls and crises can often be overwhelming and unpredictable; andWHEREAS, Though this work may be extremely demanding and difficult, the rewards are immeasurable; andWHEREAS, The workers' service and work is truly life changing and the amount of lives saved and changed every year, month, day or hour is incalculable; andWHEREAS, Without the services they provide, the performance of other first responders would be almost impossible to do; andWHEREAS, Like most first responders, there are difficulties in recruitment and retention of these workers; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the week of April 13 through 19, 2025, as "National Public Safety Telecommunications Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate thank and recognize all of this Commonwealth's emergency dispatchers and telecommunicators for their invaluable service in saving lives.20250SR0076PN0579 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
