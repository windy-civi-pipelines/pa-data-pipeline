The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The act of April 9, 1929 (P.L.343, No.176), known
as The Fiscal Code, is amended by adding a section to read:
Section 103-G. Retrofitting residential high-rise buildings
with automatic fire sprinkler systems.
A city of the first class shall have the authority to
determine the participation in a property tax abatement program
or real property tax relief program for the purpose of
incentivizing investment in retrofitting residential high-rise
buildings with automatic fire sprinkler systems and associated
monitoring or detection devices.
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
