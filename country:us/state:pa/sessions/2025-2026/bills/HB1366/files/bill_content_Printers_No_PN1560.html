AN ACT
Amending Title 34 (Game) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in hunting and furtaking licenses, further
providing for elk hunting licenses.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 2706.2 of Title 34 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:
2706.2. Elk hunting licenses.
* * *
(e) Transfer.--
(1) A recipient selected to receive a license under
subsection (b) or (c) may transfer the license to an eligible
hunter as provided under this subsection.
(2) The commission shall provide for the form and manner
in which a license may be transferred under this subsection,
including the manner in which a transferee must prove that
the transferee meets the requirements of this subsection.
(3) The transfer of a license under this subsection may

Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
