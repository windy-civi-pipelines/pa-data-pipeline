AN ACT
Amending Title 45 (Legal Notices) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in legal advertising, providing for
methods for advertising.
AMENDING TITLES 45 (LEGAL NOTICES) AND 65 (PUBLIC OFFICERS) OF
THE PENNSYLVANIA CONSOLIDATED STATUTES, IN PRELIMINARY
PROVISIONS, FURTHER PROVIDING FOR DEFINITIONS; IN LEGAL
ADVERTISING, FURTHER PROVIDING FOR USE OF TRADE PUBLICATIONS
AND PROVIDING FOR LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT ADVERTISING
REQUIREMENTS; AND, IN OPEN MEETINGS, FURTHER PROVIDING FOR
DEFINITIONS.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 45 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
§ 311. Methods for advertising.
(a) Advertisement or notice requirements.--Notwithstanding
the provisions of this title or any law or regulation to the
contrary, when an advertisement or notice is required by law,
rule or regulation, a local government unit shall satisfy the
requirement by using one of the following methods:
(1) a newspaper as defined in section 101 (relating to
definitions), printed or circulated generally in the county

(b) Legal journal requirement.--Use by a local government

posted at the principal office of the local government unit

meets, the county in which the local government unit is
located shall provide space for the postings where the local
government unit is responsible for posting its advertisements

SECTION 3. TITLE 45 IS AMENDED BY ADDING A SECTION TO READ:
§ 311. LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT ADVERTISING REQUIREMENTS.
(A) ADVERTISEMENT OR NOTICE REQUIREMENTS.--IF AN
ADVERTISEMENT OR NOTICE IS REQUIRED TO BE PUBLISHED BY THIS
TITLE OR OTHER STATUTE, A LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT SHALL FULFILL
THE REQUIREMENT BY USING ONE OF THE FOLLOWING METHODS:
(1) NEWSPAPER.
(2) (I) PUBLICATION ON THE OFFICIAL PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE
INTERNET WEBSITE OF THE COUNTY IN WHICH THE LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNIT IS LOCATED.
(II) PUBLICATION ON THE OFFICIAL PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE
INTERNET WEBSITE OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT, IF
PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE INTERNET WEBSITES EXIST.
(III) PHYSICAL POSTING OF A COPY OF THE
ADVERTISEMENT OR NOTICE AT:
(A) THE PRINCIPAL OFFICE OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT
UNIT; AND
(B) THE PUBLIC BUILDING IN WHICH THE LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNIT REGULARLY CONDUCTS PUBLIC MEETINGS.
(B) EXCEPTION.--IF A LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT DOES NOT OPERATE
OR MAINTAIN ITS OWN OFFICIAL PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE INTERNET
WEBSITE, THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT SHALL SATISFY THE
REQUIREMENTS OF SUBSECTION (A) BY DOING EACH OF THE FOLLOWING:
(1) PUBLISHING THE ADVERTISEMENT OR NOTICE ON THE
OFFICIAL PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE INTERNET WEBSITE OF THE COUNTY

SECTION 5. WITHIN 90 DAYS AFTER THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THIS
SECTION, COUNTIES SHALL DESIGNATE AN OFFICE AS THE MAIN CONTACT
FOR RECEIVING PUBLIC NOTICES FROM LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS.
CONTACT INFORMATION, INCLUDING PHONE NUMBER AND EMAIL ADDRESS,
SHALL BE MADE AVAILABLE TO EACH LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT THAT
RESIDES IN THE COUNTY.

(1) SECTION 5 OF THIS ACT AND THIS SECTION SHALL TAKE
EFFECT IMMEDIATELY.
