2025-2026 Regular Session HB 859 PN 0897 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 897 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.859 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY DELOZIER, MARCH 11, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON STATE GOVERNMENT, MARCH 11, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 62 (Procurement) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in contracts for public works, providing for specifications for contracts.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 62 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 3914. Specifications for contracts.(a) Requirements.-- No specification for a contract awarded under this chapter may be written in such a manner as to contain proprietary, exclusionary or discriminatory requirements other than those based upon performance unless : (1) the requirements are necessary to test or demonstrate a specific feature;(2) the requirements are necessary to provide for interchangeability of parts or equipment;(3) the requirements are necessary to extend or maintain compatibility with an existing system or equipment. A contract containing requirements under this paragraph shall 12345678910111213141516171819 include an explanation as to why the extension or compatibility of a system or equipment requires the specifications; or(4) no other brand or source exists.(b) Equivalent parts or equipment.--For each part or piece of equipment, the specifications shall identify the essential performance features that a brand or model must have in order to be considered an equivalent and a statement that equivalent parts or equipment will be given equal consideration.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0859PN0897 - 2 - 12345678910
