AN ACT
Amending Title 62 (Procurement) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in contracts for public works, providing for
specifications for contracts.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 62 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
3914. Specifications for contracts.
(a) Requirements.-- No specification for a contract awarded
under this chapter may be written in such a manner as to contain
proprietary, exclusionary or discriminatory requirements other
than those based upon performance unless :
(1) the requirements are necessary to test or
demonstrate a specific feature;
(2) the requirements are necessary to provide for
interchangeability of parts or equipment;
(3) the requirements are necessary to extend or maintain
compatibility with an existing system or equipment. A
contract containing requirements under this paragraph shall

(4) no other brand or source exists.
(b) Equivalent parts or equipment.--For each part or piece

performance features that a brand or model must have in order to
be considered an equivalent and a statement that equivalent
parts or equipment will be given equal consideration.
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
