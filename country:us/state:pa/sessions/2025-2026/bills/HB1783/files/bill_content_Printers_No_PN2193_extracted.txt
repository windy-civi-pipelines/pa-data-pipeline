WILLIAMS, GALLAGHER AND GILLEN, AUGUST 4, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, AUGUST 5, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for antique, classic and collectible plates.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 1340(a.1) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:ยง 1340. Antique, classic and collectible plates.* * *(a.1) Vintage registration plates.--In lieu of a special plate issued under subsection (a), the owner of an antique or classic motor vehicle may request permission from the department to display a vintage registration plate from the model year of the motor vehicle. The vintage registration plate shall be:(1) Provided by the motor vehicle owner.(2) A Pennsylvania registration plate issued between the years 1906 and [1976] 1999, or a later year as determined by the department.123456789101112131415161718 (3) Legible from a reasonable distance.The applicant shall provide information as the department may require for processing the request and a $75 application fee. The department may deny a request to use a vintage registration plate for cause.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1783PN2193 - 2 - 1234567
