AN ACT
Amending the act of December 16, 1998 (P.L.980, No.129),
entitled "An act providing for a waiver of tuition and other
fees for children of certain deceased police officers,
National Guard members, firefighters and correction employees
at community colleges and State-owned and State-related
institutions of higher education and for additional powers
and duties of the Pennsylvania Higher Education Assistance
Agency and the Department of General Services," further
providing for definitions and for Postsecondary Educational
Gratuity Program; and repealing provisions relating to
retroactivity.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The definitions of "firefighter," "municipality,"
"police officer" and "volunteer fire company" in section 2 of
the act of December 16, 1998 (P.L.980, No.129), known as the
Police Officer, Firefighter, Correction Employee and National
Guard Member Child Beneficiary Education Act, are amended to
read:

"Firefighter." An individual employed in that capacity on a
full-time basis [by a municipality] or a member of a volunteer
fire company [of this Commonwealth]. The term includes members

June 24, 1976 (P.L.424, No.101), referred to as the Emergency

["Municipality." A county, city, borough, incorporated town

"Police officer." An individual employed on a full-time or
part-time basis by the Commonwealth as a member of the
Pennsylvania State Police Force or an individual employed on a
full-time or part-time basis as a police officer [by a
municipality of this Commonwealth]. The term includes a police
officer employed outside of this Commonwealth.
* * *
"Volunteer fire company." A nonprofit chartered corporation,
association or organization [located in this Commonwealth] which
provides fire protection services [within this Commonwealth].
