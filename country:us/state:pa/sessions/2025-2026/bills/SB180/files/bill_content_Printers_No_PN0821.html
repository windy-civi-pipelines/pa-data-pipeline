AN ACT
Amending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An
act relating to the public school system, including certain
provisions applicable as well to private and parochial
schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the
laws relating thereto," in pupils and attendance, further
providing for Nonprofit School Food Program, repealing
provisions relating to school lunch and breakfast
reimbursement and establishing the Universal School Meal
Program and the Universal School Meal Fund; and making an
interfund transfer and an appropriation.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:

Section 3. The act is amended by adding a section to read:
Section 1337.2. Universal School Meal Program.-- (a) The
Universal School Meal Program is established with the goal to
provide two free meals each school day, as permitted by Federal
regulations, to each student of a school regardless of the
household income of the student.
(b) The purposes of the program are to:
(1) Further the principle that no child should experience
hunger.
(2) Provide that each student of a school benefit from
healthy meals and have the nourishment needed to achieve
academic success, free from stigma or stress.
(c) Each school that participates in the School Breakfast
Program shall provide to each enrolled student of the school a
