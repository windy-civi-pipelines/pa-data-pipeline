2025-2026 Regular Session HR 103 PN 0885 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 885 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.103 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HANBIDGE, VENKAT, MADDEN, FREEMAN, GIRAL, CERRATO, HILL-EVANS, KENYATTA, SCHLOSSBERG, McNEILL, REICHARD, NEILSON, SANCHEZ, MALAGARI, GUENST, HOWARD, KHAN, PUGH AND RIVERA, MARCH 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, MARCH 10, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of April 2025 as "National Volunteer Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The United States Census Bureau estimates that in 2021, nearly 61 million Americans volunteered and collectively served 5.8 billion hours with an economic value of $122.9 billion; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism can help unemployed people find jobs; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism can provide career experience and teach valuable skills; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism builds social ties by connecting people to others, establishing friendships and increasing social and relationship skills; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism can boost self-confidence and combat depression; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism has been associated with reduced mortality; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Volunteerism can be done anywhere, from out in the community to working at home; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism can provide a sense of fulfillment and purpose in life; andWHEREAS, Volunteerism builds community resilience in the face of natural disasters and other tests of collective strength; andWHEREAS, Volunteers are active in every major shock and stress that communities experience, from earthquakes and flooding to climate change and local conflicts; andWHEREAS, Volunteers are critical to rural communities, from running sports events and community festivals to providing essential and life-preserving services as firefighters and paramedics; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of April 2025 as "National Volunteer Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives urge all residents of this Commonwealth to find ways to make a difference in their communities, no matter how big or small the effort, because someone will be grateful that they were lent a helping hand.20250HR0103PN0885 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
