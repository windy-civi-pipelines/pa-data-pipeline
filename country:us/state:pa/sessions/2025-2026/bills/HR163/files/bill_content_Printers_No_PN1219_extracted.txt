2025-2026 Regular Session HR 163 PN 1219 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1219 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.163 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HANBIDGE, MADDEN, VENKAT, WAXMAN, McANDREW, GIRAL, FREEMAN, HADDOCK, MERSKI, ISAACSON, T. DAVIS, MAYES, HILL-EVANS, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, KHAN, KENYATTA, HOWARD, SANCHEZ, CERRATO, RIVERA, SCHLOSSBERG, O'MARA AND CONKLIN, APRIL 1, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, APRIL 1, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing June 14, 2025, as "World Blood Donor Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, A blood donor is any individual who is in good health, at least 16 years of age, weighs a minimum of 110 pounds and elects to donate blood; andWHEREAS, Blood donation can be made once every 56 days and as many as six times a year by a blood donor; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth has five major blood donation centers, including the American Red Cross Greater Pennsylvania Region, Central Pennsylvania Blood Bank, Community Blood Bank of Northwest PA and Western NY, Miller-Keystone Blood Center and Vitalant Blood Center; andWHEREAS, Every two seconds there is someone in the United States in need of blood or platelets; andWHEREAS, The only way to improve the supply of blood and platelets is through volunteer blood and platelet donors; and12345678910111213141516 WHEREAS, According to the American Cancer Society, more than 2 million individuals are estimated to have been diagnosed with cancer in the United States in 2024, and many of these individuals will need blood transfusions over the course of cancer treatment; andWHEREAS, O type blood is the blood type most in demand among hospitals; andWHEREAS, Approximately 45% of individuals across the United States have O type blood; andWHEREAS, Percentages of O type blood are higher for Latino and African Americans at 57% and 51% respectively; andWHEREAS, According to the American Red Cross, only 3% of people of eligible age donate blood each year; andWHEREAS, The World Health Organization recognizes June 14 each year as "World Blood Donor Day" to increase awareness about the need for blood donation; andWHEREAS, It is vitally important for all Pennsylvanians to be aware of the need for more blood donors across this Commonwealth; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize June 14, 2025, as "World Blood Donor Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0163PN1219 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
