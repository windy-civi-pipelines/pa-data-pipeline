2025-2026 Regular Session HR 88 PN 0718 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 718 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.88 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. MACKENZIE, GREINER, PICKETT, HAMM, PIELLI, REICHARD, STAATS, ZIMMERMAN, JAMES, KAUFFMAN, VENKAT, SMITH, HANBIDGE, KRUPA, MENTZER, NEILSON, GALLAGHER, BANTA, CIRESI AND GILLEN, FEBRUARY 21, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, FEBRUARY 21, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating August 7, 2025, as "Purple Heart Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, During major wars from World War I to the present, 32,080 Pennsylvania servicepersons were killed in combat and approximately 59,140 were wounded, making those Pennsylvanians eligible for the Purple Heart Medal; andWHEREAS, The mission of the Military Order of the Purple Heart, chartered by an act of Congress, is to foster an environment of goodwill among combat wounded veterans and their families, promote patriotism, support legislative initiatives and, most importantly, ensure that we never forget the sacrifices of those killed in war defending our freedom; andWHEREAS, Forty-five states across the United States have honored their servicepersons wounded and killed in combat by proclaiming their states to be Purple Heart states; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania was officially designated as a Purple 12345678910111213141516 Heart state in 2019; andWHEREAS, The Purple Heart Trail was established in 1992 by the Military Order of the Purple Heart as a symbolic trail throughout all 50 states to commemorate and honor all men and women who have been wounded or killed in combat while serving in the United States Armed Forces; andWHEREAS, The Purple Heart Trail originates at a monument in Mt. Vernon, Virginia, which is the burial location of George Washington; andWHEREAS, In this Commonwealth, Pennsylvania Route 45, also known as the Purple Heart Highway, winds through 100 miles of Huntingdon, Centre, Union, Northumberland and Montour Counties and is part of the Purple Heart Trail; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania Route 45 was christened the Purple Heart Highway because the Route 45 corridor has been home to many recipients of the Purple Heart; andWHEREAS, The Commonwealth of Pennsylvania honors the service and sacrifices of our nation's men and women in uniform wounded or killed by the enemy while serving to protect the freedom enjoyed by all Americans; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate August 7, 2025, as "Purple Heart Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0088PN0718 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
