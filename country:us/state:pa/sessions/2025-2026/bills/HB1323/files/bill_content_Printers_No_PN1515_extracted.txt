2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1323 PN 1515 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1515 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1323 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MULLINS, MAYES, HILL-EVANS, MADDEN, GIRAL, SANCHEZ, HARKINS, DONAHUE, INGLIS, KUZMA, CERRATO, MALAGARI, CIRESI, BOROWSKI, GREEN, WARREN AND PASHINSKI, APRIL 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON PROFESSIONAL LICENSURE, APRIL 28, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of May 22, 1951 (P.L.317, No.69), entitled "An act relating to the practice of professional nursing; providing for the licensing of nurses and for the revocation and suspension of such licenses, subject to appeal, and for their reinstatement; providing for the renewal of such licenses; regulating nursing in general; prescribing penalties and repealing certain laws," providing for advanced practice registered nurse.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of May 22, 1951 (P.L.317, No.69), known as The Professional Nursing Law, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 8.10. Advanced Practice Registered Nurse.--(a) The Board shall issue to each person who meets the following criteria a certificate setting forth that the person is certified as an advanced practice registered nurse:(1) the person holds a master's degree or post-master's degree; and(2) the person is licensed in one of the following 1234567891011121314151617181920 classifications:(i) Certified registered nurse practitioner. (ii) Clinical nurse specialist.(iii) Certified registered nurse anesthetist.(b) A person who is certified by the Board under subsection (a) is entitled to use the title "advanced practice registered nurse" and the letters "A.P.R.N." A record of each person certified as an advanced practice registered nurse in this Commonwealth shall be kept in the office of the Board and posted on the Board's publicly accessible Internet website.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1323PN1515 - 2 - 1234567891011
