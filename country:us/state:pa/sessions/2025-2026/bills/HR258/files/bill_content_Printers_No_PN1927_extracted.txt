2025-2026 Regular Session HR 258 PN 1927 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1927 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.258 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KAZEEM, RIVERA, GUENST, VENKAT, HILL-EVANS, HOHENSTEIN, PROBST, NEILSON, CONKLIN, SANCHEZ, BURGOS, MERSKI, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, GALLAGHER, D. WILLIAMS, SHUSTERMAN, DEASY, O'MARA, MENTZER AND SCHMITT, JUNE 13, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, JUNE 16, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the week of June 16 through 22, 2025, as "Women in Law Enforcement Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Pennsylvania State Police became the nation's first state police agency to fully integrate women troopers into the regular command structure in 1972; andWHEREAS, The fourteen women of the 31st Cadet Class paved the way for women in law enforcement in this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Today, there are approximately 450 women who serve in the Pennsylvania State Police, although women account for fewer than 10% of all State troopers; andWHEREAS, Women in law enforcement remain underrepresented throughout this Commonwealth and must contend with the persisting "glass ceiling," which can limit advancement into leadership roles; andWHEREAS, Many women in law enforcement must balance their careers with familial responsibilities of being a mother and caretaker; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Research has shown that increasing the number of women in law enforcement leads to positive outcomes, such as improved handling of domestic violence cases; andWHEREAS, Law enforcement professionals in this Commonwealth continuously make personal sacrifices to serve our communities throughout their careers; andWHEREAS, Women in law enforcement face unique challenges in the workforce due to their historic underrepresentation; andWHEREAS, These remarkable and courageous women deserve to be recognized for their contributions to the field of law enforcement in the face of such adversity; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the week of June 16 through 22, 2025, as "Women in Law Enforcement Week" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0258PN1927 - 2 - 1234567891011121314
