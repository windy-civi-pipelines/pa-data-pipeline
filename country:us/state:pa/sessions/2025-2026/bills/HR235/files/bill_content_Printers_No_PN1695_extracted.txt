2025-2026 Regular Session HR 235 PN 1695 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1695 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.235 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY O'MARA, MAYES, MALAGARI, VENKAT, SANCHEZ, HILL-EVANS, FREEMAN, KENYATTA, McNEILL, KHAN, DONAHUE, GIRAL, HOHENSTEIN, STEELE, SCHLOSSBERG, DALEY, DEASY, GREEN, MADDEN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, CERRATO, HADDOCK AND CURRY, MAY 12, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, MAY 13, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing July 25, 2025, as "World IVF Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, In vitro fertilization, or IVF, is a series of medical procedures designed to assist individuals or couples experiencing infertility issues get pregnant; andWHEREAS, The IVF process involves the extraction of eggs from the ovaries of the patient or donor, the fertilization of the eggs in a petri dish using sperm from a partner or donor and the transfer of the fertilized eggs to the uterus of the patient or a gestational carrier; andWHEREAS, Embryologists are necessary for the IVF process and all infertility treatments, as embryologists are the ones who monitor the fertilized eggs to ensure that they are healthy before transferring to the uterus; andWHEREAS, Embryologists also perform genetic testing to identify any potential genetic disorders that may be present in the fertilized egg, and embryologists can also perform 12345678910111213141516 Preimplantation Genetic Testing to determine if there are genetic mutations or chromosomal abnormalities present that may be the cause of miscarriage or infertility issues; andWHEREAS, Since the first baby to be successfully born from IVF on July 25, 1978, more than 10 million babies have been born using IVF worldwide, with more than 500,000 new IVF births each year; andWHEREAS, In the United States, roughly 2% of all births each year are the result of IVF; andWHEREAS, IVF often serves as the only chance for those experiencing fallopian tube damage, ovulation disorders, male factor infertility, recurrent miscarriages, genetic problems and other infertility issues to become pregnant and grow their family; andWHEREAS, The IVF process is not without risks, as the procedures not only carry a high financial cost, with each IVF cycle costing on average more than $15,000, but it also increases the odds of developing certain health complications, such as ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome or twisting of the ovary or fallopian tubes; andWHEREAS, There is also the risk of pregnancy complications, such as multiple pregnancies and ectopic pregnancies as well as low birth weight and premature birth; andWHEREAS, Despite the potential risks, access to IVF is crucial for couples who may not be able to conceive without the process; andWHEREAS, In recognition of the important role embryologists play in providing infertility treatment to those who wish to start a family, "World Embryologist Day" is celebrated on July 25; and20250HR0235PN1695 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 WHEREAS, In celebration of the advancements made in fertility medicine, "World IVF Day" is celebrated on July 25, the birthday of the first IVF baby; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize July 25, 2025, as "World IVF Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0235PN1695 - 3 - 12345
