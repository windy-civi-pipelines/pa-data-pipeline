Short title.This act shall be known and may be cited as the Commercial and Industrial NONRESIDENTIAL Products and Property Protection Act.Section 2. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this act shall have the meanings given to them in this section unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Commercial entity." The term does not include a fence system used primarily for the containment of livestock or other animals."DEPARTMENT." CONSTRUCTION.NOTHING IN THIS ACT SHALL BE CONSTRUED TO APPLY TO SECURITY ALARM SYSTEMS THAT DO NOT ATTACH TO A SECURITY FENCE SYSTEM CAPABLE OF DELIVERING AN ELECTRIC PULSE.Section 5 6.
