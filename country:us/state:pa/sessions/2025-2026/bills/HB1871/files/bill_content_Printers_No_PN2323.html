AN ACT
Providing for the installation of electrified security systems
on commercial and industrial entities' property.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Short title.
This act shall be known and may be cited as the Commercial
and Industrial Products and Property Protection Act.
Section 2. Definitions.
The following words and phrases when used in this act shall
have the meanings given to them in this section unless the
context clearly indicates otherwise:
"Commercial entity." An entity authorized to do business in
this Commonwealth by being certified to conduct business by the
Department of State.
"Electrified security system." An alarm system with an
assembly of battery-charged equipment and all of its integrated
components and equipment, including a monitored alarm device
intended to notify the entity monitoring the alarm system of

"Municipality." A county, city, township, borough,

Section 3. Electrified security system requirements.
(a) Installation.--A commercial entity or industrial entity
located in this Commonwealth may install an electrified security
system in accordance with this act to protect property, products
and any other commercial and industrial-related interests of the
commercial entity or industrial entity from any unlawful
actions.
(b) Requirements for compliance.--An electrified security
system installation shall:
(1) Comply with the specifications in the 3.0 edition of
the International Electrotechnical Commission Standard 60335-
2-76.
(2) Have an energizer that is driven by a commercial
storage battery not to exceed 12 volts direct current.
(3) Interface with a monitored alarm device that
transmits a signal to the alarm monitoring company in
response to an intrusion. The system may not directly connect
to or call law enforcement.
(4) Be located four inches to eight inches behind a
nonelectrified perimeter barrier not less than five feet in

(5) Be located on property that is not designated by a

(6) Be clearly identified with warning signs containing
the international electrified warning symbol at intervals of
not greater than 30 linear feet.
(c) Installer requirements.--The installer of an electrified
security system shall provide notice to the municipality that
the electrified security system has been installed pursuant to
subsection (b) and the notice shall contain the alarm company
installation name, the business entity name and address where
the electrified security system was installed.
(d) Local regulation.--A municipality may:
(1) Require registration of an alarm system for the
electrified security system.
(2) Prohibit an electrified security system that does
not meet the requirements of this section.
Section 4. Construction.
Nothing in this act shall be construed to apply to 18 Pa.C.S.
§ 7511(f) (relating to control of alarm devices and automatic
dialing devices).
Section 5. Effective date.
This act shall take effect immediately.
