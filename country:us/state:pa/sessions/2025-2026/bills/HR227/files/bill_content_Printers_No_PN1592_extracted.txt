2025-2026 Regular Session HR 227 PN 1592 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1592 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.227 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY FLICK, REICHARD, HAMM, FLEMING, BRENNAN, PICKETT, OLSOMMER, N. NELSON, VENKAT, GREINER, E. NELSON, PUGH, HADDOCK, PROBST, ROWE, TWARDZIK, KLUNK, SANCHEZ, COOK, GALLAGHER, CUTLER, MARCELL, NEILSON, ARMANINI, GAYDOS, JAMES, FRITZ AND WARREN, MAY 5, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, MAY 5, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the week of August 17 through 23, 2025, as "Little League World Series Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Pennsylvania's proud tradition of youth sports shines brightly each summer during the Little League Baseball® World Series, held annually in South Williamsport, Lycoming County; andWHEREAS, Founded in Pennsylvania in 1939, Little League has grown into the world's largest organized youth sports program, having a positive impact on millions of children and families; andWHEREAS, The Little League program represents an organization that is strong in vision, values and purpose; andWHEREAS, For more than seven decades, the Little League World Series has brought together young athletes to compete in the spirit of teamwork, perseverance and sportsmanship; andWHEREAS, Over the course of the baseball postseason months, 12345678910111213141516 Little Leagues across the globe select All Star baseball teams and compete in local and regional tournaments, with the goal to earn a spot in the Little League World Series; andWHEREAS, The Little League World Series highlights the dedicated athletes, but has one of the strongest networks of dedicated volunteers who serve as coaches, umpires, mentors and supporters, who selflessly give their time and energy to help young people thrive both on and off the field; andWHEREAS, The Little League World Series not only celebrates athletic excellence but also emphasizes character development, leadership and lifelong friendships; andWHEREAS, Each year, the Little League World Series draws tens of thousands of visitors to Pennsylvania, boosting the local economy and fostering a sense of community and pride; andWHEREAS, The Little League World Series serves as a shining example of the power of youth sports to bring people together across cultures and continents; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the week of August 17 through 23, 2025, as "Little League World Series Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives celebrate this iconic event and the values it promotes; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That all Pennsylvanians be encouraged to observe "Little League World Series Week" by attending games, recognizing the contributions of local volunteers and supporting youth sports in their communities.20250HR0227PN1592 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526
