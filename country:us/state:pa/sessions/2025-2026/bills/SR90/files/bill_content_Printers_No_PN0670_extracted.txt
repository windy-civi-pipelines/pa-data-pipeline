2025-2026 Regular Session SR 90 PN 0670 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 670 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.90 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MASTRIANO AND STEFANO, APRIL 28, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, APRIL 28, 2025 A RESOLUTIONEncouraging initiatives that support individuals demonstrating character and merit.WHEREAS, Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., powerfully affirmed that individuals should be judged not by the color of their skin but by the content of their character; andWHEREAS, The principles of equality and individual merit are foundational to the American republic and reflect the aspirations of our leaders, including William Penn and Abraham Lincoln; andWHEREAS, Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI) initiatives, though often well-intentioned, have in practice contributed to division and disunity in both public and private sectors; andWHEREAS, Prioritizing identity categories, including race, gender or perceived group membership may undermine the cohesion, unity and equal opportunity that merit- and character-based systems strive to promote; andWHEREAS, Team effectiveness and social harmony are best served by recognizing individual actions, character and 123456789101112131415161718 achievement over immutable or identity-based traits; andWHEREAS, It is in the public interest to affirm policies that unite rather than divide and to ensure that opportunities are distributed based on individual merit, ability and contribution; andWHEREAS, Abraham Lincoln wisely warned that "A house divided against itself cannot stand," and Dr. King envisioned a nation where all individuals are truly treated as equals under the law; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate encourage initiatives that support individuals who demonstrate character and merit; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate affirm a strong commitment to supporting demonstrated merit and character as a basis for opportunity in all realms of public service and governance, encourage the reevaluation of DEI programs that promote division, and support the adoption of initiatives that honor unity, fairness and opportunity for all individuals based on their demonstrated character and merit.20250SR0090PN0670 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819
