2025-2026 Regular Session SR 113 PN 0852 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 852 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.113 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CULVER, VOGEL, LANGERHOLC, BROWN, FONTANA, HAYWOOD AND CAPPELLETTI, MAY 30, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, MAY 30, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating May 29, 2025, as "College and Career Savings Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Pennsylvania 529 College and Career Savings Program was signed into law as the Tuition Account Programs and College and Career Savings Bond Act on April 3, 1992; andWHEREAS, The PA 529 College and Career Savings Program gives Pennsylvania residents a tax-advantaged way to save for postsecondary education, reducing the need for borrowing; andWHEREAS, Residents of this Commonwealth can choose to save through the PA 529 Guaranteed Savings Plan, in which savings grow based on college tuition inflation, or the PA 529 Investment Plan, in which savings grow based on investment decisions and market performance; andWHEREAS, The PA 529 Investment Plan was awarded the Morningstar Gold Rating, ranking it as one of only five 529 plans in the nation to achieve such a designation, and highlighting the Pennsylvania Treasury's impressive record of effective governance, reduction in plan fees and well-defined 123456789101112131415161718 plans for career development and succession planning; andWHEREAS, The Treasury Department has reduced fees associated with the PA 529 College and Career Savings Program twice since 2024, creating savings for families of nearly $1.3 million; andWHEREAS, PA 529 accounts offer generous tax benefits, including a State income tax deduction for contributions, no Federal or State income taxes on investment growth, tax-free withdrawals for qualified expenses and gift and inheritance tax benefits and PA 529 account assets have no effect on State financial aid eligibility; andWHEREAS, More than 314,000 families are currently saving with PA 529, including almost 200,000 families in the PA 529 Investment Plan, collectively holding substantial savings for future educational expenses; andWHEREAS, Savings in PA 529 accounts can be used to help pay for tuition, fees, equipment, books, room and board and other qualified expenses at most schools nationwide, including K-12, four-year colleges and universities, community colleges, technical schools and qualified apprenticeship programs; andWHEREAS, The Treasury Department has the statutory responsibility to administer the PA 529 College and Career Savings Program; andWHEREAS, Designating May 29, 2025, as "College and Career Savings Day" serves to increase public understanding and appreciation of the value of saving for postsecondary education through the PA 529 College and Career Savings Program; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate designate May 29, 2025, as "College and Career Savings Day" in Pennsylvania.20250SR0113PN0852 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526272829
