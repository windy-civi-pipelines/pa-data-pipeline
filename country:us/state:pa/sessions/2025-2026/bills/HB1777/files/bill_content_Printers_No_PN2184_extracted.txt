Any order or proclamation declaring, continuing or terminating a local disaster emergency shall be given prompt and general publicity and shall be filed promptly with the agency. The provisions of 53 Pa.C.S. § 1122.1 (relating to emergency meetings by authorized telecommunications device) shall apply to any meeting held by the governing body of a municipality under this section.* * *Section 2.
