AN ACT
Amending Title 42 (Judiciary and Judicial Procedure) of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in sentencing, providing
for sentencing for offenses involving protection from abuse
violations.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 42 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
§ 9720.9. Sentencing for offenses involving protection from
abuse violations.
In addition to any minimum term of imprisonment authorized or
established by law, an individual who commits a criminal offense
during the performance of a violation of an offense under 23
Pa.C.S. § 6114 (relating to contempt for violation of order or
agreement) shall have the criminal offense graded one degree
higher up to and including a felony of the third degree.
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
