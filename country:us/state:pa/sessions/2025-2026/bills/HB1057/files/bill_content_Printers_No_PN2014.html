AN ACT
Amending the act of May 11, 1889 (P.L.188, No.210), entitled "A
further supplement to an act, entitled 'An act to establish a
board of wardens for the Port of Philadelphia, and for the
regulation of pilots and pilotage, and for other purposes,'
approved March twenty-ninth, one thousand eight hundred and
three, and for regulating the rates of pilotage and number of
pilots," further providing for rates of pilotage and
computation, for pilotage fees and unit charge and for
charges for services.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:

Philadelphia, and for the regulation of pilots and pilotage, and
for other purposes,' approved March twenty-ninth, one thousand
eight hundred and three, and for regulating the rates of
pilotage and number of pilots," are amended to read:
