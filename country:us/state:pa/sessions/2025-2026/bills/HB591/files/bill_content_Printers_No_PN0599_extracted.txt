2025-2026 Regular Session HB 591 PN 0599 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 599 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.591 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY NEILSON, DOUGHERTY, SIEGEL, GIRAL, SANCHEZ, PIELLI, HILL-EVANS, CIRESI, SCHLOSSBERG, HOHENSTEIN, KHAN, GALLAGHER, DEASY AND GREEN, FEBRUARY 12, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, FEBRUARY 12, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of November 22, 1978 (P.L.1166, No.274), entitled "An act establishing the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency, providing for its powers and duties establishing several advisory committees within the commission and providing for their powers and duties," further providing for definitions and for powers and duties of the commission; and establishing the Constable William Davidson Reward Fund.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 1 of the act of November 22, 1978 (P.L.1166, No.274), referred to as the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency Law, is amended by adding a definition to read:Section 1. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this act shall have, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise, the meanings given to them in this section:* * *"Law enforcement officer." The term shall have the same 1234567891011121314151617181920 meaning as the term "peace officer" under 18 Pa.C.S. ยง 501 (relating to definitions).* * *Section 2. Section 3 of the act is amended by adding a paragraph to read:Section 3. Powers and duties of the commission.The commission shall have the power and its duty shall be:* * *(19) To offer and cause to be paid rewards of up to $50,000 for information leading to the arrest and conviction of perpetrators of an offense against a law enforcement officer under 18 Pa.C.S. ยง 2502 (relating to murder) or 2507 (relating to criminal homicide of law enforcement officer).Section 3. The act is amended by adding a section to read:Section 8.2. Constable William Davidson Reward Fund.(a) Establishment of fund.--The Constable William Davidson Reward Fund is established as a nonlapsing fund in the State Treasury.(b) Administration.--The fund shall be administered by the commission. The commission shall use money in the fund solely for the purpose specified under section 3(19).(c) Fund sources.--Funding shall consist of appropriations by the General Assembly.(d) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Fund." The Constable William Davidson Reward Fund.Section 4. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0591PN0599 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122232425262728
