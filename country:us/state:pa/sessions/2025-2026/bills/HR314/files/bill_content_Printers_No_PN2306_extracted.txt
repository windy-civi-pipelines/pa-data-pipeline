2025-2026 Regular Session HR 314 PN 2306 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2306 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.314 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY McNEILL, PICKETT, VENKAT, SANCHEZ, HILL-EVANS, GIRAL, JAMES, PROBST, BRENNAN, FREEMAN, PIELLI, VITALI, HOHENSTEIN, HOWARD, NEILSON AND RIVERA, SEPTEMBER 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, SEPTEMBER 11, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating September 18, 2025, as "U.S. Air Force Birthday" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The United States military obtained its first aircraft in 1909; andWHEREAS, The Air Force was not initially a separate and independent branch of the military, but was part of the United States Army; andWHEREAS, The United States Air Force had several designations such as the Aeronautical Section, Signal Corps (1909), Aviation Section, Signal Corps (1914), United States Army Air Service (1918), United States Army Corps (1926) and United States Army Air Forces (1941); andWHEREAS, In 1939, the United States Air Force expanded from 20,000 military personnel and 2,400 planes to 2.4 million military personnel and 80,000 aircrafts by 1944; andWHEREAS, Following World War II, the Department of Defense was created, which led to the merging of military branches under 1234567891011121314151617 a unified command composed of a Joint Chiefs of Staff, including Army, Navy and Air Force leaders; andWHEREAS, On July 26, 1947, President Harry S. Truman signed the National Security Act of 1947, which led to the establishment of the United States Air Force as a separate and independent branch of the military on September 18, 1947; andWHEREAS, This establishment gave the Air Force the same status as the United States Army and United States Navy, strengthening its contribution in national defense; andWHEREAS, The reorganization of the United States Air Force immensely improved the operational and tactical aptitudes of the military while guaranteeing an efficient approach to national security; andWHEREAS, The United States Air Force's birthday is commemorated on September 18 annually; andWHEREAS, It is important that the House of Representatives recognizes the formal establishment of the United States Air Force and celebrates Air Force personnel; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate September 18, 2025, as "U.S. Air Force Birthday" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0314PN2306 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
