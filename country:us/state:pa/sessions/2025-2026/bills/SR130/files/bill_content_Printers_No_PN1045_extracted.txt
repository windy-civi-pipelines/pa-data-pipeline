2025-2026 Regular Session SR 130 PN 1045 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1045 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.130 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SCHWANK, SANTARSIERO, COSTA, HUGHES, J. WARD, MALONE, MASTRIANO, SAVAL, COLLETT, PENNYCUICK, TARTAGLIONE, KIM, FONTANA, KANE, BAKER, COMITTA, ARGALL, PHILLIPS-HILL, STREET, HAYWOOD, VOGEL, STEFANO AND CAPPELLETTI, JULY 10, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, JULY 10, 2025 A RESOLUTIONCondemning the antisemitic firebombing attack on June 1, 2025, in Boulder, Colorado, against peaceful demonstrators and calling for the Commonwealth to combat the continued rise of antisemitism.WHEREAS, On June 1, 2025, during a Run for Their Lives walk, which advocated for the safe release of hostages being held by Hamas, a terrorist firebombed peaceful demonstrators for simply being Jewish or supporting the Jewish community; andWHEREAS, This act of violence wounded 12 people, who suffered burns and other injuries; andWHEREAS, The oldest victim, who is 88 years of age, also survived the Holocaust; andWHEREAS, Run for Their Lives is a national peaceful organization with chapters in 35 states and several within communities in Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, Everyone should hope for the quick healing and recovery of the victims of this violent antisemitic attack; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, The Jewish people comprise 2.4% of the population of the United States but are the victims of 67% of all religious-based hate crimes in the United States; andWHEREAS, Antisemitic incidents have risen by an estimated 344% over the past five years; andWHEREAS, Many incidents have occurred in this Commonwealth, including:(1) the attack on the Governor's Residence;(2) the distribution of antisemitic flyers at the location of Tree of Life Massacre; and(3) the attempted arson and burglary on Congregation Mikveh Israel;andWHEREAS, These violent antisemitic attacks are not just isolated to this Commonwealth, they are happening nationwide, including the murder of two Israeli diplomats in Washington, DC; andWHEREAS, These reprehensible acts against the Jewish community must end, and it is our moral and ethical duty to condemn the hate, violence and murder of Jewish people and to work and connect with the Jewish community to combat the rise of antisemitism; andWHEREAS, The Jewish community in this Commonwealth, nationwide and throughout the world should be able to live their lives without fear of being harassed or attacked for simply being Jewish; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate condemn the antisemitic firebombing attack on June 1, 2025, in Boulder, Colorado, against peaceful demonstrators, and call for this Commonwealth to combat the continued rise of antisemitism; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate pledge its solidarity and unwavering support for the Jewish community and the continued work to combat the rise of antisemitism in this Commonwealth.20250SR0130PN1045 - 3 - 1234567
