AN ACT
Amending Title 42 (Judiciary and Judicial Procedure) of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in facilities and
supplies, further providing for deposits into account.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 3733(a) heading of Title 42 of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended and the subsection
is amended by adding a paragraph to read:
§ 3733. Deposits into account.
(a) [General rule] Deposits.--
* * *
(3) Beginning on the effective date of this paragraph,
fines, fees or costs which are allocated by law or otherwise
directed to the Department of Agriculture under the act of
December 7, 1982 (P.L.784, No.225), known as the Dog Law,
shall not be affected by this subchapter.
* * *
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
