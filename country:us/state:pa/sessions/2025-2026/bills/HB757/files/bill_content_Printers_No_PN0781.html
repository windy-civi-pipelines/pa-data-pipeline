subsection to read:
§ 2206. Consumer protections and customer service.

(1) A natural gas supplier engaged in the sale of

under this paragraph must occur at a location mutually agreed

Section 3. Section 2807 heading of Title 66 is amended and
the section is amended by adding a subsection to read:
§ 2807. Duties of electric distribution companies and electric
distribution suppliers.

(1) An electricity supplier engaged in the sale of
electric power in person, including the sale of electric

the following duties:

only during the hours of 9 a.m. until 5 p.m., Monday
through Saturday.

of the customer's option to verify a solicitation
activity by furnishing the customer's phone number to the
electricity supplier and the electric distribution
company.

supplier engaged in the sale of electric power in person,
including the sale of electric power door-to-door or in any
