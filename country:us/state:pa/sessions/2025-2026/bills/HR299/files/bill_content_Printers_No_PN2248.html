A RESOLUTION
Recognizing October 3, 2025, as "Kids Music Day" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, October 3, 2025, marks the 10th anniversary of "Kids
Music Day"; and
WHEREAS, "Kids Music Day" is a national holiday founded by
the Brookhaven nonprofit Keep Music Alive; and
WHEREAS, The mission of Keep Music Alive is to help more
children reap the educational, therapeutic and social benefits
of playing music; and
WHEREAS, More than 80 schools in 24 states participated in
the inaugural "Kids Music Day" on October 7, 2016; and
WHEREAS, By October 2, 2020, more than 1,000 music schools,
music stores and other music organizations across the United
States and Canada conducted special events and physical and
virtual promotions to celebrate the fifth anniversary of "Kids
Music Day"; and
WHEREAS, Currently, more than 1,200 music schools, stores and
