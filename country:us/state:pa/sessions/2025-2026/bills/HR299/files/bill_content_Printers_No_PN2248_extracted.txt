2025-2026 Regular Session HR 299 PN 2248 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2248 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.299 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KRUEGER, CONKLIN, GREINER, HILL-EVANS, VENKAT, FREEMAN, GUZMAN, SANCHEZ, HARKINS, GIRAL, HOWARD, GALLAGHER, NEILSON, RIVERA, MAYES, KHAN AND CERRATO, AUGUST 26, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON CHILDREN AND YOUTH, AUGUST 28, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing October 3, 2025, as "Kids Music Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, October 3, 2025, marks the 10th anniversary of "Kids Music Day"; andWHEREAS, "Kids Music Day" is a national holiday founded by the Brookhaven nonprofit Keep Music Alive; andWHEREAS, The mission of Keep Music Alive is to help more children reap the educational, therapeutic and social benefits of playing music; andWHEREAS, More than 80 schools in 24 states participated in the inaugural "Kids Music Day" on October 7, 2016; andWHEREAS, By October 2, 2020, more than 1,000 music schools, music stores and other music organizations across the United States and Canada conducted special events and physical and virtual promotions to celebrate the fifth anniversary of "Kids Music Day"; andWHEREAS, Currently, more than 1,200 music schools, stores and 1234567891011121314151617 organizations across the United States celebrate "Kids Music Day" each year by offering special events and promotions that benefit children playing music; andWHEREAS, "Kids Music Day" events and activities include musical instrument petting zoos, student performances, both in-house or out in the community, instrument donation drives, kids open mics and more; andWHEREAS, In 2024, nearly 50 Pennsylvania locations participated in the ninth annual "Kids Music Day"; andWHEREAS, "Kids Music Day" is commemorated on the first Friday of October each year; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize October 3, 2025, as "Kids Music Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0299PN2248 - 2 - 12345678910111213
