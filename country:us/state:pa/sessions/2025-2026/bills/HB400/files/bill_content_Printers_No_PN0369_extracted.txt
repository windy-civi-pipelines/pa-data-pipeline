2025-2026 Regular Session HB 400 PN 0369 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 369 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.400 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY STAMBAUGH AND ZIMMERMAN, JANUARY 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON GAME AND FISHERIES, JANUARY 28, 2025 AN ACTDesignating the passenger pigeon (Ectopistes migratorius) as the official extinct species of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Findings and declarations.The General Assembly finds and declares as follows:(1) The passenger pigeon was once an abundant native species of this Commonwealth.(2) The passenger pigeon contributed greatly to the early history of this Commonwealth, including through hunting for food and sport.(3) The legacy of passenger pigeons in this Commonwealth endures through numerous places named in their honor.(4) At one time, there may have been more passenger pigeons in North America than all other birds in the world combined.(5) The extinction of the passenger pigeon helped contribute to awareness and support for preservation of other 123456789101112131415161718 endangered species.Section 2. Official State extinct animal.The passenger pigeon (Ectopistes migratorius) is designated as the official extinct species of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.Section 3. Effective date.This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0400PN0369 - 2 - 1234567
