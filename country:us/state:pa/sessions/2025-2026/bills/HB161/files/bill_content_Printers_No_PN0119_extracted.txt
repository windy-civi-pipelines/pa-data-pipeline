2025-2026 Regular Session HB 161 PN 0119 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 119 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.161 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY GLEIM, HAMM, LEADBETER, KAUFFMAN AND ZIMMERMAN, JANUARY 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LABOR AND INDUSTRY, JANUARY 16, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of August 15, 1961 (P.L.987, No.442), entitled "An act relating to public works contracts; providing for prevailing wages; imposing duties upon the Secretary of Labor and Industry; providing remedies, penalties and repealing existing laws," further providing for definitions.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 2(5) of the act of August 15, 1961 (P.L.987, No.442), known as the Pennsylvania Prevailing Wage Act, is amended and the section is amended by adding a paragraph to read:Section 2. Definitions.--As used in this act--* * *(5) "Public work" means construction, reconstruction, demolition, alteration and/or repair work other than maintenance work, done under contract and paid for in whole or in part out of the funds of a public body where the estimated cost of the total project is in excess of twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000), but shall not include school safety and security 12345678910111213141516171819 improvements or work performed under a rehabilitation or manpower training program.* * *(11) "School safety and security improvements" means construction, reconstruction, demolition, alteration or repair work performed to enhance or improve school safety and security, which is:(i) recommended or identified by a school safety and security assessment performed under Article XIII-B of the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the "Public School Code of 1949";(ii) paid for in whole or in part from money provided under section 1306-B of the "Public School Code of 1949"; or(iii) identified in the criteria developed by the school safety and security committee under section 1303-B of the "Public School Code of 1949."Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0161PN0119 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617
