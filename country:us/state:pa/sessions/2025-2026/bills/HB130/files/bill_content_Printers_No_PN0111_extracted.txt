The following amendment to the Constitution of Pennsylvania is proposed in accordance with Article XI:That section 14 of Article III be amended to read:ยง 14. Public school system funding.The General Assembly shall provide for the maintenance and support of a thorough and efficient system of public education funding to serve the needs of [the Commonwealth.] students in this Commonwealth. Education spending by the Commonwealth shall be allocated to individual students to attend the schools that fit the students' needs.Section 2. The following procedure applies to the proposed constitutional amendment in this joint resolution:(1) Upon the first passage by the General Assembly of 123456789101112131415161718 the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.(2) Upon the second passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.
