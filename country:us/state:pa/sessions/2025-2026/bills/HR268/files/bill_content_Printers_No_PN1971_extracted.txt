2025-2026 Regular Session HR 268 PN 1971 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1971 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.268 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY GOUGHNOUR, JUNE 20, 2025 INTRODUCED AS NONCONTROVERSIAL RESOLUTION UNDER RULE 35, JUNE 20, 2025 A RESOLUTIONMourning the loss of the Honorable Matthew R. Gergely of McKeesport, Allegheny County, respected member of the House of Representatives from the 35th District, who died January 19, 2025, at 45 years of age.WHEREAS, The House of Representatives commends Representative Gergely's distinguished service to the General Assembly from February 21, 2023, through January 19, 2025; andWHEREAS, The House of Representatives commends Representative Gergely's important contributions to the Mon Valley and the Allegheny County delegation; andWHEREAS, A former McKeesport Area School District business manager and McKeesport city administrator and chief revenue officer, Representative Gergely won the House of Representatives' special election on February 7, 2023; andWHEREAS, Representative Gergely was reelected to the House on November 5, 2024; andWHEREAS, Representative Gergely's committee assignments included the Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure Committee, Game and Fisheries Committee, Insurance Committee and 12345678910111213141516171819 Liquor Control Committee; andWHEREAS, In April 2024, Representative Gergely's bill amending The Clinical Laboratory Act to enhance plasma supply was enacted as Act 6 of 2024; andWHEREAS, In July 2024, Representative Gergely's Liquor Code bill providing flexibility for licensee hiring was enacted as Act 57 of 2024; andWHEREAS, To honor Representative Gergely, the Flag of Pennsylvania is flying at half-staff at public buildings until his interment at McKeesport and Versailles Cemetery; andWHEREAS, A graduate of McKeesport Area High School and Indiana University of Pennsylvania, Representative Gergely was a son of Sofia Lundberg Simile and the late Robert S. Gergely; andWHEREAS, Representative Gergely's life's work in municipal and State government reflected his ideals for public and personal investment in health, safety, education and employment; andWHEREAS, Representative Gergely faithfully dedicated his energies to solving problems and allocating resources for the benefit of residents of his native McKeesport and western Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, At his legislative offices in Harrisburg, McKeesport, Clairton and Munhall, Representative Gergely improved their lives through his focus on constituent services, his notable fairness and his easy friendships; andWHEREAS, Representative Gergely's pride and care for his wife, Holly Warren Gergely, and their young sons, Mason and Jerrett, was central to this work; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives mourn the loss of the Honorable Matthew R.
