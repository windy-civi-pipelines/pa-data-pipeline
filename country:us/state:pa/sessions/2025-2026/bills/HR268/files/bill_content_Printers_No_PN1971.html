A RESOLUTION
Mourning the loss of the Honorable Matthew R. Gergely of
McKeesport, Allegheny County, respected member of the House
of Representatives from the 35th District, who died January
19, 2025, at 45 years of age.
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives commends Representative
Gergely's distinguished service to the General Assembly from
February 21, 2023, through January 19, 2025; and
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives commends Representative
Gergely's important contributions to the Mon Valley and the
Allegheny County delegation; and
WHEREAS, A former McKeesport Area School District business
manager and McKeesport city administrator and chief revenue
officer, Representative Gergely won the House of
Representatives' special election on February 7, 2023; and
WHEREAS, Representative Gergely was reelected to the House on
November 5, 2024; and
WHEREAS, Representative Gergely's committee assignments
included the Consumer Protection and Professional Licensure
Committee, Game and Fisheries Committee, Insurance Committee and

WHEREAS, In April 2024, Representative Gergely's bill

and State government reflected his ideals for public and
personal investment in health, safety, education and employment;
and
WHEREAS, Representative Gergely faithfully dedicated his
energies to solving problems and allocating resources for the
benefit of residents of his native McKeesport and western
Pennsylvania; and
WHEREAS, At his legislative offices in Harrisburg,
McKeesport, Clairton and Munhall, Representative Gergely
improved their lives through his focus on constituent services,
his notable fairness and his easy friendships; and
WHEREAS, Representative Gergely's pride and care for his
wife, Holly Warren Gergely, and their young sons, Mason and
Jerrett, was central to this work; therefore be it
