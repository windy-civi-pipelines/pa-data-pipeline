2025-2026 Regular Session HB 88 PN 0071 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 71 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.88 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY OWLETT, PICKETT, HAMM, CAUSER, ROWE, KENYATTA, JAMES, COOK, SMITH, NEILSON AND ZIMMERMAN, JANUARY 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON ENVIRONMENTAL AND NATURAL RESOURCE PROTECTION, JANUARY 14, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), entitled "An act creating the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources consisting of certain functions of the Department of Environmental Resources and the Department of Community Affairs; renaming the Department of Environmental Resources as the Department of Environmental Protection; defining the role of the Environmental Quality Board in the Department of Environmental Protection; making changes to responsibilities of the State Conservation Commission and the Department of Agriculture; transferring certain powers and duties to the Department of Health; and repealing inconsistent acts," in Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, providing for money collected from oil or gas leases under publicly owned streambeds.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), known as the Conservation and Natural Resources Act, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 323. Money collected from oil or gas leases under publicly owned streambeds.(a) Money collected beginning January 1, 2014.--The department shall estimate the amount of money collected by the 1234567891011121314151617181920212223 department beginning January 1, 2014, and ending on the effective date of this subsection, from an oil or gas lease under a publicly owned streambed in each county where such a streambed is located. No later than 60 days from the effective date of this subsection, the department shall distribute an amount of money equal to the estimated amount of money collected by the department beginning January 1, 2014, and ending on the effective date of this subsection, in a single lump-sum payment to each county specified under this subsection.(b) Money collected after effective date of subsection.--The department shall distribute the money collected by the department after the effective date of this subsection from an oil or gas lease under a publicly owned streambed to the county where the streambed is located.(c) Use of money.--A county that is the recipient of money under this section shall use the money solely for the purpose of maintaining creeks and streams within the county at the county's discretion.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0088PN0071 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819
