2025-2026 Regular Session HB 850 PN 0880 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 880 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.850 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ISAACSON, CIRESI, FREEMAN, GUENST, KHAN, OTTEN, PIELLI, SANCHEZ, STEELE AND VITALI, MARCH 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON ENVIRONMENTAL AND NATURAL RESOURCE PROTECTION, MARCH 10, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 58 (Oil and Gas) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in development, providing for hydraulic fracturing fluid tracer.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 58 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 3222.2. Hydraulic fracturing fluid tracer.(a) Applicability.--This section shall apply to hydraulic fracturing of unconventional wells performed on or after the effective date of this subsection.(b) Required additives.--A service provider who performs any part of a hydraulic fracturing treatment and a vendor who provides hydraulic fracturing chemicals directly to the operator for a hydraulic fracturing treatment shall include a tracer substance in the base stimulation fluid used in the alteration of an unconventional well.(c) Specifications.--The department shall specify the type 123456789101112131415161718 of tracer substance that the operator is required to use in the alteration of an unconventional well. The following shall apply:(1) The tracer substance must be traceable to a specific operator after the tracer substance is used in the alteration of an unconventional well.(2) The department shall determine that the use of the tracer substance will not endanger public health or the environment.(d) Regulations.--The Environmental Quality Board shall promulgate regulations necessary to meet the requirements of this section.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0850PN0880 - 2 - 123456789101112
