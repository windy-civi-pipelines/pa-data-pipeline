2025-2026 Regular Session SB 744 PN 0794 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 794 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE BILL No.744 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY DUSH, ARGALL, HUTCHINSON, LAUGHLIN AND STEFANO, MAY 12, 2025 REFERRED TO JUDICIARY, MAY 12, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 61 (Prisons and Parole) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in miscellaneous provisions relating to inmate confinement, providing for overwatch towers.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 61 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 5912. Overwatch towers.(a) Staffing of overwatch towers.--At a correctional institution with an overwatch tower, an overwatch tower shall be manned by corrections officers during any outdoor inmate movement.(b) Exceptions.--Subsection (a) shall not apply to an overwatch tower from which inmates cannot be supervised during outdoor inmate movement.(c) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:123456789101112131415161718 "Overwatch tower." A tall building, freestanding or as part of another structure, from which corrections officers can supervise inmates.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250SB0744PN0794 - 2 - 1234
