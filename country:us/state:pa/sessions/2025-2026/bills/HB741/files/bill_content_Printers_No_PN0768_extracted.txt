2025-2026 Regular Session HB 741 PN 0768 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 768 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.741 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MALONEY, BANTA, GUENST, HADDOCK, JAMES, McANDREW AND ZIMMERMAN, MARCH 3, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON GAME AND FISHERIES, MARCH 3, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 34 (Game) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in hunting and furtaking, providing for harvesting of certain antlered deer by senior license holders.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 34 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:§ 2330. Harvesting of certain antlered deer by senior license holders.(a) Authorization.--A senior license holder may harvest an antlered deer with two or more points on one antler or a spike three or more inches in length.(b) Definition.--As used in this subsec tion, t he term "point" shall have the meaning given the term in 58 Pa. Code § 131.2 (relating to definitions).Section 2. Any regulation or rule which conflicts with the addition of 34 Pa.C.S. § 2330 is abrogated.Section 3. This act shall take effect immediately.123456789101112131415161718
