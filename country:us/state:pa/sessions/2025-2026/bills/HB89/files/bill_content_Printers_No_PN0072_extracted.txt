2025-2026 Regular Session HB 89 PN 0072 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 72 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.89 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY OWLETT, PICKETT, GILLEN, KAUFFMAN, BANTA, WARNER, BURGOS, KENYATTA, SMITH, M. MACKENZIE AND PUGH, JANUARY 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, JANUARY 14, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 66 (Public Utilities) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in alternative form of regulation of telecommunications services, providing for compensation to volunteer fire companies for equipment or infrastructure failures.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 66 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:§ 3020. Compensation to volunteer fire companies for equipment or infrastructure failures.(a) Compensation.--If a local exchange telecommunications company's equipment or infrastructure failure results in the active deployment of a volunteer firefighter to the site of the failure, the company shall physically respond to the site of the failure within 90 minutes of receipt of notice of the failure. A local exchange telecommunications company that fails to physically respond within 90 minutes as required under this 123456789101112131415161718 subsection shall pay compensation to the volunteer fire company at a rate of $1,000 for each hour of active deployment of a volunteer firefighter.(b) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Volunteer fire company." As defined in 35 Pa.C.S. § 7802 (relating to definitions)."Volunteer firefighter." As defined in 35 Pa.C.S. § 7412 (relating to definitions).Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0089PN0072 - 2 - 1234567891011
