BROWN, SOLOMON, SHAFFER AND BONNER, JANUARY 23, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON STATE GOVERNMENT, JANUARY 23, 2025 A JOINT RESOLUTIONProposing an amendment to the Constitution of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, further providing for terms of members.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby resolves as follows:Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution of Pennsylvania is proposed in accordance with Article XI:That section 3 of Article II be amended to read:ยง 3. Terms of members.(a) Senators shall be elected for the term of four years and Representatives for the term of two years.(b) A Senator shall not be eligible for election to more than three consecutive full terms of office in the Senate. A Representative shall not be eligible for election to more than six consecutive full terms of office in the House of Representatives.(c) A Senator who has served three consecutive full terms in the Senate shall be eligible for election to the Senate at an 1234567891011121314151617 election held not less than four years after the completion of the Senator's third consecutive full term of office. A Representative who has served six consecutive full terms in the House of Representatives shall be eligible for election to the House of Representatives at an election held not less than four years after completion of the Representative's sixth consecutive full term of office.Section 2. The following procedure applies to the proposed constitutional amendment in this joint resolution:(1) Upon the first passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.(2) Upon the second passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania. The Secretary of the Commonwealth shall submit the amendment to the qualified electors of this Commonwealth at the first primary, general or municipal election which meets the requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.20250HB0301PN0244 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
