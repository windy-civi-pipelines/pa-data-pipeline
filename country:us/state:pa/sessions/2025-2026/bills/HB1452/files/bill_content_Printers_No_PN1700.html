AN ACT
Amending Title 51 (Military Affairs) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in Department of Military and Veterans
Affairs, further providing for veterans registry and for PA
VETConnect Program.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:

the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes are amended to read:

714. PA VETConnect Program.
* * *
(e) Duties of department.--The department shall develop and
administer the program, which shall contain a mission statement
that shall be clearly communicated to network advocates,
partners and participants under subsection (b). The department
shall:
(1) Coordinate with Federal agencies, State agencies,
county directors of veterans' affairs, veterans' service
organizations, accredited postsecondary educational and
technical institutions, the health care provider community,
businesses and nonprofit organizations, identified by the
department, that provide programs, initiatives, reintegration
outreach, support resources for military sexual trauma and
related services to veterans and the families of veterans.
* * *
Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
