AN ACT
Amending Title 3 (Agriculture) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in domestic animals, further providing for
definitions and for use of biologicals, antibiotics, genetic
material, chemicals, diagnostic agents and other substances.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 2303 of Title 3 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a definition to read:
2303. Definitions.
The following words and phrases when used in this chapter
shall have the meanings given to them in this section unless the
context clearly indicates otherwise:
* * *
"mRNA vaccine." A vaccine that includes a substance that
stimulates the production of antibodies and provides immunity
against disease by introducing messenger ribonucleic acid (mRNA)
that corresponds to a viral protein.
* * *
