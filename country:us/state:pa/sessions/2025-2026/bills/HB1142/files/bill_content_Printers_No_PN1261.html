AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in safety and anti-pollution equipment, further
providing for windshield obstructions and wipers.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 4524 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding subsections to read:
4524. Windshield obstructions and wipers.
* * *
(e.1) Vehicle safety inspection.--A motor vehicle which is
equipped with tinted windows shall be tested utilizing a window
tint meter as part of the motor vehicle safety inspection and
shall fail inspection if the motor vehicle violates the
provisions of 67 Pa. Code Ch. 175 Subch. O (relating to vehicle
sun screening devices) .

Protection Law.--Failing to inform a purchaser of window tinting
services for a motor vehicle that an alteration or modificatio n
