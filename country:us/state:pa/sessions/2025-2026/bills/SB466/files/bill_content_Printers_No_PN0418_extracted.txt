Section 7383.1(b) of Title 35 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:ยง 7383.1. Curriculum, training and education certification management system.* * *(b) Training.--(1) Training shall be lecture-based, hands on or online.(2) Training may be conducted at the Pennsylvania State Fire Academy, a county training center, a community college or other approved educational training agency.(3) The training records shall be incorporated into the training and education certification management system 123456789101112131415161718 approved by the commissioner.(4) An individual who successfully completes fire suppression training as a condition of employment may provide a letter or certificate stating the number of hours of training completed, which is signed by the individual who conducted the training, to the Office of the State Fire Commissioner to count towards the required number of hours of instruction needed to obtain a Firefighter I certification.* * *Section 2.
