MACKENZIE, NEILSON, PICKETT, REICHARD AND STAATS, FEBRUARY 20, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON COMMERCE, FEBRUARY 20, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating February 9 through 15, 2025, as "Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., (FBLA) is a nonprofit educational organization that inspires and prepares students to become community-minded business leaders in a global society through relevant career preparation and leadership experiences; andWHEREAS, The first Pennsylvania FBLA chapter was established at Blairsville High School on March 22, 1943; andWHEREAS, Lehigh Career and Technical Institute officially chartered its FBLA local chapter on January 24, 2000; andWHEREAS, FBLA includes more than 230,000 members and advisers in 4,600 chapters nationwide in middle schools, high schools, colleges, universities, career and technical schools and private business schools; andWHEREAS, FBLA members believe that education is the right of every person to develop a future that depends on mutual understanding and cooperation among business, industry, labor, 123456789101112131415161718 religious, family and educational institutions, as well as people around the world; andWHEREAS, FBLA members believe that every person has the right to earn a living at a useful occupation while actively working toward improving social, political, community and family life; andWHEREAS, FBLA teaches middle school, high school and college students basic business and leadership principles, assists them in the transition from school to work and teaches responsibility for carrying out assigned tasks in a manner that brings credit to self, associates, school and community; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate February 9 through 15, 2025, as "Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) Week" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0079PN0681 - 2 - 1234567891011121314
