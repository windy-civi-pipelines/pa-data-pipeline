A RESOLUTION
Designating February 9 through 15, 2025, as "Future Business
Leaders of America (FBLA) Week" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., (FBLA) is
a nonprofit educational organization that inspires and prepares
students to become community-minded business leaders in a global
society through relevant career preparation and leadership
experiences; and
WHEREAS, The first Pennsylvania FBLA chapter was established
at Blairsville High School on March 22, 1943; and
WHEREAS, Lehigh Career and Technical Institute officially
chartered its FBLA local chapter on January 24, 2000; and
WHEREAS, FBLA includes more than 230,000 members and advisers
in 4,600 chapters nationwide in middle schools, high schools,
colleges, universities, career and technical schools and private
business schools; and
WHEREAS, FBLA members believe that education is the right of
every person to develop a future that depends on mutual
understanding and cooperation among business, industry, labor,
