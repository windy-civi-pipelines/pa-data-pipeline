2025-2026 Regular Session HR 33 PN 0340 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 340 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.33 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MATZIE, VENKAT, D. MILLER, DEASY, HARKINS, SANCHEZ, MALAGARI, GIRAL, BURGOS, NEILSON, HILL-EVANS, HOWARD, SCHLOSSBERG, GREEN, MADDEN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ AND MAYES, JANUARY 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, JANUARY 28, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of April 2025 as "Limb Loss Awareness Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, More than 2 million Americans of all ages, races and genders have had amputations, and another 28 million Americans are at risk for amputation; andWHEREAS, Each day, more than 300 Americans lose a limb; andWHEREAS, Limb loss is a lifetime condition, and the general public is largely unaware of the many challenges faced by the amputee community; andWHEREAS, Limb loss can result in emotional, physical and financial stress; andWHEREAS, The leading causes of amputation are vascular disease, trauma and cancer; andWHEREAS, Prosthetic devices can be quite costly and often need to be replaced every few years; andWHEREAS, Individuals afflicted with limb loss must overcome many challenges; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, The physical effects of limb loss may be the most visible, but many times the emotional difficulties surpass the physical impediments; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of April 2025 as "Limb Loss Awareness Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage all Pennsylvanians to recognize the importance of this month, celebrate individuals with limb loss who are living full and productive lives, express gratitude to caregivers who are a source of support and motivation and salute combat amputees who have lost their limbs in service to our country.20250HR0033PN0340 - 2 - 123456789101112
