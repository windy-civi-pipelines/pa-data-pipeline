2025-2026 Regular Session HB 886 PN 0927 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 927 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.886 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY WEBSTER, SANCHEZ, MADDEN, PIELLI, GIRAL, McNEILL, KHAN, HILL-EVANS, MALAGARI AND CIRESI, MARCH 13, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, MARCH 13, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 61 (Prisons and Parole) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in general administration relating to correctional institutions, providing for prerelease briefings on veterans benefits.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 61 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 1107. Prerelease briefings on veterans benefits.Prior to the release from a State correctional institution of an inmate who is a veteran, the department shall conduct a briefing with the inmate to explain and share information relating to Federal and State benefits available to veterans and veterans' families and how to apply for the benefits.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.123456789101112131415
