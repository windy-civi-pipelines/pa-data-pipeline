2025-2026 Regular Session HR 89 PN 0719 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 719 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.89 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. MACKENZIE, GREINER, PIELLI, HAMM, GUENST, KAUFFMAN, VENKAT, REICHARD, JAMES, SMITH, FREEMAN, KRUPA, MENTZER, GALLAGHER, GLEIM, CIRESI, COOPER, LEADBETER AND GILLEN, FEBRUARY 21, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, FEBRUARY 21, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing March 29, 2025, as "National Vietnam War Veterans Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Vietnam War Veterans Recognition Act, signed in 2017, designates March 29 of each year as "National Vietnam War Veterans Day"; andWHEREAS, On this day, we honor and give thanks for the service and sacrifice of Vietnam War veterans and their families and for the valor of servicepersons killed or missing in action; andWHEREAS, We remember the long engagement of United States Armed Forces, wartime support organizations and our nation's allies as well as the contributions made by civilians; andWHEREAS, We reflect on the era's advances in technology, science and medical practice and the wealth of records and research associated with the war; andWHEREAS, The House of Representatives joins in paying tribute 12345678910111213141516 to United States military personnel called to active duty between November 1, 1955, and May 15, 1975, whether in-country, in-theater or deployed elsewhere in support of operations in Southeast Asia; andWHEREAS, More than 9 million Americans were on active duty during that period, with an estimated 2.7 million serving in-country or in-waters; andWHEREAS, More than 58,000 servicepersons, including nearly 3,150 Pennsylvanians, died; andWHEREAS, Their names are etched on the black granite panels of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Wall in Washington, DC; andWHEREAS, A reported 2,500 Americans were held as prisoners of war; andWHEREAS, More than 300,000 Americans were wounded; andWHEREAS, The United States Department of Defense has indicated that 1,587 Americans are unaccounted for in Southeast Asia; andWHEREAS, To mark the 50th anniversary of the Vietnam War, the United States of America Vietnam War Commemoration was established pursuant to the 2008 National Defense Authorization Act; andWHEREAS, Public and private commemorative partner agencies and organizations began hosting official events on Memorial Day 2012; andWHEREAS, Commemorative events, including wreath-laying ceremonies and presentation of Department of Defense lapel pins for veterans and surviving spouses, are slated to continue nationwide through Veterans Day 2026; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize March 29, 2025, as "National Vietnam War Veterans Day" in 20250HR0089PN0719 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 Pennsylvania.20250HR0089PN0719 - 3 - 1
