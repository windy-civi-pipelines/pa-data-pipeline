Section 3362(a) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a paragraph to read:ยง 3362. Maximum speed limits.(a) General rule.--Except when a special hazard exists that requires lower speed for compliance with section 3361 (relating to driving vehicle at safe speed), the limits specified in this section or established under this subchapter shall be maximum lawful speeds and no person shall drive a vehicle at a speed in excess of the following maximum limits:* * *(1.3) 35 miles per hour on a dirt and gravel road.* * *Section 2.
