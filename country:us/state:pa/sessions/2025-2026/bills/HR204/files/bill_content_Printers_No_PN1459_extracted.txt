2025-2026 Regular Session HR 204 PN 1459 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1459 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.204 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, APRIL 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, APRIL 22, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating April 8, 2025, as "Dominican Remembrance Day" in Pennsylvania in honor of the victims who perished in the Jet Set Nightclub, located in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.WHEREAS, Jet Set Nightclub was an acclaimed venue known for its vibrant music scene and cultural significance and was located in the capitol of the Dominican Republic, Santo Domingo; andWHEREAS, A tragedy occurred on April 8, 2025, at the Jet Set Nightclub at approximately 12:44 a.m. Atlantic Standard Time, during a live performance by merengue musician Rubby Pérez; andWHEREAS, That night, between 500 and 1,000 people had filled the venue in anticipation of seeing the singer; andWHEREAS, Suddenly and catastrophically, the roof of the famed venue collapsed; andWHEREAS, The collapse resulted in 231 fatalities, more than 200 injuries and 189 individuals being rescued from the rubble, marking it as one of the deadliest non-natural disasters in the Dominican Republic's history; and123456789101112131415161718 WHEREAS, Among the deceased were singer Rubby Pérez, former Major League Baseball players Octavio Dotel and Tony Blanco and Nelsy Cruz, governor of Montecristi Province; andWHEREAS, In the aftermath of the collapse, Nelsy Cruz heroically managed to notify the President of the Dominican Republic of what had occurred; andWHEREAS, This tragedy has touched at least 10 nations, including the United States which lost 17 citizens and had one citizen injured; andWHEREAS, More than 81,000 Dominicans currently reside in this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, The Commonwealth joins in mourning this international tragedy at Jet Set Nightclub which has had a profound impact on the Dominican Republic, highlighting concerns about building safety standards and prompting a nationwide reflection on public safety measures; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate April 8, 2025, as "Dominican Remembrance Day" in Pennsylvania in honor of the victims who perished in the Jet Set Nightclub, located in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the Dominican Consulate in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.20250HR0204PN1459 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
