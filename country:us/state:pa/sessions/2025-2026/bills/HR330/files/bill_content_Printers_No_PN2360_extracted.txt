2025-2026 Regular Session HR 330 PN 2360 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2360 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.330 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MADDEN, BRENNAN, RIVERA, VITALI, SANCHEZ, HILL-EVANS, M. MACKENZIE, FREEMAN, PROBST, HOWARD, NEILSON, MAYES, CONKLIN, MENTZER, FRANKEL AND PARKER, SEPTEMBER 29, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGING AND OLDER ADULT SERVICES, SEPTEMBER 29, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing October 9, 2025, as "Ageism Awareness Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Ageism refers to the stereotypes, prejudice and discrimination toward others based on age; andWHEREAS, More than 90% of older adults say they regularly experience some form of ageism; andWHEREAS, The fastest growing demographic in this Commonwealth is older adults 85 years of age and older; andWHEREAS, Ageism toward older adults affects their health and longevity, financial well-being and the economy of Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, Creating awareness of and actively preventing ageism in education, employment, housing and health care benefits all individuals; andWHEREAS, It is up to all of us to ensure that older adults are respected and portrayed as capable, competent, effective and valued in all areas of society; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Ageism awareness and preventing ageism is beneficial to all residents of this Commonwealth by improving quality of life within Pennsylvania; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize October 9, 2025, as "Ageism Awareness Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0330PN2360 - 2 - 12345
