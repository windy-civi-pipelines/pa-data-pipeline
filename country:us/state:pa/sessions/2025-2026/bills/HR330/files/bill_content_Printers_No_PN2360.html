A RESOLUTION
Recognizing October 9, 2025, as "Ageism Awareness Day" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Ageism refers to the stereotypes, prejudice and
discrimination toward others based on age; and
WHEREAS, More than 90% of older adults say they regularly
experience some form of ageism; and
WHEREAS, The fastest growing demographic in this Commonwealth
is older adults 85 years of age and older; and
WHEREAS, Ageism toward older adults affects their health and
longevity, financial well-being and the economy of Pennsylvania;
and
WHEREAS, Creating awareness of and actively preventing ageism
in education, employment, housing and health care benefits all
individuals; and
WHEREAS, It is up to all of us to ensure that older adults
are respected and portrayed as capable, competent, effective and
valued in all areas of society; and
