A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the service of the Honorable Jerry A. Stern, an 11-
term former member of the House of Representatives, and
expressing condolences upon his death on December 11, 2024,
in Martinsburg, Blair County, at 69 years of age.
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives mourns the loss of
Jerry A. Stern, who served the 80th District with distinction,
representing Bedford and Blair Counties from 1993 through 2002
and Blair County from 2003 through January 6, 2015; and
WHEREAS, With integrity and purpose, Mr. Stern championed
agriculture, tourism promotion, library and school funding and
employment; and
WHEREAS, With those same values, Mr. Stern mentored other
members of the General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, From 2007 through 2010, Mr. Stern earned respect for
his leadership as Minority Caucus Secretary; and
WHEREAS, From 2011 through 2014, Mr. Stern provided
leadership as chair of the Tourism and Recreational Development
Committee; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Stern further served the General Assembly and

congregation and community; and
WHEREAS, The House of Representatives remembers Mr. Stern's

WHEREAS, Mr. Stern's wife Susan E. Stern, his children Jason
Stern and Gretchen Stern Bettwy and his grandchildren survive
