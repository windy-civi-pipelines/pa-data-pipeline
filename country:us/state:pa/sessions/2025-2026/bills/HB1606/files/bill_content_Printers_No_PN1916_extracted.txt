2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1606 PN 1916 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1916 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1606 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SANCHEZ, PICKETT, MADDEN, PROBST, HILL-EVANS, RIVERA, JAMES, WAXMAN, HOHENSTEIN, HOWARD, HADDOCK, McANDREW, BRENNAN, D. WILLIAMS, GUZMAN AND WARREN, JUNE 13, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGING AND OLDER ADULT SERVICES, JUNE 16, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in public assistance, further providing for personal needs allowance deduction for medical assistance-eligible persons in nursing facilities.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 443.8 of the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), known as the Human Services Code, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 443.8. Personal Needs Allowance Deduction for Medical Assistance-Eligible Persons in Nursing Facilities.--* * *(c) Subject to Federal approval, the personal needs allowance under subsection (a) shall be increased to not less than eighty-five dollars ($85) beginning January 1, 2026.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.1234567891011121314151617
