Section 2. Title 15 is amended by adding a section to read:
5332. Exemption from certain fees AND SOLICITATION
REQUIREMENTS FOR VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANIES .
(a) Payment.--A volunteer fire company filing articles of
incorporation under this chapter shall be exempt from the
payment of the fee established under section 153(a)(1)(i)
(relating to fee schedule).
(A.1) SOLICITATION REQUIREMENTS EXEMPTION.--
(1) A VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY FILING ARTICLES OF
INCORPORATION UNDER THIS CHAPTER SHALL BE EXEMPT FROM THE
REQUIREMENTS FOR SOLICITATION ESTABLISHED BY THE ACT OF
DECEMBER 19, 1990 (P.L.1200, NO.202), KNOWN AS THE
SOLICITATION OF FUNDS FOR CHARITABLE PURPOSES ACT.
(2) A SUPPLIER, VENDOR OR PROFESSIONAL FUNDRAISING
COUNSEL CONTRACTED BY A VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY FILING
ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION UNDER THIS CHAPTER SHALL BE EXEMPT
FROM THE REQUIREMENTS FOR SOLICITATION ESTABLISHED UNDER THE
SOLICITATION OF FUNDS FOR CHARITABLE PURPOSES ACT AS THOSE
REQUIREMENTS RELATE TO ANY CONDUCT PERFORMED IN THE RAISING
OF FUNDS FOR USE BY THE VOLUNTEER FIRE COMPANY.
(b) Offenses.--
(1) If a person fraudulently obtains a fee exemption
permitted under this section, the person commits a
misdemeanor of the third degree.
(2) If a person assigns, transfers or attempts to assign
or transfer a fee exemption or uses or attempts to use a fee
exemption contrary to this section, the person commits a
misdemeanor of the third degree.
