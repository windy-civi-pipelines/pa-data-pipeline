Amending Title 15 (Corporations and Unincorporated Associations)
of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in general
provisions relating to corporation bureau and UCC fees,
further providing for fee schedule; and, in incorporation,
providing for exemption from certain fees.

hereby enacts as follows:

(a) Payment.--A volunteer fire company filing articles of
incorporation under this chapter shall be exempt from the
payment of the fee established under section 153(a)(1)(i)
(relating to fee schedule).
(b) Offenses.--
(1) If a person fraudulently obtains a fee exemption
permitted under this section, the person commits a
misdemeanor of the third degree.
(2) If a person assigns, transfers or attempts to assign
or transfer a fee exemption or uses or attempts to use a fee
exemption contrary to this section, the person commits a
misdemeanor of the third degree.
(c) Reporting.--
(1) The department shall annually determine the total
number of fee exemptions applied for and issued and the total
dollar amount of the fee exemptions under this section.
(2) The department shall issue an annual report
containing the information under paragraph (1).
(3) Each report under paragraph (2) shall be
transmitted, electronically or otherwise, to:
(i) The chairperson and minority chairperson of the
Veterans Affairs and Emergency Preparedness Committee of
the Senate.
(ii) The chairperson and minority chairperson of the
Veterans Affairs and Emergency Preparedness Committee of
the House of Representatives.
(d) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following
words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this
subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:
"Volunteer fire company." As defined in 35 Pa.C.S. § 7802
