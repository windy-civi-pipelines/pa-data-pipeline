A RESOLUTION
Urging the Governor to develop a comprehensive legislative plan
to improve basic education in the public schools of this
Commonwealth and to establish an advisory commission to study
and make specific recommendations for components of the plan.
WHEREAS, The public schools of this Commonwealth are valued
assets that have provided a quality education to countless
public school students since their inception; and
WHEREAS, School districts in this Commonwealth have
experienced significant budget shortfalls over previous fiscal
years due to the combination of cuts in State funding, reduced
revenue from local school taxes and the proliferation of funding
for public school alternatives; and
WHEREAS, As a result of the severe budget constraints, school
districts have reluctantly been faced with the necessity of
reducing or eliminating components of their educational
programs, such as kindergarten, libraries and the arts and
furloughing experienced professional educators to balance their
budgets; and
WHEREAS, The structure of State and local funding for school

WHEREAS, The elimination of components of educational

RESOLVED, That the Senate respectfully urge the Governor to

RESOLVED, That the Senate respectfully urge the Governor, in

education, to establish an advisory commission with a sufficient
number of advisory commission members consisting of parents,
teachers, school administrators, business leaders, higher
education leaders and legislators representing a balanced cross
section of the citizens of this Commonwealth to study and
provide specific recommendations for the components of the plan,
including the following issues:
(1) what sources of State and local funding for basic
education are the best sources of revenue for reliability and
equity;
