2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1493 PN 1747 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1747 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1493 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ABNEY, WAXMAN, HILL-EVANS, KENYATTA, SANCHEZ, MADDEN, FREEMAN, DOUGHERTY, GUZMAN, SCHLOSSBERG, HOHENSTEIN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ AND MAYES, MAY 19, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON COMMUNICATIONS AND TECHNOLOGY, MAY 21, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 42 (Judiciary and Judicial Procedure) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in Administrative Office of Pennsylvania Courts, providing for data collection on race and ethnicity in criminal cases.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 42 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 1909. Data collection on race and ethnicity in criminal cases.The Administrative Office of Pennsylvania Courts shall collect and compile data on the race and ethnicity of defendants in criminal cases. The data shall be published in an easily accessible and user-friendly format on the publicly accessible Internet website of the Administrative Office of Pennsylvania Courts. The Administrative Office of Pennsylvania Courts shall submit an annual report of the compiled data to the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency for analysis.123456789101112131415161718 Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1493PN1747 - 2 - 1
