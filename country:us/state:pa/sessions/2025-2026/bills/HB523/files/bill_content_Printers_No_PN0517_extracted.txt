JONES AND STENDER, FEBRUARY 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, FEBRUARY 10, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in obstructing governmental operations, further providing for the offense of resisting arrest or other law enforcement.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 5104 of Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:ยง 5104. Resisting arrest or other law enforcement.A person commits a misdemeanor of the second degree if, with the intent of preventing a public servant from effecting a lawful arrest or discharging any other duty, the person creates a [substantial] risk of bodily injury to the public servant or anyone else, or employs means justifying or requiring [substantial] force to overcome the resistance.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.12345678910111213141516
