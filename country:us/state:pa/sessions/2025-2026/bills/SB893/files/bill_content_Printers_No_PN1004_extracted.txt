2025-2026 Regular Session SB 893 PN 1004 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1004 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE BILL No.893 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KEEFER, JUNE 24, 2025 REFERRED TO LABOR AND INDUSTRY, JUNE 24, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of October 13, 2010 (P.L.506, No.72), entitled "An act providing for the criteria for independent contractors in the construction industry and for the powers and duties of the Department of Labor and Industry and the Secretary of Labor and Industry; and imposing penalties," further providing for independent contractors.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 3 of the act of October 13, 2010 (P.L.506, No.72), known as the Construction Workplace Misclassification Act, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 3. Independent contractors.* * *(a.1) Contractor registration.--Evidence that an individual is registered as a contractor under the act of October 17, 2008 (P.L.1645, No.132), known as the Home Improvement Consumer Protection Act, at the time the individual performs services in the construction industry shall constitute prima facie evidence that the individual is an independent contractor under 1234567891011121314151617181920 subsection (a).* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250SB0893PN1004 - 2 - 123
