A RESOLUTION
Designating the week of November 17 through 21, 2025, as
"Certified Public Accountant Week" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, The Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public
Accountants (PICPA) is the premier association of accounting and
finance professionals in this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, PICPA was organized in 1897 and represents
approximately 20,000 certified public accountants (CPAs) in
public practice, industry, government and education; and
WHEREAS, The mission of PICPA is to serve the needs of its
members by promoting and maintaining high professional and
ethical standards of public accountancy; and
WHEREAS, PICPA is committed to protecting the interests of
the public; and
WHEREAS, CPAs of this Commonwealth are trusted advisors to
hundreds of thousands of taxpayers and businesses and help
foster economic growth in this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, CPAs of this Commonwealth provide career sessions to
