AN ACT
Amending the act of July 9, 1987 (P.L.238, No.43), entitled "An
act providing for certification of persons who perform radon
testing and radon remediation; providing for the
confidentiality of certain data; imposing penalties; and
making an appropriation," further providing for
confidentiality of data.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 9 of the act of July 9, 1987 (P.L.238,
No.43), known as the Radon Certification Act, is amended to
read:
Section 9. Confidentiality of data.
Except for use in conducting legitimate scientific studies,
as determined by the department, data relating to individuals
and data relating to radon gas and radon progeny contamination
at nonpublic properties, including residential dwellings,
gathered under this act shall be considered confidential by the
department. The department shall not release the data in its
possession to anyone other than the owner of the property[.], or
a previous owner of the property if the previous owner owned the
