2025-2026 Regular Session SR 105 PN 0779 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 779 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.105 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CULVER, TARTAGLIONE, PENNYCUICK, FONTANA, MARTIN, SANTARSIERO, HUGHES, BROWN, STEFANO, CAPPELLETTI, COSTA AND BAKER, MAY 12, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, MAY 12, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of May 2025 as "National Treatment Court Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Treatment courts represent a significant advancement in the administration of justice by integrating evidence-based substance use disorder treatment, mental health services, accountability, recovery support services and intensive supervision with judicial oversight; andWHEREAS, These specialized court dockets address the underlying issues contributing to criminal behavior, such as substance use disorders and mental health disorders, rather than focusing on punishment; andWHEREAS, Treatment courts in Pennsylvania, including Adult and Juvenile Drug Courts, DUI Courts, Mental Health Courts, Veterans Treatment Courts and Family Treatment Courts, have demonstrated remarkable success in reducing recidivism, promoting recovery, preventing fatal overdose and improving public safety; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Treatment courts provide a cost-effective alternative to incarceration by facilitating communitywide partnerships, bringing together public safety and public health to increase recovery capital in communities; andWHEREAS, The dedicated professionals working in Pennsylvania's treatment courts, including judges, prosecutors, defense counsel, probation officers, law enforcement, treatment providers, case management, peers, mentors and community volunteers, are to be commended for their commitment to helping individuals reclaim their lives; andWHEREAS, Recognizing "National Treatment Court Month" in Pennsylvania helps to educate the public, policymakers and stakeholders about the vital role these courts play in addressing the opioid crisis, the mental health crisis and other complex social issues; andWHEREAS, Continued support and expansion of treatment courts in Pennsylvania are essential to fostering healthier, safer and more resilient communities across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, All Rise, a national organization with 30 years of experience in advocating to improve the justice system response to substance use and mental health disorders, has designated the month of May as "National Treatment Court Month" to raise awareness of the effectiveness of treatment courts in the justice system; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the month of May 2025 as "National Treatment Court Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate commend the dedicated professionals and volunteers who contribute to the success of treatment courts in Pennsylvania and encourage continued support for these vital 20250SR0105PN0779 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 programs.20250SR0105PN0779 - 3 - 1
