2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1805 PN 2219 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2219 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1805 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, HILL-EVANS, MAYES, GAYDOS, KHAN, SANCHEZ AND PARKER, AUGUST 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON FINANCE, AUGUST 15, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 4, 1971 (P.L.6, No.2), entitled "An act relating to tax reform and State taxation by codifying and enumerating certain subjects of taxation and imposing taxes thereon; providing procedures for the payment, collection, administration and enforcement thereof; providing for tax credits in certain cases; conferring powers and imposing duties upon the Department of Revenue, certain employers, fiduciaries, individuals, persons, corporations and other entities; prescribing crimes, offenses and penalties," in general provisions, providing for deferred payment plan and installment plan penalty, fine and fee freeze.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 4, 1971 (P.L.6, No.2), known as the Tax Reform Code of 1971, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 3003.26. Deferred Payment Plan and Installment Plan Penalty, Fine and Fee Freeze.--(a) A taxpayer subject to any tax under this act who has entered into a duly authorized deferred payment plan or installment plan for the taxpayer's past due tax liability may not be charged any additional penalties, fines or fees for the late payment of the taxpayer's 1234567891011121314151617181920212223 tax liability if the taxpayer is making payments in accordance with the terms of the deferred payment plan or installment plan.(b) Entrance into or participation in a duly authorized deferred payment plan or installment plan for the taxpayer's past due tax liability, including an agreement to reinstate a sales and use tax license, shall not preclude the taxpayer from having the right to appeal the taxpayer's past due tax liability.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1805PN2219 - 2 - 123456789
