2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1766 PN 2175 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2175 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1766 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY DELLOSO, HILL-EVANS, McNEILL, KHAN, PROBST, SANCHEZ AND K.HARRIS, JULY 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LABOR AND INDUSTRY, AUGUST 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 2, 1915 (P.L.736, No.338), entitled "An act defining the liability of an employer to pay damages for injuries received by an employe in the course of employment; establishing an elective schedule of compensation; providing procedure for the determination of liability and compensation thereunder; and prescribing penalties," in liability and compensation, further providing for schedule of compensation.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 306(f.1)(1) of the act of June 2, 1915 (P.L.736, No.338), known as the Workers' Compensation Act, is amended by adding a subparagraph to read:Section 306. The following schedule of compensation is hereby established:* * *(f.1) (1) * * *(ii.1) (A) In addition to the above service, the employer shall provide for reimbursement to a qualifying patient who obtains medical marijuana for certified medical use under the act of April 17, 2016 (P.L.84, No.16), known as the "Medical 1234567891011121314151617181920 Marijuana Act." A qualifying patient shall:(I) be eligible for a dollar-for-dollar reimbursement on medical marijuana, for a maximum monthly reimbursement of two hundred fifty dollars ($250);(II) cover monthly amounts in excess of two hundred fifty dollars ($250) that the qualifying patient spends on medical marijuana or manufactured marijuana products which shall not be eligible for reimbursement under this subparagraph; and(III) be limited to a yearly maximum of three thousand dollars ($3,000) in reimbursements for medical marijuana.(B) An insurer shall establish a system to reimburse qualifying patients under this subparagraph on at least a quarterly basis.(C) A patient shall be eligible for reimbursement of the fee to obtain and renew the identification card required for a patient to be dispensed medical marijuana under the "Medical Marijuana Act."(D) As used in this subparagraph:(I) "Identification card" shall have the same meaning given to it under section 103 of the "Medical Marijuana Act."(II) "Medical marijuana" shall have the same meaning given to it under section 103 of the "Medical Marijuana Act."(III) "Patient" shall have the same meaning given to it under section 103 of the "Medical Marijuana Act."* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1766PN2175 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526
