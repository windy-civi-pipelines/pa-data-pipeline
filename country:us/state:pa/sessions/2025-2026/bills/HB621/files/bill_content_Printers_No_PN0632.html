AN ACT
Amending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in forgery and fraudulent practices,
providing for the offense of benefit transfer device fraud.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
§ 4122. Benefit transfer device fraud.
(a) Offense defined.--A person commits an offense if the
person is an unauthorized alien and possesses, uses or attempts
to use a benefit transfer device.
(b) Separate offenses.--Each time a person possesses or uses
a benefit transfer device in violation of subsection (a)
constitutes a separate offense under this section.
(c) Grading.--A person who violates subsection (a) commits a
felony of the third degree.
(d) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following
words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this

"Benefit transfer device." The Pennsylvania ACCESS card or
