Automated External Defibrillator Placement, Inspection, Maintenance and Plan.--(a) Beginning in the 2025-2026 school year and each school y ear thereafter, each school building or facility where children in kindergarten through grade twelve attend school or participate in school-sponsored activities shall have at least one automated external 12345678910111213141516171819202122 defibrillator system placed in a central location that is accessible for use. A school entity shall place an automated external defibrillator:(1) with appropriate signage in accordance with best practices and American Heart Association guidelines on the effective date of this section, including three dimensional signs and arrows, in plain view, leading to and beside the automated external defibrillator;(2) with instructions for the proper use of the automated external defibrillator at the location of the automated external defibrillator;(3) in an accessible location during normal school hours and any school-sponsored activities;(4) in compliance with 42 U.S.C. Ch. 126 (relating to equal opportunity for individuals with disabilities), including placing the automated external defibrillator not more than forty-eight (48) inches above the floor or ground; and(5) in a location that is no more than one thousand (1,000) feet in distance from a designated member of the cardiac emergency response team under subsection (d) in the school entity's protocol for access to the automated external defibrillator.(b) For an automated external defibrillator installed before the effective date of this section, a school entity shall register the automated external defibrillator within ten days of the effective date of this section.
