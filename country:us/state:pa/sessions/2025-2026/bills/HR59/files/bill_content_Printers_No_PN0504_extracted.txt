2025-2026 Regular Session HR 59 PN 0504 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 504 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.59 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KAZEEM, VITALI, SANCHEZ, RIVERA, GIRAL, HILL-EVANS, KHAN, DALEY, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ AND GREEN, FEBRUARY 5, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, FEBRUARY 5, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating November 1, 2025, as "State Correctional Institution Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, In prisons across the nation, many incarcerated individuals are held in violent, dangerous and unhealthy conditions; andWHEREAS, Legislators determine the laws that govern incarcerated individuals and the criminal justice system as they vote on legislation regarding the criminal statutes, parole and sentencing; andWHEREAS, However, legislators are not required to visit prisons, preventing them from having a comprehensive understanding of the criminal justice system; andWHEREAS, It is essential that legislators examine the conditions under which incarcerated individuals live and understand how State funding is appropriated to prisons; andWHEREAS, Those in support of this challenge assert that visiting prisons will provide legislators with a better 1234567891011121314151617 understanding of incarceration while raising accountability regarding the treatment of incarcerated individuals in prison; andWHEREAS, It is vital that legislators listen and speak with incarcerated individuals as they are responsible for representing the needs of the incarcerated population; andWHEREAS, Members of the House of Representatives are encouraged to visit a State correctional institution unannounced at a minimum of two times per year; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate November 1, 2025, as "State Correctional Institution Day" in Pennsylvania to raise awareness of the need to monitor and continue to improve conditions in our prisons to ensure that all people are treated humanely and supported in their rehabilitation.20250HR0059PN0504 - 2 - 123456789101112131415
