NELSON, JUNE 4, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, JUNE 4, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of June 2025 as "Life Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The right to life is one of the founding principles of our nation; andWHEREAS, The Constitution of Pennsylvania does not include a right to abortion; andWHEREAS, The Federal Government, the Commonwealth and local government entities have the duty to protect the life of all individuals; andWHEREAS, The development of scientific and medical technology has demonstrated repeatedly the humanity of the unborn child; andWHEREAS, More than 63 million lives have been lost over the past 50 years because of the unethical decision of Roe v. Wade; andWHEREAS, On June 24, 2022, the United States Supreme Court issued its decision in the Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health 1234567891011121314151617 Organization case, which overturned Roe v. Wade; andWHEREAS, A new birth of freedom began again for the unborn; andWHEREAS, Numerous states have passed laws protecting the unborn since Roe v. Wade was overturned, and preliminary data suggests approximately 32,000 lives were saved in the first six months of 2023 as a result; andWHEREAS, Our children and our children's children will not live in a time in America when we killed the most innocent among us; andWHEREAS, The General Assembly must continue to protect and affirm the lives of all Pennsylvanians; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of June 2025 as "Life Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives call on all churches, businesses and communities to celebrate the three-year anniversary of the historic overturning of Roe v.
