A RESOLUTION
Designating the month of June 2025 as "Life Month" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, The right to life is one of the founding principles
of our nation; and
WHEREAS, The Constitution of Pennsylvania does not include a
right to abortion; and
WHEREAS, The Federal Government, the Commonwealth and local
government entities have the duty to protect the life of all
individuals; and
WHEREAS, The development of scientific and medical technology
has demonstrated repeatedly the humanity of the unborn child;
and
WHEREAS, More than 63 million lives have been lost over the
past 50 years because of the unethical decision of Roe v. Wade;
and
WHEREAS, On June 24, 2022, the United States Supreme Court
issued its decision in the Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health

WHEREAS, A new birth of freedom began again for the unborn;

WHEREAS, Numerous states have passed laws protecting the

WHEREAS, Our children and our children's children will not
live in a time in America when we killed the most innocent among
us; and
WHEREAS, The General Assembly must continue to protect and
affirm the lives of all Pennsylvanians; therefore be it
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the
month of June 2025 as "Life Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it
further
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives call on all
churches, businesses and communities to celebrate the three-year
anniversary of the historic overturning of Roe v. Wade on June
24, 2025.
