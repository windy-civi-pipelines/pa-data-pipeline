2025-2026 Regular Session HB 35 PN 0014 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 14 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.35 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CONKLIN, HANBIDGE, GIRAL, HILL-EVANS, CIRESI, HOHENSTEIN AND HARKINS, JANUARY 10, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, JANUARY 10, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in school safety and security, providing for school safety blueprint requirement.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 1302.2-B. School Safety Blueprint Requirement.(a) Requirement.--Each school entity shall provide the Pennsylvania State Police or municipal police department that has jurisdiction over the school entity with current blueprints for each building of the school entity and provide updated blueprints as changes are made to the building layout.(b) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this 12345678910111213141516171819 subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"School entity." A public school district, joint school district, charter school, cyber charter school, private school, nonpublic school, intermediate unit or area career and technical school operating within this Commonwealth.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0035PN0014 - 2 - 123456
