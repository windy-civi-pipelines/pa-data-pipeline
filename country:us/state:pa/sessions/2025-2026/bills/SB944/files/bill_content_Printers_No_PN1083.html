AN ACT
Amending Title 67 (Public Welfare) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, providing for nondiscrimination in
adoption and foster care family agencies; and promulgating
regulations.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 67 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a chapter to read:
CHAPTER 78
NONDISCRIMINATION IN ADOPTION AND
FOSTER CARE FAMILY AGENCIES
Sec.
7801. Definitions.
7802. Nondiscrimination.
7803. No establishment of religion.
7804. Regulations.
§ 7801. Definitions.
The following words and phrases when used in this chapter

"Gender identity or expression." Having or being perceived

with the individual's sex assigned at birth.
"Kinship caregiver." An individual who meets the
requirements under the laws of this Commonwealth to provide
kinship care for a child.
§ 7802. Nondiscrimination.
A county agency, or public or private agency that is
authorized or approved by the county agency or department to
provide foster family care, kinship care or adoption services,
may not:
(1) Deny an individual the opportunity to become an
adoptive or a foster parent or kinship caregiver solely on
the basis of a real or perceived disability, race, creed,
religion, color, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity or
expression, marital status, national origin, ancestry or any
communicable disease, including HIV, of the individual or a
member of the individual's household.
(2) Deny to care for a specific child on the basis of a
real or perceived disability, race, creed, religion, color,
sex, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression,
national origin, ancestry or any communicable disease,
including HIV, of the child unless the denial of care is
documented, has a clear nexus to the ability to meet the
needs of the child and is not detrimental to the health or
welfare of the child.
(3) Delay or deny the placement of a child for adoption

§ 7803. No establishment of religion.
No county agency, or public or private agency that is
authorized or approved by the county agency or department to
provide foster family care, kinship care or adoption services,
may deny any otherwise eligible individual the opportunity to
become an adoptive or a foster parent or kinship caregiver, due
solely to a preference for any religious establishment or mode
of worship in violation of section 3 of Article I of the
Constitution of Pennsylvania.
§ 7804. Regulations.
The department shall promulgate regulations as necessary to
effectuate this chapter.
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
