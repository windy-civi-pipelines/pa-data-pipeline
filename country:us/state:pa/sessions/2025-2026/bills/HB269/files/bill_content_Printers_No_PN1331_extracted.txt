Section 13.8(a)(3) and (h) of the act of April 14, 1972 (P.L.233, No.64), known as The Controlled Substance, Drug, Device and Cosmetic Act, are amended and the section is amended by adding subsections to read:Section 13.8. Drug Overdose Medication.--(a) The department, in carrying out its duties under 28 Pa.
