REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LABOR AND INDUSTRY, SEPTEMBER 25, 2025
A RESOLUTION
Designating October 3, 2025, as "Manufacturing Day" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Manufacturing is a cornerstone of this
Commonwealth's economy, generating more than $112 billion each
year; and
WHEREAS, Manufacturing is a major driver of this
Commonwealth's economy, accounting for approximately 13% of
gross State product; and
WHEREAS, Manufacturers employ more than 562,000 hardworking
Pennsylvanians in facilities across this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, Additional Pennsylvania jobs are directly sustained
through supply chains, distribution networks, warehousing and
logistics tied to manufacturing; and
WHEREAS, Manufacturing careers provide above-average wages
and benefits; and
WHEREAS, Manufacturing is more than making products and plays
a vital role in shaping the future by relying on creators and
