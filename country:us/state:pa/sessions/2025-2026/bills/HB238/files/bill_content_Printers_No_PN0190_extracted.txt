2025-2026 Regular Session HB 238 PN 0190 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 190 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.238 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BENNINGHOFF, ARMANINI, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, FREEMAN, HANBIDGE, KUZMA, NEILSON, OTTEN, PICKETT, SCHEUREN, SMITH, STENDER, WARREN, WATRO, KRUPA AND HADDOCK, JANUARY 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, JANUARY 22, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in licensing of drivers, providing for application for REAL ID.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 1506.1. Application for REAL ID.(a) Verification of Social Security number.--The department shall accept a Social Security card, W-2 form, SSA 1099 form, a non-SSA 1099 form or a pay stub that fulfills the requirements of the REAL ID Act of 2005 (Public Law 109-113, 119 Stat. 302) for the purpose of reviewing an application for a REAL ID and verifying the applicant's Social Security number.(b) Definitions.--As used in this section, the term "REAL ID" means a photo identification card or driver's license issued by the department under this chapter or Chapter 16 (relating to commercial drivers) that complies with the REAL ID Act of 2005.123456789101112131415161718 Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB0238PN0190 - 2 - 1
