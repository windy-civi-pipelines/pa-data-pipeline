2025-2026 Regular Session HR 190 PN 1375 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1375 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.190 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CEPHAS, BURGOS, HILL-EVANS, FREEMAN, VENKAT, MAYES, PIELLI, HANBIDGE, HOHENSTEIN, SCHLOSSBERG, CERRATO, SANCHEZ, RIVERA, D. WILLIAMS, K.HARRIS AND O'MARA, APRIL 15, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, APRIL 15, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the week of April 7 through 11, 2025, as "Health Equity Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Health Equity Leader Exchange Network suggests that health equity exists when all people, regardless of age, race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, socioeconomic status, geographic location or other societal constructs, have fair and just access, opportunity and resources to achieve their highest potential for health; andWHEREAS, The social determinants of health are factors in the social environment that contribute to or detract from the health of individuals and communities; andWHEREAS, The political determinants of health involve the systematic process of structuring relationships, distributing resources and administering power, operating simultaneously in ways that mutually reinforce or influence one another to shape opportunities that either advance health equity or exacerbate health inequities; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Both the social and political determinants of health have greatly impacted the lives of all people especially within marginalized communities; andWHEREAS, Many public, private, nonprofit and governmental organizations have worked honorably to identify and support communities and populations impacted by health inequities; andWHEREAS, These efforts have led to significant progress but have also made clear that more education, partnerships and systematic change is needed; andWHEREAS, Recognizing "Health Equity Week" in Pennsylvania sends a strong message to all residents of this Commonwealth that our State recognizes the manifold health inequities faced by residents and supports effective methods of addressing those health inequities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the week of April 7 through 11, 2025, as "Health Equity Week" in Pennsylvania to raise awareness of health inequities and to call on all residents to join in the work of making health equity realized throughout all sectors and communities in this Commonwealth.20250HR0190PN1375 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920
