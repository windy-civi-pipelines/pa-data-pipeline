WILLIAMS, BELLMON AND RIVERA, MARCH 11, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON STATE GOVERNMENT, MARCH 11, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of April 2025 as "National Poetry Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Academy of American Poets established its observance of "National Poetry Month" in 1996; andWHEREAS, As led by the Academy of American Poets, "National Poetry Month" has become the largest literary celebration in the world; andWHEREAS, "National Poetry Month" highlights the extraordinary legacy and ongoing achievement of American poets; andWHEREAS, "National Poetry Month" introduces Americans to the pleasures and benefits of reading poetry; andWHEREAS, "National Poetry Month" reinforces the importance of poetry in children's learning and assists teachers in bringing poetry into their classrooms; andWHEREAS, Poetry has generated some of our nation's leading creative artists; andWHEREAS, Poetry has inspired other artists in fields spanning 1234567891011121314151617 music, theater, film, dance and the visual arts; andWHEREAS, In 2024, an event known as "Poetry Out Loud" included more than 3,000 high school students who participated in the competition in 73 schools across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Numerous communities throughout this Commonwealth celebrate their local poets by appointing them as poet laureate of their community; andWHEREAS, Language arts, including poetry, integrate the creative arts and humanities and reflect the present and historic educational, economic and social fabric of our communities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of April 2025 as "National Poetry Month" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0110PN0914 - 2 - 12345678910111213
