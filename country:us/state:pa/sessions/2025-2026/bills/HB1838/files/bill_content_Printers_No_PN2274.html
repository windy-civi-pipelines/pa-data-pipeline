AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in powers of department and local authorities,
further providing for authority to erect traffic-control
devices.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 6122 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:
§ 6122. Authority to erect traffic-control devices.
* * *
(e) Payment of costs to install and maintain traffic-control
signals.--The department shall pay for the costs associated with
the installation and maintenance of traffic-control signals on
State-designated highways. If a State-designated highway
intersects with a highway under the jurisdiction of a local
authority, the department shall pay for the cost of installing
and maintaining a traffic-control signal at the intersection in
proportion to the number of State-designated highways at the
intersection and the local authority shall pay for the balance
