AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for
vehicles exempt from registration; and, in licensing of
drivers, further providing for persons exempt from licensing.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Sections 1302 and 1502 of Title 75 of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes are amended by adding
paragraphs to read:
1302. Vehicles exempt from registration.
The following types of vehicles are exempt from registration:
