2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1008 PN 1095 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1095 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1008 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BURNS, PROBST, GIRAL, HANBIDGE, SANCHEZ, KENYATTA, MERSKI, VENKAT, RABB, SCHLOSSBERG, HADDOCK, CURRY, CERRATO, PARKER, DONAHUE AND CIRESI, MARCH 24, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, MARCH 24, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for renewal of registration.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 1309 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:ยง 1309. Renewal of registration.At least 60 days prior to the expiration of each registration, the department shall send to the registrant an application for renewal of registration[.], along with a text message or email, if the department has the necessary information to enable that communication, notifying the registrant of the expiration date of the registration. The telephone number or email address shall be voluntarily provided to the department, and the department may not provide this information to third parties. The text message or email shall be sent once each week until the registration has been renewed. 123456789101112131415161718 Upon return of the application, accompanied by self-certification of financial responsibility and the applicable fee or fees, the department shall send to the registrant a renewed registration card. An application for renewal of registration for a motor carrier vehicle shall also be accompanied by self-certification of current safety inspection. Failure to receive a renewal application shall not relieve a registrant from the responsibility to renew the registration.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1008PN1095 - 2 - 123456789
