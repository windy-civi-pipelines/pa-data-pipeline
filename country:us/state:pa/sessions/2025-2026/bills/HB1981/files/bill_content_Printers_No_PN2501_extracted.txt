MACKENZIE AND SMITH, OCTOBER 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, OCTOBER 23, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for vehicles exempt from registration; and, in fees, further providing for farm vehicles.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 1302(10) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subparagraph to read:ยง 1302. Vehicles exempt from registration.The following types of vehicles are exempt from registration:* * *(10) Any farm vehicle used exclusively upon a farm or farms owned or operated by the owner of the vehicle, subject to the following:* * *(v) A law enforcement officer may not stop a farm vehicle properly displaying an exemption plate issued under section 1924(c) (relating to farm vehicles) solely 12345678910111213141516171819 for the purpose of requiring the operator to provide proof of a biennial certificate of exemption for the farm vehicle.* * *Section 2. Section 1924 of Title 75 is amended by adding a subsection to read:ยง 1924. Farm vehicles.* * *(c) Exemption plate.--The department shall establish an exemption plate for a farm vehicle described under section 1302(10) (relating to vehicles exempt from registration) and shall issue the plate to a person who has been issued the certificate of exemption and paid the fee for the plate. The cost of the plate may not exceed $20. A person who has obtained the plate for a farm vehicle may display the plate on the rear of the farm vehicle.Section 3. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1981PN2501 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617
