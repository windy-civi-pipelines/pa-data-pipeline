The following amendment to the Constitution of Pennsylvania is proposed in accordance with Article XI:That Article I be amended by adding a section to read:ยง 30. Will of the people.To ensure that the sovereign will of the people is always the highest law of the Commonwealth, the legislative, executive and judicial branches must execute their responsibilities within the clear meanings of the words and phrases in this Constitution and the Constitution of the United States and any amendments thereto at the time of their writing.Section 2. The following procedure applies to the proposed constitutional amendment in this joint resolution:(1) Upon the first passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall 123456789101112131415161718 proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.(2) Upon the second passage by the General Assembly of the amendment, the Secretary of the Commonwealth shall proceed immediately to comply with the advertising requirements of section 1 of Article XI of the Constitution of Pennsylvania.
