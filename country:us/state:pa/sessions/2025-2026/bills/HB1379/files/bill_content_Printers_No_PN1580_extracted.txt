2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1379 PN 1580 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1580 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1379 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY STENDER, ARMANINI, GILLEN, HAMM, E. NELSON, RADER AND WATRO, MAY 2, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, MAY 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), entitled "An act creating the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources consisting of certain functions of the Department of Environmental Resources and the Department of Community Affairs; renaming the Department of Environmental Resources as the Department of Environmental Protection; defining the role of the Environmental Quality Board in the Department of Environmental Protection; making changes to responsibilities of the State Conservation Commission and the Department of Agriculture; transferring certain powers and duties to the Department of Health; and repealing inconsistent acts," in Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, further providing for forests.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 302(a) and (d) of the act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), known as the Conservation and Natural Resources Act, are amended by adding paragraphs to read:Section 302. Forests.(a) Acquisition, establishment and disposition.--The department has the following powers and duties with respect to the acquisition, establishment and disposition of State forest lands and certain other Commonwealth-owned resources:1234567891011121314151617181920212223 * * *(14) To reimburse activities approved and submitted under subsection (d)(13) and (14).* * *(d) Chief Forest Fire Warden.--The Chief Forest Fire Warden, subject to the approval of the secretary, shall have the following powers and duties to:* * *(13) Approve activities to be carried out by local forest fire wardens, including:(i) Controlled or prescribed burns.(ii) Public relations events.(iii) Fire investigations.(iv) Training required by the department for local fire wardens.(14) Establish a form and manner for local forest fire wardens to request reimbursement from the department for activities approved under paragraph (13).* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1379PN1580 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920
