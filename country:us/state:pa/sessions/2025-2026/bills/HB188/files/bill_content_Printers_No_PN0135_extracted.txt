2025-2026 Regular Session HB 188 PN 0135 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 135 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.188 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BOROWICZ, HAMM, BANTA, TWARDZIK, BARGER, SMITH AND ZIMMERMAN, JANUARY 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, JANUARY 16, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in size, weight and load, further providing for limits on number of towed vehicles, for length of vehicles and for application to tow trucks.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 4904(c)(2) and (h) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes are amended to read:§ 4904. Limits on number of towed vehicles.* * *(c) Towing vehicles requiring service.--* * *(2) A combination, including a truck tractor with no more than two trailers, requiring emergency service may be towed to a [nearby garage or other place of safety.] place of repairs of the owner's choosing or other place of safety off of a highway as provided under sections 4923 (relating to length of vehicles) and 4949 (relating to application to tow trucks).12345678910111213141516171819 * * *(h) Certain combinations permitted under section 4968.--Combinations consisting of a truck and one trailer or a truck tractor and one trailer which exceeds the maximum vehicle lengths authorized in section 4923 [(relating to length of vehicles)] and which shall not exceed 102 inches in width, or a truck tractor and no more than two trailers, each trailer of which shall not exceed 102 inches in width and 28 1/2 feet in length may be operated under a permit issued under section 4968 (relating to permit for movement during course of manufacture).* * *Section 2. Section 4923(b.1) of Title 75 is amended by adding a paragraph to read:§ 4923. Length of vehicles.* * *(b.1) Combinations.--* * *(10) This subsection shall not apply to a tow truck towing a truck tractor with no more than two trailers requiring emergency service as provided in section 4904(c)(2) (relating to limits on number of towed vehicles).* * *Section 3. Section 4949 of Title 75 is amended to read:§ 4949. Application to tow trucks.The weight restrictions set forth in this subchapter do not apply to a combination consisting of any tow truck towing a disabled motor vehicle or truck tractor with no more than two trailers to a place of repairs or other place of safety as long as the overweight combination travels directly to the [first available such location and the movement is performed at the 20250HB0188PN0135 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 direction of authorized emergency personnel or a qualified Commonwealth employee.] place of repairs or place of safety.Section 4. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0188PN0135 - 3 - 123
