The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 40 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a chapter to read:
CHAPTER 52
NOTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR
OLDER ADULT HEALTH INSURANCE POLICIES

5202. Power of attorney notification.

context clearly indicates otherwise:
"Agent." A person designated by a principal, who is an older

"Health insurance policy." As defined in section 4001

"Older adult." An individual who is 60 years of age or older
and who is a resident of this Commonwealth upon enrollment in a
health insurance policy.
§ 5202. Power of attorney notification.
(a) Duties of insurer.--An insurer shall:
(1) If an older adult is enrolling in a health insurance
policy, ask the older adult if the older adult has a power of
attorney which provides for health care decision making and
request that the older adult provide the name and contact
information of the agent under the power of attorney.
(2) Inform the older adult that the purpose of
collecting the information under paragraph (1) is to provide
the agent with notification of the older adult enrolling in
or canceling a health insurance policy.
(3) If the insurer has an agent on file for an older
adult, notify the agent in writing if the older adult enrolls
in or cancels a health insurance policy and send a copy of
the notification to the older adult. The notification shall
inform the older adult of the rescission period under
subsection (b) and the effect of invoking the right of
rescission.
(b) Rescission period.-- If an agent is notified as provided
under subsection (a), the older adult has the right, within 30
days, to rescind enrollment in or cancellation of the health
insurance policy without penalty.

revokes a power of attorney and notifies the insurer of the

Section 2. The addition of 40 Pa.C.S. Ch. 52 shall apply to

older adults that occur after the effective date of this
