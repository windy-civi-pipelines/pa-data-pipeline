A RESOLUTION
Honoring the memory of the Honorable Isabella V. "Izzy"
Fitzgerald, a former member of the House of Representatives,
who died December 6, 2024.
WHEREAS, Ms. Fitzgerald faithfully represented the 203rd
District, Northwest Philadelphia, Philadelphia County, as a
member of the General Assembly from 2017 through 2023; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Fitzgerald notably contributed to the
Legislative Black Caucus, the Philadelphia Delegation and
PennSERVE; and
WHEREAS, In addition to her terms as a lawmaker, Ms.
Fitzgerald's legacy of service to the House of Representatives
includes her work as a legislative assistant to her predecessor,
Representative Dwight Evans; and
WHEREAS, From 2000 through 2002, Ms. Fitzgerald was a special
assistant to United States Representative Robert A. Brady; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Fitzgerald has earned further recognition as a
community organizer and ward leader; and
WHEREAS, A Philadelphia native, born December 9, 1949, Ms.

significant impact on the community and serve as a lasting

WHEREAS, On December 18, 2024, after a funeral service at her
church, Ms. Fitzgerald was interred at Philadelphia's Ivy Hill
