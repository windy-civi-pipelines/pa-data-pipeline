A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the month of May 2025 as "National Water Safety
Month" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, The residents of this Commonwealth understand the
vital role that swimming and aquatic-related activities play in
good physical and mental health and enhance the quality of life
for all people; and
WHEREAS, Education regarding the topic of water safety is
essential to preventing drownings and recreational water-related
injuries; and
WHEREAS, In boating accidents in this Commonwealth from 2022
to 2024, of 28 fatalities involving boats, only eight
individuals were wearing life jackets; and
WHEREAS, In 2021, 23 year old James DeAngelo, a graduate from
Peters Township High School, drowned at a marina in Washington
County, suspected to be caused by an electrical shock from below
the water; and
WHEREAS, Members of the recreational water industry,
