2025-2026 Regular Session HR 178 PN 1264 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1264 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.178 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MIHALEK, REICHARD, ZIMMERMAN, VENKAT, PICKETT, ROWE, GILLEN, WARREN, M. MACKENZIE AND ARMANINI, APRIL 4, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, APRIL 4, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of May 2025 as "National Water Safety Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The residents of this Commonwealth understand the vital role that swimming and aquatic-related activities play in good physical and mental health and enhance the quality of life for all people; andWHEREAS, Education regarding the topic of water safety is essential to preventing drownings and recreational water-related injuries; andWHEREAS, In boating accidents in this Commonwealth from 2022 to 2024, of 28 fatalities involving boats, only eight individuals were wearing life jackets; andWHEREAS, In 2021, 23 year old James DeAngelo, a graduate from Peters Township High School, drowned at a marina in Washington County, suspected to be caused by an electrical shock from below the water; andWHEREAS, Members of the recreational water industry, 1234567891011121314151617 including the Pool and Hot Tub Alliance, American Red Cross, National Drowning Prevention Alliance, World Waterpark Association and the National Recreation and Parks Association, have combined resources in an effort to educate Americans to be "water aware" during "National Water Safety Month" in May; andWHEREAS, These organizations are using this opportunity to promote water safety by providing education on preventing recreational water-related injuries and deaths to families and individuals of all ages, whether owners of private pools, users of public swimming facilities or visitors to water parks; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of May 2025 as "National Water Safety Month" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0178PN1264 - 2 - 1234567891011121314
