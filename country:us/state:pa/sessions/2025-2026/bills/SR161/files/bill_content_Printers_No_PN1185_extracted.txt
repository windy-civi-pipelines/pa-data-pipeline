WARD, OCTOBER 1, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, OCTOBER 1, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of October 2025 as "Pork Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The pork industry is an important sector of Pennsylvania's agricultural industry; andWHEREAS, Pork producers are established contributors to our communities; andWHEREAS, Pork production is an integral part of Pennsylvania's agriculture and overall economy, generating $1.14 billion of economic activity annually; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania ranks 13th in the nation in terms of pounds of pork produced; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania ranks 12th nationally in total inventory; andWHEREAS, The pork industry supports more than 14,051 jobs across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, There are 2,777 swine farms in Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, Pork producers, their families and other pork industry employees should be recognized and celebrated; 123456789101112131415161718 therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate designate the month of October 2025 as "Pork Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate bring awareness to the current hardships facing the pork industry in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the immense value that Pennsylvania's pork industry injects into our State and local economies; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate applaud the hard work and resiliency of our pork producers and their families; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate promote the purchase and consumption of the high-quality pork products produced here at home in Pennsylvania.20250SR0161PN1185 - 2 - 123456789101112131415
