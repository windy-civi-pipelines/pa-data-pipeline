A RESOLUTION
Recognizing November 11, 2025, as "Veterans Day" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Since the founding of our Commonwealth to modern day
operations and missions, Pennsylvania veterans have served our
great nation; and
WHEREAS, This Commonwealth is home to one of the highest
veteran populations in the United States, with more than 800,000
men and women who have served our country; and
WHEREAS, Veterans make up 6.2% of the population in
Pennsylvania; and
WHEREAS, Veterans thrive as members of their communities,
supporting and inspiring the next generation to lead; and
WHEREAS, Veterans have a higher workforce participation than
nonveterans; and
WHEREAS, Individuals in public service have a duty to serve
those who have served our country; and
