MACKENZIE, DELLOSO, HANBIDGE, HAMM, PUGH, JAMES, STENDER, CERRATO, KAUFFMAN, CIRESI, GLEIM, DONAHUE, CONKLIN, ROWE, PICKETT, NEILSON AND O'MARA, JANUARY 23, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, JANUARY 23, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing November 11, 2025, as "Veterans Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Since the founding of our Commonwealth to modern day operations and missions, Pennsylvania veterans have served our great nation; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth is home to one of the highest veteran populations in the United States, with more than 800,000 men and women who have served our country; andWHEREAS, Veterans make up 6.2% of the population in Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, Veterans thrive as members of their communities, supporting and inspiring the next generation to lead; andWHEREAS, Veterans have a higher workforce participation than nonveterans; andWHEREAS, Individuals in public service have a duty to serve those who have served our country; and12345678910111213141516 WHEREAS, "Veterans Day" reminds us of our shared responsibility to recognize the service and sacrifice of veterans and extend our continued support; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize November 11, 2025, as "Veterans Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0015PN0247 - 2 - 12345
