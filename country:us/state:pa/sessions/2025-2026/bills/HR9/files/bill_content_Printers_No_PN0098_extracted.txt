WILLIAMS, NEILSON AND MENTZER, JANUARY 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, JANUARY 14, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of January 2025 as "School Director Recognition Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Commonwealth of Pennsylvania recognizes the public service of the 4,500 locally elected school directors who carry out voluntary public service to advance student achievement and well-being in the school districts and communities they serve; andWHEREAS, School directors are responsible for providing leadership that ensures a clear, shared vision of public education for their schools, sets high standards for the education of all students and requires the effective and efficient operation of their school districts; andWHEREAS, The work of school directors is complex and diverse as they establish local policies, approve educational programs, provide necessary physical facilities, hire qualified employees and share along with the Commonwealth the burden of being fiscal stewards of taxpayer dollars to operate district educational 1234567891011121314151617 programs with transparency and accountability; andWHEREAS, School directors are steadfast advocates for public education and are responsible for communicating the needs of the school district and students with Federal, State and local leaders; andWHEREAS, School board service is an honor which carries significant responsibility to students, parents, district staff, fellow board members and the larger community; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of January 2025 as "School Director Recognition Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the Pennsylvania School Boards Association.20250HR0009PN0098 - 2 - 12345678910111213
