A RESOLUTION
Designating the month of January 2025 as "School Director
Recognition Month" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, The Commonwealth of Pennsylvania recognizes the
public service of the 4,500 locally elected school directors who
carry out voluntary public service to advance student
achievement and well-being in the school districts and
communities they serve; and
WHEREAS, School directors are responsible for providing
leadership that ensures a clear, shared vision of public
education for their schools, sets high standards for the
education of all students and requires the effective and
efficient operation of their school districts; and
WHEREAS, The work of school directors is complex and diverse
as they establish local policies, approve educational programs,
provide necessary physical facilities, hire qualified employees
and share along with the Commonwealth the burden of being fiscal
stewards of taxpayer dollars to operate district educational

WHEREAS, School directors are steadfast advocates for public

significant responsibility to students, parents, district staff,

month of January 2025 as "School Director Recognition Month" in

RESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to
