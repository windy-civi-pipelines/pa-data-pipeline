A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the week of May 11 through 17, 2025, as "National
Hospital Week" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, The hardworking professionals of this Commonwealth's
hospitals and health systems ensure that every resident of this
Commonwealth can receive high-quality health care 24 hours a
day, seven days a week, 365 days a year; and
WHEREAS, Throughout the many waves and ever-changing
circumstances of the pandemic, opioid emergency and behavioral
health crisis, and despite the physical and emotional toll on
themselves and their families, this Commonwealth's hospital and
health system professionals never wavered in caring for our
communities; and
WHEREAS, This Commonwealth's hospitals provided nearly $10
billion in uncompensated care to members of our communities in
fiscal year 2023; and
WHEREAS, This Commonwealth's hospitals work to keep our
families whole and strong via, among other services, 56 trauma

WHEREAS, This Commonwealth's hospitals provided 1.5 million

WHEREAS, This Commonwealth takes pride in its 123 teaching

WHEREAS, Hospitals are the largest employer in 22 counties in
this Commonwealth and among the top 10 employers in 35
additional counties in this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, Hospitals and health systems are responsible for one
in nine jobs across this Commonwealth, including more than
273,000 of our families, friends and neighbors who are directly
employed, and more than 353,000 individuals who are supported by
the hospital-generated industry; and
WHEREAS, Hospitals and their affiliated medical schools bring
nearly $1.9 billion in Federal health care research grants into
this Commonwealth and generate approximately $186.5 billion in
other economic activity for our State and local economies,
accounting for approximately 19% of this Commonwealth's gross
domestic product; and
WHEREAS, "National Hospital Week" celebrates hospitals and
the individuals who support the health of their communities with
dedication and compassion; and
WHEREAS, The observance of "National Hospital Week" is a
