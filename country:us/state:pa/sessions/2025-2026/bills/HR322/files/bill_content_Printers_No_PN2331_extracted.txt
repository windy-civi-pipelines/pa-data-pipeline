2025-2026 Regular Session HR 322 PN 2331 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2331 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.322 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY WAXMAN, VENKAT, HILL-EVANS, GIRAL, RABB, BRENNAN, SANCHEZ, HOWARD, FREEMAN, MAYES, HADDOCK AND GALLAGHER, SEPTEMBER 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON COMMUNICATIONS AND TECHNOLOGY, SEPTEMBER 23, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the week of October 5 through 11, 2025, as "National Newspaper Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Our nation's founders recognized, in their vision, that a democracy depends upon the convictions and actions of an informed public, and that its citizens must have access to the most complete and accurate information about their community, their nation and the world; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth is the birthplace of our nation's independence, and the freedom enjoyed by the people of the United States, supported by the right to free speech and a free press, is a treasured principle upon which this Commonwealth and the United States thrive; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth was a pioneer in the nation's newspaper industry, highlighted by the Pennsylvania Gazette, a fledgling publication reestablished in 1729 by Founding Father and publisher Benjamin Franklin that became the most successful newspaper in the American colonies and shaped the political and 1234567891011121314151617 cultural landscape; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth's newspapers have evolved through the centuries to meet the increasing needs and challenges of a diverse population to reflect the highest standards of journalism and public service, accuracy and fairness; andWHEREAS, Since 1940, the Newspaper Association Managers Inc., has sponsored and supported "National Newspaper Week," a weeklong celebration emphasizing the importance of newspapers in fostering improved community service, participation and responsiveness in the everyday lives of citizens; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the week of October 5 through 11, 2025, as "National Newspaper Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives acknowledge the vital role played by the newspaper industry as a pillar of our democracy and congratulate the Pennsylvania NewsMedia Association and its membership and affiliates upon the celebration of "National Newspaper Week" and for their role in encouraging an active citizenry and an informed public; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the Pennsylvania NewsMedia Association.20250HR0322PN2331 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
