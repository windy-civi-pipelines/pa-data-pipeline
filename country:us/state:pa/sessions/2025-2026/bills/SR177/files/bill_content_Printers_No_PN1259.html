A RESOLUTION
Directing the Joint State Government Commission to establish a
legislative task force with an advisory committee of
stakeholders to review, update and codify Pennsylvania's
emergency management services law.
WHEREAS, Emergency management is a core function of
government; and
WHEREAS, Pennsylvania's Emergency Management Code, contained
within Title 35 (Health and Safety) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, directs and authorizes every political
subdivision to have an emergency management program that
includes a trained Emergency Management Coordinator, an
Emergency Operations Plan and a functioning Emergency Operations
Center with a trained staff; and
WHEREAS, Title 35 has not been updated since 1996, although
our emergency response network has improved dramatically over
the past 30 years; and
WHEREAS, It is imperative to update the Pennsylvania
Emergency Management Code to reflect the lessons learned over
