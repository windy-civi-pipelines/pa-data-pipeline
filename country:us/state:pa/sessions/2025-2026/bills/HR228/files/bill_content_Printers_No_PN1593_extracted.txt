2025-2026 Regular Session HR 228 PN 1593 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1593 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.228 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CUTLER, SCHWEYER, VENKAT, KHAN, FREEMAN, STAATS, COOK, GUENST, HANBIDGE, REICHARD, SANCHEZ, D. WILLIAMS, NEILSON AND DELLOSO, MAY 5, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LABOR AND INDUSTRY, MAY 5, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating July 31, 2025, as "Intern Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The Commonwealth of Pennsylvania recognizes the positive impact that experiential learning has on preparing students for success in life; andWHEREAS, All students within this Commonwealth should have access to experiential learning opportunities; andWHEREAS, Experiential learning opportunities, including work-based learning are important to the economic vitality of this Commonwealth and its communities; andWHEREAS, Work-based learning serves to connect students' skills and competencies gained from education with those used in the workplace; andWHEREAS, There are more than 80 career and technical centers across this Commonwealth which help provide students with workplace skills, helping them gain placement in internships and the workforce; andWHEREAS, On the last Thursday in July, "National Intern Day" 1234567891011121314151617 is celebrated across the country by millions of students, higher education institutions, employers and other organizations; andWHEREAS, In recognition of "National Intern Day," Pennsylvania is committed to elevating awareness, advancing meaningful and diverse work-based learning opportunities and acknowledging the students, educational institutions and employers involved in internships in Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, The Commonwealth recognizes the importance of internships and strives to be the best place to live, work and raise a family; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate July 31, 2025, as "Intern Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0228PN1593 - 2 - 123456789101112
