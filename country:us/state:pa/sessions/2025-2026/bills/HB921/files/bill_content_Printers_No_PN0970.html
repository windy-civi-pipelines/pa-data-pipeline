AN ACT
Amending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An
act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public
welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in general powers and
duties of the Department of Public Welfare, providing for
Low-Income Household Water Assistance Program; and making an
editorial change.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Article II heading of the act of June 13, 1967
(P.L.31, No.21), known as the Human Services Code, is amended to
read:
ARTICLE II
GENERAL POWERS AND DUTIES
OF THE DEPARTMENT OF [PUBLIC WELFARE] HUMAN SERVICES
Section 2. The act is amended by adding a section to read:
Secti on 217. Low-Income Household Water Assistance
Program.--(a) No later than July 1, 2026 , the department, in
consultation with the Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission,

shall establish the Low-Income Household Water Assistance
Program, to be administered by the department.
(b) The purpose of the program is to provide assistance for
eligible individuals who are water system customers or
wastewater system customers in this Commonwealth for overdue
water system bills or wastewater system bills upon the
termination of the temporary Federal Low-Income Household Water
Assistance Program.
(c) The department shall develop eligibility requirements
under the program in accordance with this subsection, without
limitation. An individual eligible for assistance under the
program must meet all of the following requirements:
(1) Own or rent a home in this Commonwealth.
(2) Be a water system customer or wastewater system customer
and be financially responsible for paying the water system bills
or wastewater system bills.
(3) Have an overdue water system bill or wastewater system
bill, which has resulted in arrearages, a termination notice or
the termination of water system services or wastewater system
services.
(4) Comply with certain income guidelines as determined by
the department.
(d) A water system entity or wastewater system entity may
apply for a grant under the program on behalf of eligible
individuals described under subsection (c) on a form and in a
manner determined by the department. A completed application
must contain all of the following:
(1) The name and contact information of the water system
entity or wastewater system entity seeking the grant.
(2) The names and addresses of the water system customers or

(3) The amount of the overdue water system bills or

(e) The department shall review each application under

receive assistance under the program and any conditions
regarding the award of the grant.
(f) A grant under the program may only be used for the
following purposes :
(1) Preventing the termination of water system services or
wastewater system services.
(2) Paying past-due water system bills or wastewater system
bills.
(3) Restoring terminated water system services or wastewater
system services.
(g) The following shall apply to reports:

(ii) The water system entities and wastewater system
entities that have received a grant under the program.
