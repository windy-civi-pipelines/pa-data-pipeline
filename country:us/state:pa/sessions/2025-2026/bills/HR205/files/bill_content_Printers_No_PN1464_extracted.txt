2025-2026 Regular Session HR 205 PN 1464 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1464 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.205 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, HILL-EVANS, MERSKI, HOHENSTEIN, GIRAL, SANCHEZ, PASHINSKI, K.HARRIS, RIVERA, KHAN AND GREEN, APRIL 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, APRIL 22, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating June 24, 2025, as "Universal Day of the Romanian Blouse" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, There are more than 167,000 Romanian immigrants living in the United States; andWHEREAS, More than 4,200 Romanian immigrants reside in this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, The traditional Romanian blouse holds profound cultural significance as an iconic symbol of Romanian heritage and craftsmanship; andWHEREAS, The Romanian blouse represents centuries of tradition, artistry and cultural identity of the Romanian people; andWHEREAS, Designating a specific day to honor the Romanian blouse provides an opportunity for residents of this Commonwealth to commemorate and showcase this integral part of Romanian culture; andWHEREAS, It is essential to recognize and celebrate the 1234567891011121314151617 cultural diversity of this Commonwealth and its residents; andWHEREAS, The first "Universal Day of the Romanian Blouse" was designated on June 24, 2013, by the online community La Blouse Roumaine; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate June 24, 2025, as "Universal Day of the Romanian Blouse" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0205PN1464 - 2 - 1234567
