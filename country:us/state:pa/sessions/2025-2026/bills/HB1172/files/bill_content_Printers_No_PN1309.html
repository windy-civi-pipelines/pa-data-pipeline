AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles, further
providing for definitions, for designation of snowmobile and
ATV roads and for operation in safe manner.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 7702 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a definition to read:
7702. Definitions.
The following words and phrases when used in this chapter
shall have, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise, the
meanings given to them in this section:
* * *
"Utility terrain vehicle" or "UTV." A motorized off-highway
vehicle equipped with four or more tires, bucket or bench seats,
not including a saddle, that has:
(1) a maximum width of 64 inches and a maximum dry
weight of 1,700 pounds; or

(2) a width which exceeds 64 inches or a dry weight

The term does not include an ATV, snowmobile, trail bike,
motorboat, golf cart, aircraft, dune buggy, automobile,
construction machine, truck or home utility machine; military,
fire, emergency and law enforcement vehicle; implement of
husbandry; multipurpose agricultural vehicle; vehicle used by
the department; any vehicle that is or is required to be
registered under Chapter 13; or an off-road motor vehicle used
exclusively as a utility vehicle for an agricultural or business
operation and incidentally operated or moved upon the highway.

Section 2. Section 7722 heading and (a) of Title 75 are
amended to read:
7722. Designation of snowmobile, UTV and ATV roads.
(a) [General rule.--The] Authority to designate.--

traffic or whether snowmobiles, UTV's and ATV's may share
this designated road with vehicular traffic.

ATV road, or combination, on which snowmobiles, UTV's, ATV's
or a combination may share the State-designated highway with

vehicular traffic:

[(5) Without a securely fastened helmet on the head of

or ATV or who is being towed or otherwise propelled by a
