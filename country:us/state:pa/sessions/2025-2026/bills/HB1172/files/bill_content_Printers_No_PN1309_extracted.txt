2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1172 PN 1309 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1309 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1172 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SMITH, BANTA, JAMES, MOUL, BARGER, COOK, BARTON, STRUZZI, ARMANINI, OWLETT, BASHLINE, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ AND KERWIN, APRIL 9, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, APRIL 9, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles, further providing for definitions, for designation of snowmobile and ATV roads and for operation in safe manner.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 7702 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a definition to read:§ 7702. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this chapter shall have, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise, the meanings given to them in this section:* * *"Utility terrain vehicle" or "UTV." A motorized off-highway vehicle equipped with four or more tires, bucket or bench seats, not including a saddle, that has:(1) a maximum width of 64 inches and a maximum dry weight of 1,700 pounds; or123456789101112131415161718 (2) a width which exceeds 64 inches or a dry weight which exceeds 1,700 pounds.The term does not include an ATV, snowmobile, trail bike, motorboat, golf cart, aircraft, dune buggy, automobile, construction machine, truck or home utility machine; military, fire, emergency and law enforcement vehicle; implement of husbandry; multipurpose agricultural vehicle; vehicle used by the department; any vehicle that is or is required to be registered under Chapter 13; or an off-road motor vehicle used exclusively as a utility vehicle for an agricultural or business operation and incidentally operated or moved upon the highway.* * *Section 2. Section 7722 heading and (a) of Title 75 are amended to read:§ 7722. Designation of snowmobile, UTV and ATV roads.(a) [General rule.--The] Authority to designate.--(1) Except as provided under paragraph (2), the Department of Transportation on State-designated highways and local authorities on any highway, road or street within its jurisdiction may designate any highway, road or street within its jurisdiction as a snowmobile road, a UTV road, an ATV road, or [both] combination, and may, in its discretion, determine whether such road shall be closed to vehicular traffic or whether snowmobiles, UTV's and ATV's may share this designated road with vehicular traffic.(2) A county may, through the adoption of a resolution, designate a State-designated highway located within the boundaries of the county as a snowmobile road, a UTV road, an ATV road, or combination, on which snowmobiles, UTV's, ATV's or a combination may share the State-designated highway with 20250HB1172PN1309 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 vehicular traffic within the boundaries of the county.* * *Section 3. Section 7726(c) introductory paragraph and (5) of Title 75 are amended and the section is amended by adding a subsection to read:§ 7726. Operation in safe manner.* * *(c) Operation on highways and streets open to snowmobiles [or], UTV's, ATV's and vehicular traffic.--No person shall operate a snowmobile, UTV or ATV in any of the following ways on highways and streets open to snowmobiles, UTV's or ATV's and vehicular traffic:* * *[(5) Without a securely fastened helmet on the head of an individual who operates or is a passenger on a snowmobile or ATV or who is being towed or otherwise propelled by a snowmobile. The department shall specify the types of helmets allowed through rules and regulations.](d) Helmets.--(1) Except as provided under paragraph (2), an individual may not operate a snowmobile or ATV on highways and streets open to snowmobiles or ATV's and vehicular traffic without a securely fastened helmet on the head of the individual and any passenger on the snowmobile or ATV or being towed or otherwise propelled by the snowmobile or ATV.(2) A helmet is not required for an individual who is at least 18 years of age and operates or is a passenger on a UTV that is equipped with all of the following:(i) A bucket or bench seat in lieu of a saddle seat.(ii) A seat belt for the operator and each 20250HB1172PN1309 - 3 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 passenger.(iii) A roll cage.(3) The department shall specify the types of helmets allowed under this subsection through rules and regulations.Section 4. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1172PN1309 - 4 - 12345
