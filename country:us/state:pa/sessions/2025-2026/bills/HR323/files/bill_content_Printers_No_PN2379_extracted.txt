2025-2026 Regular Session HR 323 PN 2379 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRIOR PRINTER'S NO. 2337PRINTER'S NO. 2379THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.323 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SAPPEY, HILL-EVANS, McNEILL, WAXMAN, SCHLOSSBERG, HOWARD, BRENNAN, VITALI, GUENST, GALLAGHER, SHUSTERMAN, SANCHEZ, CONKLIN, STEELE, ISAACSON, FRANKEL, CIRESI, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, PASHINSKI, KLUNK AND NEILSON, SEPTEMBER 24, 2025 AS REPORTED FROM COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE AND RURAL AFFAIRS, HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, AS AMENDED, SEPTEMBER 30, 2025A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month MONTHS of October 2025 AND OCTOBER 2026 as "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" is dedicated to raising awareness about how to keep animals safe and protected; andWHEREAS, Animal safety and protection efforts in the United States began in Massachusetts, where the General Court included a law in the Body of Liberties of 1641 that prohibited animal cruelty; andWHEREAS, The exact origins of the national observance are unclear but it is widely believed to have been established by an organization focused on promoting the safe coexistence of humans and animals; andWHEREAS, People can celebrate the holiday by microchipping their pets to help prevent them from getting lost, volunteering at animal shelters to support animals in need and visiting zoos <--1234567891011121314151617 to further educate themselves about animal care and conservation; andWHEREAS, "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" is important because it promotes animal safety by educating the public on proper care and precautions, helps to reduce animal abuse by raising awareness about what animal abuse is and how to report it and supports animal shelters through increased donations from animal lovers during the month; andWHEREAS, "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" is observed annually in October; andWHEREAS, It is important for all Pennsylvanians to be aware of "National Animal Safety and Protection Month," the origin of "National Animal Safety and Protection Month," when "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" is held annually and the purpose and impact of "National Animal Safety and Protection Month"; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month MONTHS of October 2025 AND OCTOBER 2026 as "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0323PN2379 - 2 - <--12345678910111213141516171819
