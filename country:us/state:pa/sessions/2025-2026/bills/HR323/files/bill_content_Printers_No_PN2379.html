A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the month MONTHS of October 2025 AND OCTOBER 2026 as
"National Animal Safety and Protection Month" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" is
dedicated to raising awareness about how to keep animals safe
and protected; and
WHEREAS, Animal safety and protection efforts in the United
States began in Massachusetts, where the General Court included
a law in the Body of Liberties of 1641 that prohibited animal
cruelty; and
WHEREAS, The exact origins of the national observance are
unclear but it is widely believed to have been established by an
organization focused on promoting the safe coexistence of humans
and animals; and
WHEREAS, People can celebrate the holiday by microchipping
their pets to help prevent them from getting lost, volunteering
at animal shelters to support animals in need and visiting zoos

WHEREAS, "National Animal Safety and Protection Month" is

donations from animal lovers during the month; and

WHEREAS, It is important for all Pennsylvanians to be aware

purpose and impact of "National Animal Safety and Protection
Month"; therefore be it
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the
month MONTHS of October 2025 AND OCTOBER 2026 as "National
Animal Safety and Protection Month" in Pennsylvania.
