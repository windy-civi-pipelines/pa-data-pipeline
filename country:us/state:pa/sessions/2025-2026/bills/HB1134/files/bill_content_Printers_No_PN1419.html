AN ACT
Amending Title 3 (Agriculture) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in food protection, further providing for
misbranding of food.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 5729(a) of Title 3 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a paragraph and the
section is amended by adding a subsection to read:
5729. Misbranding of food.
(a) General rule.--A food shall be misbranded:
* * *
(13) If it bears or contains Blue 1 (CAS 3844-45-9),
Blue 2 (CAS 860-22-0), Green 3 (CAS 2353-45-9), Red 40 (CAS
25956-17-6), Yellow 5 (CAS 1934-21-0) or Yellow 6 (CAS 2783-
94-0), unless it bears the following warning, in boldface
type, on the principle display panel and in a type size no

Section 2. This act shall take effect in one year.
