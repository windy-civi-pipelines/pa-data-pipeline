AN ACT

"An act designating the official flag of the Commonwealth of
Pennsylvania, and describing the same; providing for the
carrying of such flag by the regiments of the National Guard
of Pennsylvania; authorizing the Secretary of the
Commonwealth to provide, and have deposited in the office of
Secretary of the Commonwealth, a model of said flag, and
making an appropriation therefor," further providing for
title of act; and providing for the proper procedure to fold
the designated flag of the Commonwealth.

Designating the official flag of the Commonwealth of
Pennsylvania, and describing the same; providing for the
carrying of such flag by the regiments of the National Guard
of Pennsylvania; authorizing the Secretary of the
Commonwealth to provide, and have deposited in the office of
Secretary of the Commonwealth, a model of said flag,
providing for the proper procedure to fold the flag, and
making an appropriation therefor.
Section 2. The act is amended by adding a section to read:
Section 3.1. The following procedures constitute the proper
way to fold the flag for presentation or display:
(1) Fold the flag four times lengthwise so that one section
displays the Commonwealth crest.
(2) Fold each side behind the displayed section at a ninety-
degree angle so that the display section forms a triangle.
(3) Take the section ending with the hoist and fold it at a
ninety-degree angle across the bottom of the display section and
then fold the hoist back over so it is aligned with the middle
of the display section.
(4) Fold the other protruding section directly upwards so
that its edge is flush with the display section and then fold it
upwards along a forty-five-degree angle so that a mirror of the
display section triangle is formed.
(5) Fold the mirror section in half from the point upwards,
then fold the remaining portion upwards, tucking it between the
display section and the remainder of the flag.
Section 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
