2025-2026 Regular Session HR 210 PN 1522 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1522 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.210 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY GROVE, REICHARD, PROBST, COOPER AND KAZEEM, APRIL 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LOCAL GOVERNMENT, APRIL 28, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the week of May 18 through 24, 2025, as "National Public Works Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Public works professionals focus on infrastructure, facilities and services that are of vital importance to sustainable and resilient communities and to the public health, high quality of life and well-being of the residents of this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Infrastructure, facilities and services could not be provided without the dedicated efforts of public works professionals who are engineers, managers and employees at all levels of government and the private sector; andWHEREAS, Public works professionals are responsible for rebuilding, improving and protecting our nation's transportation, water supply, water treatment and solid waste systems, public buildings and other structures and facilities essential for our citizens; andWHEREAS, It is in the public interest for the residents, civic leaders and children in this Commonwealth to gain 123456789101112131415161718 knowledge of and maintain an ongoing interest and understanding of the importance of public works and public works programs in their respective communities; andWHEREAS, The year 2025 marks the 65th annual "National Public Works Week" sponsored by the American Public Works Association and the Canadian Public Works Association; andWHEREAS, The theme for "National Public Works Week" in 2025 is "People, Purpose, Presence," highlighting the ideals that motivate public works professionals to serve their communities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the week of May 18 through 24, 2025, as "National Public Works Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives urge all residents to join with representatives of the American Public Works Association and government agencies in activities, events and ceremonies designed to pay tribute to our public works professionals, engineers, managers and employees; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the substantial contributions that public works professionals make to protecting our national health and safety and advancing quality of life for all.20250HR0210PN1522 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
