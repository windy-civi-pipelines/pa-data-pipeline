A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the month of October 2025 as "Dyslexia Awareness
Month" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Dyslexia is a specific learning disability that is
neurological in origin and characterized by difficulties with
accurate and/or fluent word recognition, poor spelling and
decoding abilities; and
WHEREAS, Dyslexia affects one in five individuals, impacting
people of all ages, races and socioeconomic backgrounds; and
WHEREAS, Increased awareness and understanding of dyslexia
can lead to early diagnosis and intervention, significantly
improving the educational and life outcomes for individuals with
dyslexia; and
WHEREAS, It is vital to provide support and resources for
educators, parents and individuals affected by dyslexia to help
them achieve their full potential; and
WHEREAS, Recognizing the month of October 2025 as "Dyslexia
Awareness Month" promotes awareness, understanding and advocacy
