2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1380 PN 1581 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1581 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1380 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY STENDER, ARMANINI, GILLEN, HAMM, E. NELSON, RADER AND WATRO, MAY 2, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, MAY 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), entitled "An act creating the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources consisting of certain functions of the Department of Environmental Resources and the Department of Community Affairs; renaming the Department of Environmental Resources as the Department of Environmental Protection; defining the role of the Environmental Quality Board in the Department of Environmental Protection; making changes to responsibilities of the State Conservation Commission and the Department of Agriculture; transferring certain powers and duties to the Department of Health; and repealing inconsistent acts," in Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, further providing for forests.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 302(d) of the act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), known as the Conservation and Natural Resources Act, is amended by adding a paragraph to read:Section 302. Forests.* * *(d) Chief Forest Fire Warden.--The Chief Forest Fire Warden, subject to the approval of the secretary, shall have the following powers and duties to:1234567891011121314151617181920212223 * * *(13) Submit an annual report no later than March 1 of each calendar year to the chairperson and minority chairperson of the Environmental Resources and Energy Committee of the Senate, the chairperson and minority chairperson of the Environmental and Natural Resource Protection Committee of the House of Representatives, the chairperson and minority chairperson of the Veterans Affairs and Emergency Preparedness Committee of the Senate and the chairperson and minority chairperson of the Veterans Affairs and Emergency Preparedness Committee of the House of Representatives, which shall include the following information:(i) Activities carried out under fire resource plans in the previous calendar year.(ii) The amount and locations of wildfires in this Commonwealth in the previous calendar year.(iii) Actions taken by the Chief Forest Fire Warden or local forest fire wardens to prevent wildfires in this Commonwealth in the previous calendar year.(iv) The amount and locations of prescribed or controlled burns carried out in this Commonwealth in the previous calendar year.(v) The estimated Statewide manpower for combating wildfires, including an active list of firefighters submitted by local forest fire wardens in the previous calendar year.(vi) A list of on-hand equipment for combating wildfires submitted by local forest fire wardens in the previous calendar year.20250HB1380PN1581 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 * * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1380PN1581 - 3 - 12
