2025-2026 Regular Session SR 166 PN 1217 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1217 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.166 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MARTIN, KANE, PHILLIPS-HILL, ROTHMAN, TARTAGLIONE, STEFANO AND COSTA, OCTOBER 7, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, OCTOBER 7, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating October 5 through 11, 2025, January 11 through 17, 2026, and April 5 through 11, 2026, as "PIAA Officials Appreciation Weeks" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The observance of "PIAA Officials Appreciation Weeks" serves to acknowledge and celebrate the contributions of the more than 13,000 Pennsylvania Interscholastic Athletic Association (PIAA) registered officials for interscholastic athletics; andWHEREAS, The PIAA Board of Directors announced that October 5 through 11, 2025, will mark the first of three "PIAA Officials Appreciation Weeks" taking place this year; andWHEREAS, The appreciation week for winter sports officials will take place January 11 through 17, 2026, and for spring sports it will take place April 5 through 11, 2026; andWHEREAS, The PIAA is promoting the appreciation weeks and requests its member schools to do the same because a game would not be possible without officials; andWHEREAS, PIAA officials make a difference in the lives of 123456789101112131415161718 student-athletes everywhere, encouraging exercise through competition, promoting sportsmanship and establishing lifelong fundamentals of fairness; andWHEREAS, PIAA officials work tirelessly to create a safe and fair athletic environment for student-athletes and coaches; andWHEREAS, PIAA officials have a difficult job due to pressure to make correct calls while maintaining order between opposing fan bases and in the game itself; andWHEREAS, On October 25, 2023, the Senate renewed its commitment to the safety of our sports officials by passing Senate Bill 842, creating a separate offense of harassment towards an official of a sporting event; andWHEREAS, It is both reasonable and appropriate to designate "PIAA Officials Appreciation Weeks" in Pennsylvania and encourage schools, coaches, student-athletes and fans to participate and thank officials who put their time and dedication into sports and the PIAA community; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate designate October 5 through 11, 2025, January 11 through 17, 2026, and April 5 through 11, 2026, as "PIAA Officials Appreciation Weeks" in Pennsylvania.20250SR0166PN1217 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920
