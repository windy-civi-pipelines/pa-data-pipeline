AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in licensing of drivers, further providing for
issuance and content of driver's license.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 1510 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:
§ 1510. Issuance and content of driver's license.
* * *
(a.2) Person with disability.--
(1) A person with a disability who renews a driver's
license or identification card and meets the qualifications
of section 1338(a)(1), (2), (3), (4), (5), (6), (7) or (8)
(relating to person with disability plate and placard) or is
a veteran whose service-connected disability is certified at
100% by the service unit of the armed forces in which the
veteran served or by the United States Department of Veterans

Affairs or who has a service-connected disability of the type
enumerated in section 1338 may request that the department
issue a driver's license or identification card using the
most recent color photograph or photographic facsimile and
signature or facsimile of the signature of the person with a
disability in the department's possession.
(2) The department may not issue a driver's license or
identification card to the person with a disability if there
is no color photograph or photographic facsimile or signature
or facsimile of the signature of the person with a disability
in the department's possession. The department may require
additional information or documentation necessary to comply
with this subsection.
(3) The department may not issue a driver's license or
identification card to the person with a disability if
material changes need to be made to the person's record,
including, but not limited to, change of name or a correction
of information such as height or eye color, which shall be
made in person.
(4) The department may not issue a driver's license or
identification card that complies with the act of May 26,
2017 (P.L.6, No.3), known as the Pennsylvania REAL ID
Compliance Act, to the person with a disability if the color
photograph or photographic facsimile of the person with a
disability in the department's possession is:
(i) over sixteen years of age; or
(ii) will be over sixteen years of age at the next
four-year renewal interval required by section 1514
(relating to expiration and renewal of drivers'
licenses).
