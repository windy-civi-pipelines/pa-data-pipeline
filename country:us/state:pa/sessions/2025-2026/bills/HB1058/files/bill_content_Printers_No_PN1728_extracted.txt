WILLIAMS, GREEN, KHAN AND WEBSTER, MARCH 26, 2025 AS AMENDED ON SECOND CONSIDERATION, HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, MAY 14, 2025AN ACTAmending the act of August 26, 1971 (P.L.351, No.91), entitled "An act providing for a State Lottery and administration thereof; authorizing the creation of a State Lottery Commission; prescribing its powers and duties; disposition of funds; violations and penalties therefor; exemption of prizes from State and local taxation and making an appropriation," in State Lottery, further providing for STATEMENT OF PURPOSE AND FOR powers and duties of secretary while generating maximum profits from lottery activities to sustain or increase the funding levels for the programs that benefit Commonwealth seniors each fiscal year.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 303(a)(11)(iv) of the act of August 26, 1971 (P.L.351, No.91), known as the State Lottery Law, is amended to read:SECTION 1. SECTIONS 301 AND 303(A)(11)(IV) OF THE ACT OF AUGUST 26, 1971 (P.L.351, NO.91), KNOWN AS THE STATE LOTTERY LAW, ARE AMENDED TO READ:SECTION 301.
