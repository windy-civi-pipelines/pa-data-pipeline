2025-2026 Regular Session HR 72 PN 0584 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 584 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.72 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY WEBSTER AND HANBIDGE, FEBRUARY 12, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON RULES, FEBRUARY 12, 2025 A RESOLUTIONAmending the Rules of the House of Representatives to provide for meeting publications.RESOLVED, That the Rules of the House of Representatives be amended by adding a rule to read:RULE 79Meeting PublicationsAn in-person or virtual meeting between a member and a nonconstituent where State law, regulation, legislation or proposed addition, repeal or amendment to law, regulation or legislation is discussed shall be published on the member's official State website. The following information shall be included in the publication on the member's website:(1) Date and time of meeting.(2) Names of all nonconstituents attending the meeting.(3) Primary organizational affiliations of all nonconstituents attending the meeting.In addition to the provisions of this rule, all provisions of 65 Pa.C.S. Ch. 7 (relating to open meetings) shall be followed.123456789101112131415161718
