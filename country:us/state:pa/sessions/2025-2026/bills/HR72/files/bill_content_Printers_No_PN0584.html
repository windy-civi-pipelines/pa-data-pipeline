A RESOLUTION
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to provide
for meeting publications.
RESOLVED, That the Rules of the House of Representatives be
amended by adding a rule to read:
RULE 79
Meeting Publications
An in-person or virtual meeting between a member and a
nonconstituent where State law, regulation, legislation or
proposed addition, repeal or amendment to law, regulation or
legislation is discussed shall be published on the member's
official State website. The following information shall be
included in the publication on the member's website:
(1) Date and time of meeting.
(2) Names of all nonconstituents attending the meeting.
(3) Primary organizational affiliations of all
nonconstituents attending the meeting.
In addition to the provisions of this rule, all provisions of
65 Pa.C.S. Ch. 7 (relating to open meetings) shall be followed.
