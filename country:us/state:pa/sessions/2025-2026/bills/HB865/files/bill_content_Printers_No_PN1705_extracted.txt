DAVIS, K.HARRIS, RUSNOCK AND SCHEUREN, MARCH 11, 2025 AS AMENDED ON SECOND CONSIDERATION, HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, MAY 13, 2025AN ACTAmending Title 51 (Military Affairs) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in Military Family Education Program, further providing for definitions, for eligibility, for Military Family Education Program grants and for limitations.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The definition of "eligible member" in section 3210.1 of Title 51 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:§ 3210.1. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this subchapter shall have the meanings given to them in this section unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:* * *"Eligible member." A member of the Pennsylvania National Guard who [has completed an initial service obligation in the 12345678910111213141516 Pennsylvania National Guard and] is eligible to enlist or reenlist in the Pennsylvania National Guard for a period of six years or, if a commissioned officer, is eligible to serve in the Pennsylvania National Guard for [an additional] six years.* * *Section 2. Sections 3210.2(a)(2), 3210.4(a) and 3210.6 of Title 51 are amended to read:§ 3210.2. Eligibility.(a) General rule.--A family member is eligible to receive a Military Family Education Program grant under this subchapter if all of the following eligibility criteria are met:* * *(2) The eligible member accepted an obligation to serve in the Pennsylvania National Guard for a period of six years and has not accepted an obligation under Subchapter A (relating to Educational Assistance Program) or AND this subchapter for the same six-year period.* * *§ 3210.4. Military Family Education Program grants.(a) General rule.--From the funds appropriated for the purposes of this subchapter, Military Family Education Program grants shall be provided to eligible family members for the purpose of pursuing an approved program of education. A Military Family Education Program grant shall be treated as a benefit earned by an eligible member for honorable service to the country and this Commonwealth. A Military Family Education Program benefit shall ONLY be renewable with each SUBSEQUENT six-year service commitment in the Pennsylvania National Guard. * * *§ 3210.6.
