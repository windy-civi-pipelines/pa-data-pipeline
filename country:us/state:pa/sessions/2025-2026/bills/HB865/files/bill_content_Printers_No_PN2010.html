AN ACT
Amending Title 51 (Military Affairs) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in Military Family Education Program,
further providing for definitions, for eligibility, for
Military Family Education Program grants and for limitations.

CONSOLIDATED STATUTES, IN EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM,
FURTHER PROVIDING FOR DEFINITIONS, FOR ELIGIBILITY AND FOR
RECOUPMENT OF GRANT PAYMENTS; AND, IN MILITARY FAMILY
EDUCATION PROGRAM, FURTHER PROVIDING FOR DEFINITIONS, FOR
ELIGIBILITY, FOR LIMITATIONS AND FOR RECOUPMENT OF MILITARY
FAMILY EDUCATION PROGRAM GRANT PAYMENTS.
