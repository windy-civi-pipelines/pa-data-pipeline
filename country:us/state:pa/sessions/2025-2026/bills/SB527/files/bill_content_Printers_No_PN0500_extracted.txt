Penalties and enforcement of nonpayment of taxes.The council of a city of the first class shall have the power to prescribe and enforce penalties for the nonpayment, within the time fixed for their payment, of taxes imposed under the authority of this chapter and for the violation of the provisions of ordinances passed under the authority of this chapter.Section 604.
