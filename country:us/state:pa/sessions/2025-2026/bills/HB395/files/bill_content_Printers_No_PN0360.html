AN ACT
Amending Title 35 (Health and Safety) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in emergency medical services system,
further providing for emergency medical responders.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 8114 of Title 35 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:
§ 8114. Emergency medical responders.
* * *
(b.1) National certification reciprocity.--
(1) The department shall provide a provisional
certification as an EMR to an individual who possesses a
valid and current certification from the National Registry of
Emergency Medical Technicians as an emergency medical
responder, a certified first responder, an emergency medical
technician, an advanced emergency medical technician or a
paramedic.
(2) A provisional certification issued under paragraph

(3) A candidate who is seeking provisional certification
