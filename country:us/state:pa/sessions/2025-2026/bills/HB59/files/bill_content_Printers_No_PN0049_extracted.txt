2025-2026 Regular Session HB 59 PN 0049 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 49 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.59 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. BROWN, ROWE AND JAMES, JANUARY 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON PROFESSIONAL LICENSURE, JANUARY 14, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of May 3, 1933 (P.L.242, No.86), entitled "An act to promote the public health and safety by providing for examination, licensing and granting of permits for those who desire to engage in the profession of cosmetology; defining cosmetology, and regulating cosmetology salons, schools, students, apprentices, teachers, managers, manicurists and cosmetologists; conferring powers and duties upon the Commissioner of Professional and Occupational Affairs of the Department of State; providing for appeals to certain courts by applicants and licensees; and providing penalties," further providing for powers and duties of board.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 13 of the act of May 3, 1933 (P.L.242, No.86), referred to as the Cosmetology Law, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 13. Powers and Duties of Board.--* * *(d) The board shall appoint inspectors whose title shall be "Inspector, State Board of Cosmetology, Commonwealth of Pennsylvania." Each inspector shall be a licensed cosmetologist who has been actively engaged in the practice of cosmetology for at least five years. Each inspector shall hold office at the 12345678910111213141516171819202122 pleasure of the board and shall receive compensation as fixed by the board with the approval of the department. Each inspector shall have the right of entry into any place where the business or profession of cosmetology is carried out for the purpose of inspection and for the investigation of complaints before the board and for other matters as the board may direct.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0059PN0049 - 2 - 1234567
