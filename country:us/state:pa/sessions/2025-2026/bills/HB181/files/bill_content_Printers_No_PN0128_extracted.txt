2025-2026 Regular Session HB 181 PN 0128 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 128 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.181 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MERSKI, SANCHEZ, PIELLI, HILL-EVANS, HADDOCK, DONAHUE, HARKINS, KUZMA, KHAN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, PROBST, STEHR AND COOK, JANUARY 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LOCAL GOVERNMENT, JANUARY 16, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 53 (Municipalities Generally) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in general provisions relating to government and administration, providing for transparency of political subdivision authorities; and, in municipal authorities, providing for transparency of municipal authority.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 53 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding sections to read:ยง 1144. Transparency of political subdivision authorities.All authorities created by a political subdivision shall maintain a publicly accessible Internet website which includes the following:(1) the name of each board member of the authority;(2) the length and expiration date of the term to which each board member was appointed;(3) an email address which may be used to contact each board member;12345678910111213141516171819 (4) the name and office of the individual who appointed each board member; and(5) the publicly accessible Internet website of the government entity that established the authority.ยง 5624. Transparency of municipal authority.An authority shall maintain a publicly accessible Internet website which includes the following:(1) the name of each board member of the authority;(2) the length and expiration date of the term to which each board member was appointed;(3) an email address which may be used to contact each board member;(4) the name of the municipality that appointed each board member; and(5) the publicly accessible Internet website of the municipality that established the authority or, in cases of authorities established by multiple municipalities, the publicly accessible Internet website of each municipality that established the authority.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0181PN0128 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920
