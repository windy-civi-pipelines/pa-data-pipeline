2025-2026 Regular Session HR 244 PN 1802 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1802 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.244 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BURGOS, HILL-EVANS, SANCHEZ AND CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, JUNE 2, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, JUNE 2, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDirecting the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee to conduct a performance audit of the Philadelphia Department of Public Health's Tobacco Policy and Control Program as it relates to the investigations of tobacco outlets in the city.WHEREAS, The Philadelphia Department of Public Health established the Tobacco Policy and Control Program to address tobacco use and its health implications; andWHEREAS, The goal of the Tobacco Policy and Control Program is to reduce smoking rates and expand smoke-free environments throughout the city; andWHEREAS, The Tobacco Policy and Control Program enforces policies that restrict smoking and restrict those who can buy tobacco products; andWHEREAS, For the City of Philadelphia's fiscal year 2024, $408,645 of State funding was specifically allocated toward the Comprehensive Tobacco Control Program Grant designated for the Tobacco Policy and Control Program; andWHEREAS, The purpose of the Comprehensive Tobacco Control Program Grant is to reduce health complications related to 12345678910111213141516171819 tobacco use through prevention, cessation, community-based public awareness and other services and initiatives; andWHEREAS, The Tobacco Policy and Control Program helps to conduct investigations to ensure that tobacco restrictions are systematically and uniformly enforced; andWHEREAS, However, concerns have been expressed that the investigations are not being conducted in a systematic and uniform manner; andWHEREAS, The Legislative Budget and Finance Committee has the authority to conduct a performance review of the Tobacco Policy and Control Program due to the program's funding through a State grant; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives direct the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee to conduct a performance audit of the Philadelphia Department of Public Health's Tobacco Policy and Control Program as it relates to the investigations of tobacco outlets in the city; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the audit include a review of the performance measures and objectives of the Tobacco Policy and Control Program; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee report its findings and conclusions to the House of Representatives within 18 months of the adoption of this resolution.20250HR0244PN1802 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324
