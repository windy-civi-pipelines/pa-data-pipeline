AN ACT
Amending Title 8 (Boroughs and Incorporated Towns) of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in associations and
organizations, further providing for county and regional
associations of boroughs.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 702(b) of Title 8 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:
§ 702. County and regional associations of boroughs.
* * *
(b) Appropriation.--A borough may annually appropriate [no
more than $100] funds for the support of the county or regional
association of boroughs.
* * *
Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
