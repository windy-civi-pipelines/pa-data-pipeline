2025-2026 Regular Session SB 196 PN 0146 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 146 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE BILL No.196 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BROWN, TARTAGLIONE AND HAYWOOD, JANUARY 29, 2025 REFERRED TO AGING AND YOUTH, JANUARY 29, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of July 15, 2004 (P.L.731, No.85), entitled "An act providing for immunization against the influenza virus and pneumococcal disease for elderly persons," further providing for definitions; and making editorial changes.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The title and section 1 of the act of July 15, 2004 (P.L.731, No.85), known as the Elderly Immunization Act, are amended to read:AN ACTProviding for immunization against the influenza virus and pneumococcal disease for elderly and high risk persons.Section 1. Short title.This act shall be known and may be cited as the Elderly and High Risk Immunization Act.Section 2. The definition of "eligible person" in section 2 of the act is amended to read:Section 2. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this act shall 12345678910111213141516171819 have the meanings given to them in this section unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:* * *"Eligible person." A person:(1) 65 years of age or older[.] for pneumococcal vaccination.(2) 50 years of age or older for influenza vaccination.* * *Section 3. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250SB0196PN0146 - 2 - 123456789
