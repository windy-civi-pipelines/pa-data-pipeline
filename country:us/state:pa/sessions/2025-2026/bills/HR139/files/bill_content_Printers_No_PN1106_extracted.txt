2025-2026 Regular Session HR 139 PN 1106 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1106 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.139 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BENNINGHOFF, BELLMON, COOPER, DIAMOND, FREEMAN, GREINER, HANBIDGE, HEFFLEY, KENYATTA, M. MACKENZIE, MALAGARI, MARCELL, McNEILL, NEILSON, PICKETT, VENKAT AND ZIMMERMAN, MARCH 24, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, MARCH 24, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the week of September 14 through 20, 2025, as "Truck Driver Appreciation Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Alexander Winton invented the semi-trailer in Cleveland, Ohio, in 1898; andWHEREAS, The United States Bureau of Labor Statistics estimates there are 1 million professional men and women classified as light-delivery or heavy and tractor-trailer truck drivers; andWHEREAS, More than 80% of United States' communities depend solely on trucking for delivery of their goods and commodities; andWHEREAS, Truckers deliver approximately 10 billion tons of freight or about 70% of all freight moved in the United States; andWHEREAS, The average trucker drives more than 100,000 road miles per year; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania is home to more than 71,170 trucking 1234567891011121314151617 companies that are primarily small, locally owned businesses, and the industry fuels more than 362,200 jobs, accounting for 1 in 15 jobs in this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, The Bureau of Labor Statistics estimates that approximately 89,920 truckers live in Pennsylvania, and they continue to play an essential and irreplaceable role in keeping this Commonwealth's economy strong and ensuring the efficient movement of goods throughout this Commonwealth and the United States; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania truckers contribute significantly to this Commonwealth's workforce by tirelessly transporting goods across highways and roads throughout this Commonwealth, often in challenging weather conditions, long hours and demanding schedules; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the week of September 14 through 20, 2025, as "Truck Driver Appreciation Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage the residents, organizations and communities of this Commonwealth to join in this observance by commending professional truck drivers for the essential role they have played in the lives of every Pennsylvanian.20250HR0139PN1106 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
