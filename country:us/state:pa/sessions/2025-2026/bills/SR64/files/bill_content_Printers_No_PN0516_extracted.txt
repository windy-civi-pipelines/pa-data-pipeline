2025-2026 Regular Session SR 64 PN 0516 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 516 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.64 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ROBINSON, MASTRIANO, ROTHMAN, PENNYCUICK, YAW, DUSH, FARRY, FONTANA, BARTOLOTTA, BROOKS, BROWN, KANE, PHILLIPS-HILL, LAUGHLIN, CULVER, HAYWOOD, COSTA, MARTIN, STEFANO AND J. WARD, APRIL 2, 2025 REFERRED TO RULES AND EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS, APRIL 2, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing April 15, 2025, as "Purple Up! for Military Kids Day" and the month of April 2025 as the "Month of the Military Child" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, From September 11, 2001, to the end of the Global War on Terrorism, more than 30,000 Pennsylvania National Guard members, reservists and active-duty personnel were deployed; andWHEREAS, The estimated number of children in this Commonwealth having at least one parent currently serving in the National Guard, Reserves or active components of the United States Armed Forces exceeds 37,000; andWHEREAS, Nationwide, more than 2 million children have experienced the deployment of one or more parents since 2001; andWHEREAS, Nearly 44% of military personnel have children who are left behind during these deployments; andWHEREAS, The United States Department of Defense, the United States Armed Forces and other Federal agencies as well as 1234567891011121314151617 nonprofit organizations, businesses and communities throughout our nation join in this observance; andWHEREAS, It is fitting that we recognize and honor the sacrifices and celebrate the spirit of our men and women in uniform; andWHEREAS, Our military personnel deserve to know that we are taking care of their children while they are busy protecting us; andWHEREAS, When parents serve in the military, their children serve as well; andWHEREAS, Purple, symbolizing all branches of the military, is worn as a tribute to our bravely serving men and women; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize April 15, 2025, as "Purple Up! for Military Kids Day" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate encourage all Pennsylvanians to show support for our military children by wearing the color purple on April 15, 2025; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Senate recognize the month of April 2025 as the "Month of the Military Child."20250SR0064PN0516 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
