[Before] Except as provided under subsection (e.1), before a person under the age of 18 years may take the examination for a junior driver's license, including a Class M license to operate a motorcycle, the minor must:123456789101112131415161718 * * *(e.1) Military exception for learner's permits.--For purposes of determining compliance with subsection (e)(1) and (2), the department shall credit the time period during which a learner's permit from another state is held and the number of hours completed of practical driving experience by a minor in the other state, if:(1) The minor is a family member of an active duty service member in the military, including a reserve component or National Guard.(2) The minor resides in this Commonwealth with the active duty service member.(3) The minor has a valid learner's permit issued by another state.(4) The department has issued a learner's permit to the individual.* * *Section 2.
