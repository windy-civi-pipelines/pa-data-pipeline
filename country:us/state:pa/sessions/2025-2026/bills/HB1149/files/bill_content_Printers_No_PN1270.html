AN ACT
Amending the act of November 10, 1999 (P.L.491, No.45), entitled
"An act establishing a uniform construction code; imposing
powers and duties on municipalities and the Department of
Labor and Industry; providing for enforcement; imposing
penalties; and making repeals," in training and certification
of inspectors, providing for residential building inspectors.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The act of November 10, 1999 (P.L.491, No.45),
known as the Pennsylvania Construction Code Act, is amended by
adding a section to read:
Section 704. Residential building inspectors.
In addition to any other requirements from the department, a
residential building inspector shall also complete five hours of
continuing education in recognizing faulty construction
practices, including proper installation of stucco, the laws and
legal liability of builders in faulty construction practices and
the remedies available under Federal or State law for homeowners
