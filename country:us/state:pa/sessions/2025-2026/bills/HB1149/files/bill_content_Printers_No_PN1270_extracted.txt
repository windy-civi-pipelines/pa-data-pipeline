2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1149 PN 1270 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1270 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1149 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HOHENSTEIN, ISAACSON, GALLAGHER, PIELLI, SANCHEZ, GIRAL, McNEILL, KENYATTA, HILL-EVANS, GUENST, PROKOPIAK, CERRATO, PARKER, NEILSON, DONAHUE, BRENNAN, D. WILLIAMS, MADDEN, MALAGARI, BOROWSKI, MAYES, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, WEBSTER AND FREEMAN, APRIL 4, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HOUSING AND COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT, APRIL 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of November 10, 1999 (P.L.491, No.45), entitled "An act establishing a uniform construction code; imposing powers and duties on municipalities and the Department of Labor and Industry; providing for enforcement; imposing penalties; and making repeals," in training and certification of inspectors, providing for residential building inspectors.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of November 10, 1999 (P.L.491, No.45), known as the Pennsylvania Construction Code Act, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 704. Residential building inspectors.In addition to any other requirements from the department, a residential building inspector shall also complete five hours of continuing education in recognizing faulty construction practices, including proper installation of stucco, the laws and legal liability of builders in faulty construction practices and the remedies available under Federal or State law for homeowners 123456789101112131415161718 in addressing faulty construction practices.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1149PN1270 - 2 - 12
