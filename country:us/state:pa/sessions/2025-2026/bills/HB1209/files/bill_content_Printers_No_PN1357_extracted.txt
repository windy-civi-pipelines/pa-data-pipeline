2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1209 PN 1357 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1357 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1209 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ABNEY, WAXMAN, HILL-EVANS, GIRAL, PROBST, MADDEN, PIELLI, SANCHEZ, MAYES, KAZEEM, KENYATTA, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, RIVERA, HADDOCK, D. WILLIAMS, STEELE AND CIRESI, APRIL 15, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, APRIL 15, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in charter schools, providing for State certification for cyber charter school professional staff members.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 1743.1-A. State certification. Beginning on the effective date of this section, a cyber charter school's professional staff members must hold appropriate State certification, including appropriate State certification in special education if the professional staff member is responsible for providing special education services to students enrolled in the cyber charter school.12345678910111213141516171819 Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1209PN1357 - 2 - 1
