2025-2026 Regular Session HB 124 PN 0105 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 105 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.124 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. MACKENZIE, KAUFFMAN AND JAMES, JANUARY 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON INTERGOVERNMENTAL AFFAIRS AND OPERATIONS, JANUARY 16, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 25, 1982 (P.L.633, No.181), entitled "An act providing for independent oversight and review of regulations, creating an Independent Regulatory Review Commission, providing for its powers and duties and making repeals," further providing for proposed regulations and procedures for review.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 5(a) of the act of June 25, 1982 (P.L.633, No.181), known as the Regulatory Review Act, is amended by adding a paragraph to read:Section 5. Proposed regulations; procedures for review.(a) On the same date that an agency submits a proposed regulation to the Legislative Reference Bureau for publication of notice of proposed rulemaking in the Pennsylvania Bulletin as required by the Commonwealth Documents Law, the agency shall submit to the commission and the committees a copy of the proposed regulation and a regulatory analysis form which includes the following:* * *1234567891011121314151617181920 (15) If the proposed regulation accepts or adopts guidance from the World Health Organization, United Nations or the World Economic Forum, all of the following:(i) A statement demonstrating how the guidance from the World Health Organization, United Nations or the World Economic Forum applies to this Commonwealth.(ii) A description of any data stating that the guidance from the World Health Organization, United Nations or the World Economic Forum applies to this Commonwealth. The description shall include a detailed explanation of how the data was obtained and why the data is acceptable data. An agency advocating that any data is acceptable data shall have the burden of proving that the data is acceptable.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0124PN0105 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516
