Section 1721 of Title 51 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:ยง 1721. Veterans' Trust Fund.* * *(e.1) Grant Evaluation Committee.--(1) The Grant Evaluation Committee is established within the department.(2) The Grant Evaluation Committee shall consist of the following members:(i) Five members appointed by the Adjutant General, including the chairperson of the Pennsylvania State Veterans Commission or a designee, the chairperson of the 123456789101112131415161718 Pennsylvania War Veterans Council or a designee and a county director of veterans affairs.(ii) One veteran appointed by the President pro tempore of the Senate.(iii) One veteran appointed by the Minority Leader of the Senate.(iv) One veteran appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives.(v) One veteran appointed by the Minority Leader of the House of Representatives.(3) The Grant Evaluation Committee shall:(i) Review and evaluate applications for grants provided under this subchapter.(ii) Provide recommendations on the use of funds under this subchapter to the Adjutant General.(4) The review and evaluation of applications for grants provided under this subchapter and the making of recommendations for the use of funding under this subchapter performed by individuals pursuant to the statement of policy for the maintenance and use of the fund required under subsection (e) prior to the effective date of this subsection shall be performed by the Grant Evaluation Committee as provided under paragraph (3).* * *Section 2.
