2025-2026 Regular Session HR 265 PN 1963 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1963 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.265 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MALAGARI, SANCHEZ, KHAN, CERRATO, GIRAL, GREEN AND K.HARRIS, JUNE 18, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, JUNE 18, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating September 4, 2025, as "Taekwondo Grandmasters Appreciation Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Taekwondo is a Korean martial art that developed through thousands of years of Korean history; andWHEREAS, The origins of taekwondo are rooted in the ancient kingdom of Koguryo with the defense martial art known as "Subak" or "Taekkyon"; andWHEREAS, The word taekwondo is composed of three parts: "tae," meaning foot or to step on, "kwon," meaning fist or fight and "do," meaning the way or discipline; andWHEREAS, While both hands and feet are used in taekwondo, the sport is characterized by its unique combination of kick movements; andWHEREAS, In addition to sporting and self-defense skills, taekwondo also provides health benefits, including flexibility, stamina, strength and posture; andWHEREAS, Taekwondo also emphasizes mental discipline and 1234567891011121314151617 character through the taekwondo tenets of courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control and indomitable spirit; andWHEREAS, In 1973, the World Taekwondo Federation was founded as the official governing body over Taekwondo; andWHEREAS, In 2008, South Korea designated September 4 as "International Taekwondo Day" in recognition of the day Taekwondo became an Olympic Sport on September 4, 1994; andWHEREAS, Today, taekwondo is practiced worldwide by 80 million people of all ages and backgrounds; andWHEREAS, The title of "grandmaster" is reserved for the highest-ranking, most experienced and most respected taekwondo practitioners; andWHEREAS, Taekwondo and its grandmasters provide numerous physical and mental benefits to many Pennsylvanians across our Commonwealth; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate September 4, 2025, as "Taekwondo Grandmasters Appreciation Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0265PN1963 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718
