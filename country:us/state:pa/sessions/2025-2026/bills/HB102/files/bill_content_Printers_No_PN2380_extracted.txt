Section 2709(c) of Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended and subsection (f) is amended by adding a definition to read:§ 2709. Harassment.* * *(c) Grading.--(1) Except as provided under [paragraph (3)] paragraphs (3) and (4), an offense under subsection (a)(1), (2) or (3) shall constitute a summary offense.(2) An offense under subsection (a)(4), (5), (6) or (7) or (a.1) shall constitute a misdemeanor of the third degree.(3) The grading of an offense under subsection (a)(1), <--123456789101112131415161718 (2) or (3) shall be enhanced one degree if the person has previously violated an order issued under 23 Pa.C.S. § 6108 (relating to relief) involving the same victim, family or household member.(4) The grading of an offense under subsection (a)(1), (2) or (3) shall constitute a misdemeanor of the third degree where the victim is a sports official who was harassed as a result of official acts or status as a sports official.* * *(f) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection:* * *"Sports official." A person at a sporting event who enforces the rules of the event, including a person who supervises the participants. The term includes a coach, umpire, referee, trainer, team attendant, game manager, athletic director, assistant athletic director, president, dean, headmaster, principal and assistant principal of a school, college or university.Section 2. Title 18 is amended by adding a section to read:§ 2709.2. Harassment of sports official.(a) Offense defined.--A person who violates section 2709(a)(1), (2), (3) or (4) (relating to harassment), where the victim is a sports official who was harassed as a result of official acts or status as a sports official, is guilty of harassment of a sports official.(b) Grading.--An offense under subsection (a) shall constitute a misdemeanor of the third degree.(c) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following 20250HB0102PN2380 - 2 - <--123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Sports official." A person at a sporting event who enforces the rules of the event, such as an umpire or referee, or a person who supervises the participants, such as a coach. The term includes a trainer, team attendant, game manager, athletic director, assistant athletic director, president, dean, headmaster, principal and assistant principal of a school, college or university.Section 3 2.
