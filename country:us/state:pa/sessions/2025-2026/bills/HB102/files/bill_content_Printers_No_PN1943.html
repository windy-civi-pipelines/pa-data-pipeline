AN ACT
Amending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in assault, FURTHER PROVIDING FOR THE
OFFENSE OF HARASSMENT AND providing for the offense of
harassment of sports official.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:

Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:

"SPORTS OFFICIAL." A PERSON AT A SPORTING EVENT WHO ENFORCES

PARTICIPANTS. THE TERM INCLUDES A COACH, UMPIRE, REFEREE,
TRAINER, TEAM ATTENDANT, GAME MANAGER, ATHLETIC DIRECTOR,
ASSISTANT ATHLETIC DIRECTOR, PRESIDENT, DEAN, HEADMASTER,
PRINCIPAL AND ASSISTANT PRINCIPAL OF A SCHOOL, COLLEGE OR
UNIVERSITY.
SECTION 2. TITLE 18 IS AMENDED BY ADDING A SECTION TO READ:
§ 2709.2. Harassment of sports official.
(a) Offense defined.--A person who violates section 2709(a)
(1), (2), (3) or (4) (relating to harassment), where the victim
is a sports official who was harassed as a result of official
acts or status as a sports official, is guilty of harassment of
a sports official.

(c) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following
words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this
subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:
"Sports official." A person at a sporting event who enforces
the rules of the event, such as an umpire or referee, or a
person who supervises the participants, such as a coach. The
term includes a trainer, team attendant, game manager, athletic
director, assistant athletic director, president, dean,
headmaster, principal and assistant principal of a school,
college or university.
