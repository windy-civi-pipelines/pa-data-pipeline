AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles, further
providing for operation by persons under age sixteen.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 7725 of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:
7725. Operation by persons under age sixteen.
* * *
(c.1) Examination.--An examination for a safety certificate
under subsection (c)(3) shall be offered in person at no less
than one physical location in each county of this Commonwealth
as approved by the department.
* * *
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
