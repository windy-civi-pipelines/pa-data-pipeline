A JOINT RESOLUTION
Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the Commonwealth
of Pennsylvania, providing for right to medical freedom.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby resolves as follows:
Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution of
Pennsylvania is proposed in accordance with Article XI:
That Article I be amended by adding a section to read:
§ 30. Right to medical freedom.
The right of an individual to refuse any medical procedure,
treatment, injection, vaccine or prophylactic may not be
questioned or interfered with in any manner. Equality of rights
under the law shall not be denied or abridged to any person in
this Commonwealth because of the exercise of the right under
this section.
