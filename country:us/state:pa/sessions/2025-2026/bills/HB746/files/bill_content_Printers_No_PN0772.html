Section 3. The following shall apply:
(1) The amendment or addition of 30 Pa.C.S. § 301(a),
(d) and (g) shall apply to members of the Pennsylvania Fish
and Boat Commission who, beginning on the effective date of
this section, are starting a new term or are filling a
vacancy due to the death, resignation or removal from office
of a member.
(2) The amendment or addition of 34 Pa.C.S. § 301(a),
(d) and (e) shall apply to members of the Pennsylvania Game
Commission who, beginning on the effective date of this
section, are starting a new term or are filling a vacancy due
to the death, resignation or removal from office of a member.
Section 4. The Governor's Advisory Council for Hunting,
Fishing and Conservation established by Executive Order No.
2015-13, 4 Pa. Code Ch. 6 Subch. AAA, has no authority to select
and appoint commissioners of the Pennsylvania Game Commission or
Pennsylvania Fish and Game Commission.
Section 5. This act shall take effect as follows:
(1) The following provisions shall take effect
immediately:
The addition of 30 Pa.C.S. § 301(a)(3)(v).
The addition of 34 Pa.C.S. § 301(a)(3)(v).
(2) Sections 3 and 4 of this act shall take effect
immediately.
(3) This section shall take effect immediately.
(4) The remainder of this act shall take effect in one
year.
