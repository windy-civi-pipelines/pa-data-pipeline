2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1987 PN 2506 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 2506 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1987 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY RABB AND RIVERA, OCTOBER 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, OCTOBER 23, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in preliminary provisions, providing for limitation on school mascots.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 135. Limitation on School Mascots.--(a) A school entity may not have or adopt a name, symbol or image that depicts or refers to a Native American tribe, individual, custom or tradition for a mascot, nickname, logo or letterhead unless the school entity:(1) Has received the express written consent of a tax-exempt corporation in good standing founded prior to 1945 whose mission focuses exclusively on Native American rights.(2) C onducts a school-wide program on Native American 1234567891011121314151617181920 culture at least twice per school year.(3) O ffers a course of study to the school entity's students outlining Native American contributions to society.(4) F iles an annual report with the State Board of Education detailing what academic programs on Native American culture and history the school entity has offered during the school year.(b) A school entity that fails to comply with this section shall be ineligible to be a member of the Pennsylvania Interscholastic Athletic Association or participate in Pennsylvania Interscholastic Athletic Association sponsored athletic contests.(c) As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"School entity." Any of the following:(1) A school district. (2) An intermediate unit. (3) An area career and technical school. (4) A charter school, cyber charter school or regional charter school.(5) A private school.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1987PN2506 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
