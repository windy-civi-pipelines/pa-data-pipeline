2025-2026 Regular Session SR 63 PN 0577 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 577 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE RESOLUTION No.63 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MASTRIANO, APRIL 9, 2025 REFERRED TO INTERGOVERNMENTAL OPERATIONS, APRIL 9, 2025 A RESOLUTIONUrging each member of the Pennsylvania Congressional Delegation to commend and support the Department of Government Efficiency.WHEREAS, The Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) has made remarkable strides in enhancing government efficiency, curbing the waste of taxpayers' dollars and eliminating unnecessary overspending; andWHEREAS, Through focused efforts, DOGE has addressed critical inefficiencies by cutting through bureaucratic red tape and updating outdated processes; andWHEREAS, The most recent accomplishments of DOGE include saving an estimated $105 billion for taxpayers; andWHEREAS, This impressive figure results from initiatives such as fraud detection and elimination, the cancellation of unnecessary contracts and leases, the renegotiation of contracts and leases to achieve better terms, asset sales, grant cancellations, workforce reductions, programmatic changes and regulatory savings aimed at streamlining government operations; and12345678910111213141516171819 WHEREAS, While many seniors, disabled veterans and hardworking Pennsylvanians struggle to make ends meet, billions of dollars continue to flow to foreign countries for unwarranted projects that serve no public purpose; andWHEREAS, The illuminating work of DOGE has exposed these money laundering schemes and corruption, ensuring transparency and accountability in our government; andWHEREAS, Elon Musk's innovative approach to efficiency-leveraging automation, data-driven decisionmaking and reimagining legacy systems has not only revolutionized private industry, but has also delivered substantial savings for taxpayers; andWHEREAS, As the founder and CEO of Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink and The Boring Company, Mr. Musk has been a leader in disruptive innovations across various sectors, from electric vehicles to space exploration; andWHEREAS, It is essential now to recognize and harness Mr. Musk's insights for the public sector; andWHEREAS, By embracing technological advancements and incorporating them into public services, public officials can reduce costs, create new job opportunities and foster a government that is responsive, sustainable and forward-thinking; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the Senate of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania urge each member of the Pennsylvania Congressional Delegation to commend and support the Department of Government Efficiency;
