AN ACT
Amending the act of June 30, 2011 (P.L.81, No.18), entitled "An
act providing for the establishment of a searchable budget
database-driven Internet website detailing certain
information concerning taxpayer expenditures and
investments," further providing for searchable budget
database and for administration.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 3(g) of the act of June 30, 2011 (P.L.81,
No.18), known as the Pennsylvania Web Accountability and
Transparency (PennWATCH) Act, is amended and the section is
amended by adding a subsection to read:
Section 3. Searchable budget database.
* * *
(f.1) Settlements.--
(1) The website shall include information on each
settlement paid to an employee of a Commonwealth agency or
paid to an individual as a result of an action taken by an
employee of a Commonwealth agency for each claim under the

Employee Liability Self-Insurance Program, the Automobile
Liability Self-Insurance Program, the Motor License Tort
Claim Fund, notary bonds and the General Tort Claims Self-
Insurance Fund. The information shall include all of the
following:
(i) The Commonwealth agency that is the subject of
the claim.
(ii) The date when the claim was filed.
(iii) The legal basis for the settlement.
(iv) The date when the settlement was paid.
(v) The amount of the settlement.
(2) No later than 30 days after the date when a
settlement is paid, the office shall post the information
under paragraph (1) on the website.
(3) No later than 30 days after the effective date of
this paragraph and every 30 days thereafter, the Department
of General Services shall electronically transmit the
information under paragraph (1) regarding each settlement
paid in the previous 30 days to all the members of the
General Assembly.
(4) No later than 30 days after the effective date of
this paragraph, the Department of General Services shall
electronically transmit the information under paragraph (1)
regarding each settlement paid in the period beginning
January 1, 2025, and ending on the effective date of this
paragraph to all the members of the General Assembly.
