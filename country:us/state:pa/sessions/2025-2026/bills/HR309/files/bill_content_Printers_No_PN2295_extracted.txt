2025-2026 Regular Session HR 309 PN 2295 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 2295 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.309 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MATZIE, DEASY, ISAACSON, HARKINS, VITALI, CONKLIN, MERSKI, WARREN, SAPPEY, HOHENSTEIN, RIVERA, GUZMAN, HILL-EVANS, BELLMON, WAXMAN, SANCHEZ, PICKETT, COOPER AND ZIMMERMAN, SEPTEMBER 9, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE AND RURAL AFFAIRS, SEPTEMBER 10, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of October 2025 as "Wine, Wineries and Grapes Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Pennsylvania growers produce many of the finest food products available worldwide; andWHEREAS, Today, the growth of Pennsylvania grapes occurs thanks to the tireless efforts of generations of Pennsylvanians who played instrumental roles in the founding of this nation's great wine industry; andWHEREAS, The nation's first commercial grapevine nursery operated in Montgomery County in southeastern Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, With more than 300 wineries in this Commonwealth, Pennsylvania ranks seventh in the nation for the number of wineries and fourth for the amount of wine produced; andWHEREAS, Our wineries, grapes and grape juice provide an annual 1.5-billion-dollar economic impact to this Commonwealth; and12345678910111213141516 WHEREAS, Since 2010, Commonwealth wineries boasted more than 100 award-winning entries in several national and international wine competitions; andWHEREAS, In light of the many successes, the Pennsylvania Winery Association is proud to celebrate the industry during October's "Pennsylvania Wine Month"; andWHEREAS, This October, wine lovers can revel in the year's bountiful harvest as many of the wineries and trails throughout this Commonwealth are offering harvest events and celebrations; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of October 2025 as "Wine, Wineries and Grapes Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the significant and invaluable contributions made by the grape industry and the wine industry to this Commonwealth's history, economy and quality of life; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage the residents of this Commonwealth to celebrate "Wine, Wineries and Grapes Month" by enjoying the many grape products offered by Pennsylvania growers and responsibly partaking in favored Pennsylvania wine products, all while engaging in the festivities and gaining awareness of the services and products offered by Pennsylvania wineries throughout all seasons of the year.20250HR0309PN2295 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122232425
