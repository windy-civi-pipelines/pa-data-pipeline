2025-2026 Regular Session HB 472 PN 0455 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 455 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.472 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BIZZARRO, GIRAL, KHAN, SANCHEZ, HILL-EVANS, SCHLOSSBERG, FLEMING, HOHENSTEIN, DEASY, CERRATO AND GREEN, FEBRUARY 4, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, FEBRUARY 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in licensing of drivers, further providing for learners' permits, for application for driver's license or learner's permit, for application for driver's license or learner's permit by minor and for issuance and content of driver's license; and, in fees, further providing for driver's license and learner's permit.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 1505(g) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended and subsection (e) is amended by adding a paragraph to read:§ 1505. Learners' permits.* * *(e) Authorization to test for driver's license and junior driver's license.--A person with a learner's permit is authorized to take the examination for a regular or junior driver's license for the class of vehicle for which a permit is held. Before a person under the age of 18 years may take the examination for a junior driver's license, including a Class M 1234567891011121314151617181920 license to operate a motorcycle, the minor must:* * *(2.1) Present the certification form required by paragraph (2) signed by a teacher, social worker or children and youth caseworker if that person meets the definition of "homeless children and youths" under 42 U.S.C. Ch. 119 (relating to homeless assistance).* * *(g) Liability.--Submission of a certification under subsection (e)(2) shall not subject [the parent, guardian, person in loco parentis or spouse of a married minor to any liability based upon the certification.] any of the following to any liability based on the certification:(1) the parent, guardian, person in loco parentis or spouse of a married minor; or(2) teacher, social worker or children and youth caseworker of any minor who meets the definition of "homeless children and youths" under 42 U.S.C. Ch. 119 (relating to homeless assistance).* * *Section 2. Section 1506 of Title 75 is amended by adding a subsection to read:§ 1506. Application for driver's license or learner's permit.* * *(c) Homeless children and youth.--A person who meets the definition of "homeless children and youths" under 42 U.S.C. Ch. 119 (relating to homeless assistance) shall be permitted to use a temporary address, including the address of a school, foster home or shelter, for any application under this section.Section 3. Section 1507(a) of Title 75 is amended to read:20250HB0472PN0455 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 § 1507. Application for driver's license or learner's permit by minor.(a) Signature of parent or guardian.--The application of any person under the age of 18 years for a learner's permit or driver's license shall also be signed by the father, mother, guardian or person in loco parentis which signature shall be verified before a person authorized to administer oaths or before an authorized department employee. In addition to the requirements of this subsection, the signature of a teacher, social worker or children and youth caseworker shall be accepted for a person who meets the definition of "homeless children and youths" under 42 U.S.C. Ch. 119 (relating to homeless assistance).* * *Section 4. Sections 1510 and 1951 of Title 75 are amended by adding subsections to read:§ 1510. Ch. 119 (relating to homeless assistance) shall not be required to pay a fee to obtain a driver's license or identification card.Section 5.
