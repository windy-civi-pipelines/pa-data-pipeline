AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, IN GENERAL PROVISIONS, FURTHER PROVIDING FOR
DEFINITIONS; in licensing of drivers, further providing for
learners' permits, for application for driver's license or
learner's permit, for application for driver's license or
learner's permit by minor and for issuance and content of
driver's license; and, in fees, further providing for
driver's license and learner's permit.

hereby enacts as follows:

Section 2 3. Section 1506 of Title 75 is amended by adding a
subsection to read:
1506. Application for driver's license or learner's permit.
* * *
(c) Homeless children and youth.--A person who meets the
definition of "homeless children and youths" under 42 U.S.C. Ch.
119 (relating to homeless assistance) MINOR EXPERIENCING
HOMELESSNESS shall be permitted to use a temporary address,
including the address of a school, foster home or shelter, for
any application under this section.
