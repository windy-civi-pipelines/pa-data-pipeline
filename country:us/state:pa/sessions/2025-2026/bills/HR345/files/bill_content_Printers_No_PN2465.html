A RESOLUTION
Designating the month of July 2026 as "Culinary Arts Month" in
Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Pennsylvania's culinary education programs, career
and technical education centers and community colleges equip
students with essential skills for careers in food service,
hospitality and culinary innovation and prepare them for
postsecondary education and a globally competitive workforce;
and
WHEREAS, Pennsylvania has a rich culinary heritage, including
restaurants, wineries and food producers, whose creativity,
innovation and entrepreneurship strengthen local economies,
tourism and community engagement; and
WHEREAS, Observed since 2002 and recognized annually in the
month of July, "National Culinary Arts Month" celebrates the
skill, creativity and dedication of culinary professionals, from
chefs and bakers to educators and food industry leaders; and
WHEREAS, Designating "Culinary Arts Month" in Pennsylvania
