2025-2026 Regular Session HR 345 PN 2465 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 2465 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.345 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY GUZMAN, HOHENSTEIN, FREEMAN, NEILSON, HOWARD, SANCHEZ, BELLMON, CIRESI, RIVERA, D. WILLIAMS, MAYES AND FRANKEL, OCTOBER 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LABOR AND INDUSTRY, OCTOBER 16, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of July 2026 as "Culinary Arts Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Pennsylvania's culinary education programs, career and technical education centers and community colleges equip students with essential skills for careers in food service, hospitality and culinary innovation and prepare them for postsecondary education and a globally competitive workforce; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania has a rich culinary heritage, including restaurants, wineries and food producers, whose creativity, innovation and entrepreneurship strengthen local economies, tourism and community engagement; andWHEREAS, Observed since 2002 and recognized annually in the month of July, "National Culinary Arts Month" celebrates the skill, creativity and dedication of culinary professionals, from chefs and bakers to educators and food industry leaders; andWHEREAS, Designating "Culinary Arts Month" in Pennsylvania 1234567891011121314151617 encourages students, educators and communities to explore culinary arts, participate in educational programs, support local food businesses and develop skills that contribute to workforce readiness, economic growth and the cultural richness of this Commonwealth; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of July 2026 as "Culinary Arts Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That during "Culinary Arts Month," the residents of this Commonwealth be encouraged to celebrate chefs, culinary professionals, students and educational programs across this Commonwealth; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That schools, career and technical education centers, community colleges, culinary associations and local food businesses be encouraged to participate in educational and celebratory activities throughout the month.20250HR0345PN2465 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516
