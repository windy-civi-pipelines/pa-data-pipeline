2025-2026 Regular Session HR 23 PN 0270 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 270 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.23 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY PIELLI, GIRAL, HILL-EVANS, KHAN, SANCHEZ AND CERRATO, JANUARY 23, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON STATE GOVERNMENT, JANUARY 23, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDirecting the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee to study and make recommendations on implementing location-based pay for State government employees.WHEREAS, Government wages must remain competitive to attract the best talent into public service; andWHEREAS, Cost of living varies across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, The Federal Government, California and New York already adjust salaries based on where government employees work; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives direct the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee to conduct a study and make recommendations on implementing location-based pay for State government employees; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the study include:(1) information on the cost of living by county and metropolitan area across this Commonwealth;(2) the impact that uniform Statewide wages have on the hiring and retention of employees;123456789101112131415161718 (3) different methods of implementing location-based pay in this Commonwealth, including by metropolitan area, by county and by other geographic areas if useful;(4) the fiscal impact of implementing location-based pay based on the latest wage levels; and(5) recommendations on implementing location-based pay in this Commonwealth for State government employees;and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee use other studies and existing resources that may assist in completing the study; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the Legislative Budget and Finance Committee issue a report of its findings and recommendations to the House of Representatives no later than one year from the adoption of this resolution.20250HR0023PN0270 - 2 - 123456789101112131415
