2025-2026 Regular Session HR 306 PN 2265 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2265 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.306 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MATZIE, MAYES, FREEMAN, HOHENSTEIN, WAXMAN, RIVERA, MERSKI, HILL-EVANS, PARKER, GALLAGHER, HADDOCK, SANCHEZ, NEILSON, GREINER, PICKETT AND DELOZIER, SEPTEMBER 3, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON COMMERCE, SEPTEMBER 4, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the week of October 12 through 18, 2025, as "Credit Union Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Credit unions are individual, independent financial cooperatives founded by individuals working to achieve economic advancement with individuals seeking to improve their financial future; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania and New Jersey have nearly 500 credit unions, serving approximately 5.5 million members; andWHEREAS, Founded on the philosophy of "not for profit, not for charity, but for service," credit unions maintain a focus on service to their members; andWHEREAS, Pooling personal resources and leadership abilities for the good of the cooperative, credit unions provide low-cost financial services to their members; andWHEREAS, These institutions strive to educate their members to use credit wisely while encouraging the development of savings programs; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Acting as sponsors for activities to promote the charitable acts of their members, credit unions encourage community involvement and participation for the betterment of all; andWHEREAS, Through the efforts of volunteers organized by credit unions, many worthwhile causes have benefited throughout this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Credit unions are receiving worldwide recognition during the commemoration of the 77th anniversary of "International Credit Union Day" on October 16; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the week of October 12 through 18, 2025, as "Credit Union Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be transmitted to the CrossState Credit Union Association, 4309 North Front Street, Harrisburg, PA 17110.20250HR0306PN2265 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516
