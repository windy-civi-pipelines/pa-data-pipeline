AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for
special plates for current members of the armed forces of the
United States.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 1364.1(b) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:
§ 1364.1. Special plates for current members of the armed
forces of the United States.
* * *
(b) Eligibility.--A plate issued under subsection (a) shall
be for members of the Pennsylvania National Guard and the
following branches of the armed forces of the United States:
(1) Army.
(2) Navy.
(3) Air Force.
(4) Marine Corps.
(5) Coast Guard.
