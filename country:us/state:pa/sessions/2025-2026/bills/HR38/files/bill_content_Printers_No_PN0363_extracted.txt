2025-2026 Regular Session HR 38 PN 0363 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 363 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.38 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ORTITAY, VENKAT, FREEMAN, JAMES, STENDER, MERSKI, CERRATO, CIRESI, KHAN, CONKLIN, NEILSON, REICHARD, MENTZER, ZIMMERMAN, SHUSTERMAN AND DALEY, JANUARY 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, JANUARY 28, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the week of April 6 through 12, 2025, as "National Library Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The first "National Library Week" was observed in 1958 with the theme "Wake up and Read!"; andWHEREAS, In the mid-1950s, research showed that Americans were spending less money on books and more on radios, televisions and musical instruments; andWHEREAS, Due to this shift, the American Library Association and the American Book Publishers formed an organization in 1954 called the National Book Committee; andWHEREAS, The committee's goals were aimed at encouraging people to read and the individuals involved developed a plan that led to the first "National Library Week"; andWHEREAS, "National Library Week" is an annual celebration that highlights the valuable role of libraries, librarians and library workers; andWHEREAS, Libraries across the United States observe and 1234567891011121314151617 celebrate this week dedicated to libraries and the impact they have; andWHEREAS, Libraries and the individuals who work there are dedicated to working on transforming lives through the power of reading and strengthening the communities they serve; andWHEREAS, In addition to providing books to the community, libraries also provide technology such as computers that can be used for research; andWHEREAS, Libraries in Pennsylvania serve millions of visitors and conduct programs for children, parents, entrepreneurs, senior citizens and students; andWHEREAS, Librarians and library workers vigorously work to ensure that libraries across this Commonwealth are well equipped to promote learning in their communities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the week of April 6 through 12, 2025, as "National Library Week" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0038PN0363 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617
