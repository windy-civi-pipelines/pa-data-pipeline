A RESOLUTION
Recognizing the week of April 6 through 12, 2025, as "National
Library Week" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, The first "National Library Week" was observed in
1958 with the theme "Wake up and Read!"; and
WHEREAS, In the mid-1950s, research showed that Americans
were spending less money on books and more on radios,
televisions and musical instruments; and
WHEREAS, Due to this shift, the American Library Association
and the American Book Publishers formed an organization in 1954
called the National Book Committee; and
WHEREAS, The committee's goals were aimed at encouraging
people to read and the individuals involved developed a plan
that led to the first "National Library Week"; and
WHEREAS, "National Library Week" is an annual celebration
that highlights the valuable role of libraries, librarians and
library workers; and
WHEREAS, Libraries across the United States observe and

WHEREAS, Libraries and the individuals who work there are

WHEREAS, Libraries in Pennsylvania serve millions of visitors

senior citizens and students; and
WHEREAS, Librarians and library workers vigorously work to

week of April 6 through 12, 2025, as "National Library Week" in
Pennsylvania.
