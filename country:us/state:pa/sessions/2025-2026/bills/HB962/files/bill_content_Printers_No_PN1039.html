AN ACT
Amending the act of December 19, 1988 (P.L.1262, No.156),
entitled "An act providing for the licensing of eligible
organizations to conduct games of chance, for the licensing
of persons to distribute games of chance, for the
registration of manufacturers of games of chance, and for
suspensions and revocations of licenses and permits;
requiring records; providing for local referendum by
electorate; and prescribing penalties," in games of chance,
providing for games of chance operations.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The act of December 19, 1988 (P.L.1262, No.156),
known as the Local Option Small Games of Chance Act, is amended
by adding a section to read:
Section 302.1. Games of chance operations.
(a) Raffles and drawings.--Virtual sales of games of chance
shall be conducted as follows:
(1) An eligible organization may accept electronic
payment via credit card, debit card, mobile payment service
or other electronic payment method in accordance with
subsection (b).

(2) An eligible organization may only use an Internet-
based conferencing software application in the conduct of the
game of chance limited to the reveal of the winner of the
game of chance.
(3) The reveal of the winner of the game of chance shall
not use or display animation in identifying the winner.
(4) The reveal of the winner of the game of chance shall
not use or display moving or still images in identifying the
winner.
(5) The reveal of the winner of the game of chance shall
not use, make or display a graphic presentation in
identifying the winner.
(6) The reveal of the winner of the game of chance shall
not use or be completed with the aid of a slot machine,
hybrid slot machine or skill slot machine as defined in 4
Pa.C.S. 1103 (relating to definitions).
(7) The reveal of the winner of the game of chance shall
not use or be completed with the aid of a video gaming
terminal as defined in 4 Pa.C.S. 3102 (relating to
definitions).
(8) The reveal of the winner of the game of chance shall
not use or be completed with the aid of an electronic video
monitor or a simulated gambling program as defined in 18
Pa.C.S. 5513(f) (relating to g ambling devices, gambling,
etc.).

payment service under subsection (a)(1) for the conduct or
operation of the game of chance, an eligible organization shall
take all of the following actions:
(1) Employ a mechanism to detect the physical location
