2025-2026 Regular Session HR 329 PN 2359 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2359 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.329 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HILL-EVANS, FREEMAN, GUZMAN, BELLMON, VENKAT, PROBST, RIVERA, GUENST, KHAN, JAMES, HADDOCK, GALLAGHER, HOWARD, CURRY, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, D. WILLIAMS, HOHENSTEIN, NEILSON, FRANKEL, SANCHEZ AND CIRESI, SEPTEMBER 29, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, SEPTEMBER 29, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of October 2025 as "Arts and Culture Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Art and culture embody much of the accumulated wisdom, intellect and imagination of humankind; andWHEREAS, Art and culture enhance and enrich the lives of every Pennsylvanian and make the United States a more prosperous nation; andWHEREAS, Cities and states, through their local and state agencies representing thousands of cultural organizations, have recognized and celebrated the value and importance of art and culture in the lives of Americans; andWHEREAS, The arts can evoke emotions and foster connections with others, providing comfort, inspiration and a sense of belonging across cultures and communities; andWHEREAS, Diverse art forms broaden perspectives and cultivate empathy by helping us understand different viewpoints and 12345678910111213141516 experiences, fostering a deeper understanding of the complexities of our changing world; andWHEREAS, The arts and culture industry strengthens our economy by generating $1.2 trillion in total economic activity annually, representing 4.2% of the nation's Gross Domestic Product and by supporting the full-time equivalent of 5.4 million jobs; andWHEREAS, Art and culture have been celebrated throughout the month of October in the United States for decades; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of October 2025 as "Arts and Culture Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives reaffirm its support for art and culture and observe this month with appropriate programs, ceremonies and celebrations; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives call upon the residents of this Commonwealth to celebrate and honor the artists and scholars who lift us up, speak to our souls and shape our communities across this Commonwealth and the nation.20250HR0329PN2359 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
