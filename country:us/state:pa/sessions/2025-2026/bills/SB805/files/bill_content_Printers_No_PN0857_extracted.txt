2025-2026 Regular Session SB 805 PN 0857 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 857 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE BILL No.805 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BOSCOLA, FONTANA AND HAYWOOD, MAY 30, 2025 REFERRED TO JUDICIARY, MAY 30, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in other offenses, providing for sky lantern tethering; and imposing penalties.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 7518. Sky lantern tethering.(a) Offenses defined.--A person commits an offense if the person:(1) intentionally releases a sky lantern, aerial luminary lantern or similar floating device outdoors unless the sky lantern, aerial luminary lantern or similar floating device is tethered by a weight or other fixed structure connected to the ground; or(2) tethers a sky lantern, aerial luminary lantern or similar floating device and intentionally leaves the sky lantern, aerial luminary lantern or similar floating device unattended.12345678910111213141516171819 (b) Penalties.--(1) A violation of subsection (a)(1) is a summary offense punishable by a fine of not less than $500.(2) A violation of subsection (a)(2) in which the sky lantern, aerial luminary lantern or similar floating device is released outdoors beyond the control of the person who tethered it is a summary offense punishable by a fine of not less than $1,500.(c) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Aerial luminary lantern." A sky lantern."Municipality." A county, city, borough, incorporated town or township."Person." As follows:(1) An individual, corporation, partnership, limited liability company, business trust, association, firm, estate, trust, foundation, municipality or other business or governmental entity.(2) The term does not include the Commonwealth." Sky lantern." A small hot air balloon made of paper, or similar material, with an opening at the bottom where a small fire is suspended, which causes the lantern to rise in the air.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250SB0805PN0857 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324
