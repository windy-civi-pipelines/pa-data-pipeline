AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in registration of vehicles, further providing for
vehicles exempt from registration.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 1302(23) of Title 75 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended and the section is amended by
adding a paragraph to read:
§ 1302. Vehicles exempt from registration.
The following types of vehicles are exempt from registration:
* * *
(23) Any all-terrain vehicle exempt from registration
under Chapter 77 (relating to snowmobiles and all-terrain
vehicles) and operated as an emergency vehicle by a police or
fire department or emergency medical services agency when the
vehicle does not travel on public highways in excess of two
miles and is primarily for off-highway use and only operated
incidentally upon [the highway] a highway in a county of the

(24) Any all-terrain vehicle exempt from registration
under Chapter 77 and operated as an emergency vehicle by a
police or fire department or emergency medical services

excess of 15 miles and is primarily for off-highway use and

sixth class, a county of the seventh class or a county of the
eighth class. An all-terrain vehicle so operated shall comply
with section 4571.
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
