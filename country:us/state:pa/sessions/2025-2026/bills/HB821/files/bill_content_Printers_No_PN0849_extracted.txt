2025-2026 Regular Session HB 821 PN 0849 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 849 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.821 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KHAN, MARCELL, PIELLI, HILL-EVANS, CONKLIN, SANCHEZ, NEILSON, DONAHUE, CIRESI, SHUSTERMAN, BOROWSKI, FREEMAN, FLEMING, OTTEN, GREEN AND HOGAN, MARCH 6, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, MARCH 6, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), entitled "An act to consolidate, editorially revise, and codify the public welfare laws of the Commonwealth," in departmental powers and duties as to licensing, further providing for regulations.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 1021(a) of the act of June 13, 1967 (P.L.31, No.21), known as the Human Services Code, is amended by adding a paragraph to read:Section 1021. Regulations.--(a) * * *(3) Within sixty days of the effective date of this paragraph, the department shall take all the necessary actions, including the submission of required documents to the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services of the United States Department of Health and Human Services , to include assisted living services and supplemental health care services as covered medical assistance services in the Community HealthChoices program and to allow licensed assisted living residences to be 123456789101112131415161718 certified as medical assistance providers for these services.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0821PN0849 - 2 - 123
