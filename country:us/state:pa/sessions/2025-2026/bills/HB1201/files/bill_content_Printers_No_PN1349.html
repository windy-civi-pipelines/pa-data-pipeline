AN ACT
Amending the act of July 2, 1935 (P.L.589, No.210), entitled "An
act to safeguard human health and life by providing for the
issuance of permits to, and regulation of persons and
entities selling milk and milk products; conferring powers,
and imposing duties on the Secretary of Agriculture; and
otherwise providing for the administration of the act; and
imposing penalties," further providing for raw milk and
handling; and abrogating a regulation.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 8 of the act of July 2, 1935 (P.L.589,
No.210), referred to as the Milk Sanitation Law, is amended by
adding a subsection to read:
Section 8. "Raw Milk" shall be produced and handled in the
following manner:
* * *
(f) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the
contrary, the "secretary" shall allow every holder of a permit
to sell raw milk to sell milk products containing "Raw Milk" and
manufactured dairy products containing "Raw Milk."
