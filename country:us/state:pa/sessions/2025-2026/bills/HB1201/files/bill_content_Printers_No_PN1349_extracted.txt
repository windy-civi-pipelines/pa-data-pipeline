2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1201 PN 1349 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1349 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1201 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ZIMMERMAN, HAMM, GREINER, STAMBAUGH, KAUFFMAN, KRUPA, GLEIM, JAMES, ROWE AND T. JONES, APRIL 15, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE AND RURAL AFFAIRS, APRIL 15, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of July 2, 1935 (P.L.589, No.210), entitled "An act to safeguard human health and life by providing for the issuance of permits to, and regulation of persons and entities selling milk and milk products; conferring powers, and imposing duties on the Secretary of Agriculture; and otherwise providing for the administration of the act; and imposing penalties," further providing for raw milk and handling; and abrogating a regulation.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 8 of the act of July 2, 1935 (P.L.589, No.210), referred to as the Milk Sanitation Law, is amended by adding a subsection to read:Section 8. "Raw Milk" shall be produced and handled in the following manner:* * *(f) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the "secretary" shall allow every holder of a permit to sell raw milk to sell milk products containing "Raw Milk" and manufactured dairy products containing "Raw Milk."1234567891011121314151617181920 Section 2. The provisions of 7 Pa. Code ยง 59a.402(b) are abrogated insofar as they are inconsistent with the addition of section 8(f) of the act.Section 3. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1201PN1349 - 2 - 1234
