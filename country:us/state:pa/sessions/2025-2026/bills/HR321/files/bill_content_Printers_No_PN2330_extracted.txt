2025-2026 Regular Session HR 321 PN 2330 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2330 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.321 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SCOTT, BRIGGS, HANBIDGE, MALAGARI, N. NELSON, SANCHEZ, WEBSTER, KHAN, GUENST, HILL-EVANS AND GALLAGHER, SEPTEMBER 19, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, SEPTEMBER 23, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating October 3, 2025, as "Sharon Cullen Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, On October 3, 1965, Sharon L. Cullen was born in Philadelphia to Max and Florence Stein; andWHEREAS, Mrs. Cullen was a proud graduate of North Penn High School, Class of 1983; andWHEREAS, Mrs. Cullen devoted her professional career to public service in the Montgomery County District Attorney's Office and the Montgomery County Commissioners' Office and finally as the Montgomery County Director of Parking Facilities and Fleet Management; andWHEREAS, While working for Montgomery County, Mrs. Cullen met her husband, Sean Cullen; andWHEREAS, The Cullens' passion for helping others led them to found the Montgomery County Hero Fund in 2012; andWHEREAS, The Montgomery County Hero Fund aids and supports Military Personnel, Law Enforcement, Firefighters, First 1234567891011121314151617 Responders and their families, in times of tragedy; andWHEREAS, The Hero Fund is unique in that the members of its board of directors, which Mrs. Cullen served on, are all volunteers, and the fund guarantees 100% of all money raised is given to families in need; andWHEREAS, Mrs. Cullen was the loving owner of two huskies, Sasha Bear and Mishka Bear, and one cat, Justice; andWHEREAS, Mrs. Cullen passed away from cancer on July 29, 2025, at the age of 59; andWHEREAS, Mrs. Cullen was a kind, selfless and loving person, who positively impacted all those she came into contact with and who made her community a better place; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate October 3, 2025, as "Sharon Cullen Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0321PN2330 - 2 - 1234567891011121314
