AN ACT
Amending Title 34 (Game) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in hunting and furtaking licenses, further
providing for resident license and fee exemptions.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 2706 of Title 34 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a subsection to read:
§ 2706. Resident license and fee exemptions.
* * *
(i) College students.--
(1) A college student who attends a college or
university located within this Commonwealth shall be eligible
for a resident hunting license if the college student:
(i) Has applied to an issuing agent or the
commission.
(ii) Provides a valid college identification card as
proof of enrollment.
(iii) Pays the issuing agent or the commission a
license fee and issuing agent fee as provided in section
