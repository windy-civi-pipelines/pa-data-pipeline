A RESOLUTION
Designating the month of May 2025 as "Month of May (MOM) Mom
Month" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Mothers are nurturers, providers, supporters and
caregivers; and
WHEREAS, Mothers are vital to the fabric of this nation and
the story of motherhood; and
WHEREAS, Mothers have a significant role in raising the next
generation and deserve to be celebrated for the special ability
they have to bring life into this world; and
WHEREAS, The significance of mothers in our society is such
that the second Sunday in May each year is designated as
Mother's Day; and
WHEREAS, It is important to recognize the sacrifices and
contributions of the mothers in our lives, reflect on the
meaning of motherhood and consider the importance of maternal
bonds in our community; and
WHEREAS, The role of mothers in society can be
underappreciated and overlooked; and

WHEREAS, While more women are part of the workforce, their
responsibilities as mothers do not end; and
WHEREAS, Mothers may neglect their own health and well-being
to ensure that their families are well taken care of; and
WHEREAS, It is important to remind mothers to take time for
themselves; therefore be it
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the
month of May 2025 as the "Month of May (MOM) Mom Month" in
Pennsylvania; and be it further
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the
important role that mothers have in society; and be it further
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage mothers
to focus on themselves and their health.
