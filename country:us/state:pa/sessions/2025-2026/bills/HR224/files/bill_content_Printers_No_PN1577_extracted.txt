BROWN, PICKETT, KAZEEM, K.HARRIS, VENKAT, COOK, M. MACKENZIE, MENTZER AND SCHMITT, MAY 2, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON CHILDREN AND YOUTH, MAY 2, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of May 2025 as "Month of May (MOM) Mom Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Mothers are nurturers, providers, supporters and caregivers; andWHEREAS, Mothers are vital to the fabric of this nation and the story of motherhood; andWHEREAS, Mothers have a significant role in raising the next generation and deserve to be celebrated for the special ability they have to bring life into this world; andWHEREAS, The significance of mothers in our society is such that the second Sunday in May each year is designated as Mother's Day; andWHEREAS, It is important to recognize the sacrifices and contributions of the mothers in our lives, reflect on the meaning of motherhood and consider the importance of maternal bonds in our community; andWHEREAS, The role of mothers in society can be underappreciated and overlooked; and123456789101112131415161718 WHEREAS, While more women are part of the workforce, their responsibilities as mothers do not end; andWHEREAS, Mothers may neglect their own health and well-being to ensure that their families are well taken care of; andWHEREAS, It is important to remind mothers to take time for themselves; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of May 2025 as the "Month of May (MOM) Mom Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the important role that mothers have in society; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage mothers to focus on themselves and their health.20250HR0224PN1577 - 2 - 12345678910111213
