2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1515 PN 1771 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1771 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1515 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY TOMLINSON AND GUENST, MAY 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HOUSING AND COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT, MAY 29, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of December 7, 1982 (P.L.784, No.225), entitled "An act relating to dogs, regulating the keeping of dogs; providing for the licensing of dogs and kennels; providing for the protection of dogs and the detention and destruction of dogs in certain cases; regulating the sale and transportation of dogs; declaring dogs to be personal property and the subject of theft; providing for the abandonment of animals; providing for the assessment of damages done to animals; providing for payment of damages by the Commonwealth in certain cases and the liability of the owner or keeper of dogs for such damages; imposing powers and duties on certain State and local officers and employees; providing penalties; and creating a Dog Law Restricted Account," providing for insurance.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of December 7, 1982 (P.L.784, No.225), known as the Dog Law, is amended by adding an article to read:ARTICLE I-AADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURESSection 101-A. Insurance.(a) Prohibited conduct.--Subject to subsection (b), an insurer may not, based solely upon an insured's harboring or owning of a dog of a specific breed or mixture of breeds:123456789101112131415161718192021222324 (1) Refuse to issue or renew a homeowner's insurance policy of the insured.(2) Cancel a homeowner's insurance policy of the insured.(3) Charge or impose an increased premium or rate for a homeowner's insurance policy of the insured.(b) Construction.--Subsection (a) shall not be construed to prohibit an insurer from refusing to issue or renew a homeowner's insurance policy, canceling a homeowner's insurance policy or charging or imposing an increased premium or rate for a homeowner's insurance policy based upon the insured's harboring or owning of a dog of any breed or mixture of breeds that has been determined to be a dangerous dog in accordance with section 502-A.(c) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Homeowner's insurance policy." A policy, contract or plan of casualty insurance that provides property and liability coverage to an insured."Insurer." A person licensed by the Insurance Department to provide a homeowner's insurance policy within this Commonwealth in accordance with the act of May 17, 1921 (P.L.682, No.284), known as The Insurance Company Law of 1921.Section 2. The addition of section 101-A of the act shall apply to homeowner's insurance policies issued, renewed or modified on or after the effective date of this section.Section 3. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1515PN1771 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122232425262728
