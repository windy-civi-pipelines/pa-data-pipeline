2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1709 PN 2102 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2102 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1709 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SMITH-WADE-EL, SANCHEZ, FREEMAN, HILL-EVANS, WAXMAN, K.HARRIS, HOHENSTEIN, BOYD, D. WILLIAMS, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ AND RIVERA, JULY 8, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, JULY 9, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in miscellaneous provisions relating to institutions of higher education, providing for employment certification for adjunct and part-time faculty.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 2005-I. Employment certification for adjunct and part-time faculty.(a) Lecture or classroom time. --A public institution of higher education shall, in completing the employer portion of the employment certification form, credit an adjunct or part-time faculty member with at least 3.35 hours worked for each hour of lecture or classroom time.(b) Noninstructional faculty.--In completing the employer 1234567891011121314151617181920 portion of the employment certification form for an adjunct or part-time faculty member with noninstructional assignments, a public institution of higher education shall credit the employee hour for hour with no adjustment factor.(c) Exemption.--The adjustment factor specified under subsection (a) may not supersede a higher adjustment factor established by a public institution of higher education through a collective bargaining agreement or employer policy in recognition of the amount of out-of-class work that is associated with instruction, including performance of office hours.(d) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Employment certification form." The form used by the United States Department of Education to certify an individual's employment at a public service organization for the purposes of the Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program."Public institution of higher education." Any of the following:(1) A community college operating under Article XIX-A. (2) A rural regional college established under Article XIX-G.(3) A university within the State System of Higher Education under Article XX-A.(4) A State-related institution as defined in section 2001-C.(5) The Thaddeus Stevens College of Technology.(6) The Pennsylvania College of Technology. "Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program." The Public 20250HB1709PN2102 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 Service Loan Forgiveness Program established under 34 CFR 685.219 (relating to Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program (PSLF)).Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB1709PN2102 - 3 - 1234
