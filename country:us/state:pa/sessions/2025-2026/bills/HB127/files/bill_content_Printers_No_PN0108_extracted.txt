2025-2026 Regular Session HB 127 PN 0108 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 108 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.127 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY DIAMOND, JAMES, M. MACKENZIE, KAUFFMAN AND LEADBETER, JANUARY 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON COMMERCE, JANUARY 16, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of December 17, 1968 (P.L.1224, No.387), entitled "An act prohibiting unfair methods of competition and unfair or deceptive acts or practices in the conduct of any trade or commerce, giving the Attorney General and District Attorneys certain powers and duties and providing penalties," further providing for definitions.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 2(4) of the act of December 17, 1968 (P.L.1224, No.387), known as the Unfair Trade Practices and Consumer Protection Law, is amended by adding a subclause to read:Section 2. Definitions.--As used in this act.* * *(4) "Unfair methods of competition" and "unfair or deceptive acts or practices" mean any one or more of the following:* * *(xx.1) Refusing to accept cash payment for the retail sale of goods or services having a sale price of less than five hundred dollars ($500) when the purchaser is physically present 1234567891011121314151617181920 at the point of sale. This subclause does not apply to the retail sale of goods or services where the seller has a written policy that currency in excess of a certain denomination will not be accepted as payment and has posted the policy conspicuously for purchasers to view prior to the point of sale and the purchaser tenders cash payment in a denomination that exceeds the amount specified in the policy.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0127PN0108 - 2 - 123456789
