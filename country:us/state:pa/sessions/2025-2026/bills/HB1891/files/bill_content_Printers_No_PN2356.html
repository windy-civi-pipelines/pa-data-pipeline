AN ACT
Amending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in firearms and other dangerous
articles, further providing for sale or transfer of firearms.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 6111(b)(1), (1.1)(i) and (6) and (f)(2)
of Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes are
amended and the section is amended by adding a subsection to
read:
§ 6111. Sale or transfer of firearms.

(k) Electronic reporting exemption.--A licensed importer,
licensed manufacturer or licensed dealer who conducts 20 or
fewer firearm sales and transfers per year shall not be required
to use an electronic application/record of sale. The following
shall apply:
(1) Beginning in 2027, the Pennsylvania State Police
shall conduct an annual review of the transactions of each
licensed importer, licensed manufacturer and licensed dealer
over the past calendar year to determine whether the licensed
importer, licensed manufacturer or licensed dealer is
exempted from the use of an electronic application/record of
sale. The Pennsylvania State Police shall notify each
licensed importer, licensed manufacturer and licensed dealer
of an exemption to the use of an electronic
application/record of sale no later than April 1 of each
year.
