2025-2026 Regular Session HR 342 PN 2442 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 2442 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.342 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HILL-EVANS, GIRAL, PROBST, FREEMAN, SANCHEZ, BRENNAN, KHAN, NEILSON, GALLAGHER, M. JONES, BELLMON AND RIVERA, OCTOBER 9, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON STATE GOVERNMENT, OCTOBER 9, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating November 15, 2025, as "Articles of Confederation Day" in Pennsylvania; and recognizing the City of York, York County, for its important contribution to the forging of our nation.WHEREAS, The Continental Congress, in flight from British troops advancing on Philadelphia, chose the City of York, York County, as its refuge and met there from September 30, 1777, to June 27, 1778, to direct national affairs during a critical period in the American Revolution; andWHEREAS, The Continental Congress adopted the Articles of Confederation while in session in the York County Courthouse on November 15, 1777, establishing the first constitution that formally united the 13 colonies as one nation; andWHEREAS, The Articles of Confederation guided the functions of the national government until the enactment of the United States Constitution in 1789; andWHEREAS, The Continental Congress ratified both the Treaty of Amity and Commerce and the Treaty of Alliance with France while 123456789101112131415161718 in session in the York County Courthouse on May 4, 1778; andWHEREAS, These treaties marked the first official recognition by a foreign power of the United States of America as an independent country and helped secure a patriot victory in the American Revolution; andWHEREAS, The Continental Congress adjourned from the York County Courthouse on June 27, 1778, and returned to Independence Hall in Philadelphia after receiving word from General George Washington that the British had abandoned the city; andWHEREAS, November 15, 2025, is the 248th anniversary of the adoption of the Articles of Confederation in the City of York, York County; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate November 15, 2025, as "Articles of Confederation Day" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the City of York, York County, for its important contribution to the forging of our nation.20250HR0342PN2442 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718
