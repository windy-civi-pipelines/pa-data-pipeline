AN ACT
Amending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An
act relating to the public school system, including certain
provisions applicable as well to private and parochial
schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the
laws relating thereto," in high schools, further providing
for assessment of civic knowledge.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 1605.1(c) of the act of March 10, 1949
(P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is
amended and the section is amended by adding a subsection to
read:
Section 1605.1. Assessment of Civic Knowledge.--* * *
(a.1) Beginning in the 2028-2029 school year and each school
year thereafter, in order to graduate from high school or obtain
a general equivalency diploma, a student shall correctly answer
at least sixty per centum (60%) of the questions on the
assessment under subsection (a)(1).
