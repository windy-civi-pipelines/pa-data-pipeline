2025-2026 Regular Session HB 392 PN 0357 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 357 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.392 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY GROVE, STENDER, KUTZ, STAATS AND GILLEN, JANUARY 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON INSURANCE, JANUARY 28, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of May 17, 1921 (P.L.682, No.284), entitled "An act relating to insurance; amending, revising, and consolidating the law providing for the incorporation of insurance companies, and the regulation, supervision, and protection of home and foreign insurance companies, Lloyds associations, reciprocal and inter-insurance exchanges, and fire insurance rating bureaus, and the regulation and supervision of insurance carried by such companies, associations, and exchanges, including insurance carried by the State Workmen's Insurance Fund; providing penalties; and repealing existing laws," in casualty insurance, further providing for billing.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 635.7 of the act of May 17, 1921 (P.L.682, No.284), known as The Insurance Company Law of 1921, is amended by adding subsections to read:Section 635.7. Billing.--* * *(b.1) When an insurer reimburses for a nonnetwork EMS agency emergency medical service and the reimbursement payment is paid directly to an insurance policyholder, the payment shall be accompanied with a notification in writing that includes the following information:1234567891011121314151617181920212223 (1) the date the emergency medical service was performed;(2) the name of the EMS agency that performed the emergency medical service;(3) an itemized explanation of the claim amount and reimbursement amount issued by the insurer;(4) the EMS agency billing address that filed the claim for the emergency medical service performed; and(5) a disclosure that states:This reimbursement payment is issued to the policyholder (insert NAME), for a claim filed for nonnetwork emergency medical services on (insert DATE of emergency service). The reimbursement funds enclosed are to be used for the sole purpose of payment of a liability for emergency medical services outlined in this notification. The use of these funds for any other purpose other than to satisfy the emergency medical services liability may result in an insurance fraud claim against the policyholder or individual that received the emergency medical service on (insert DATE).(b.2) An insurer that reimburses for a nonnetwork EMS agency emergency medical service shall notify the EMS agency monthly that a claim payment has been issued by the insurer to the policyholder for the billing of a medically necessary emergency medical service.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0392PN0357 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122232425
