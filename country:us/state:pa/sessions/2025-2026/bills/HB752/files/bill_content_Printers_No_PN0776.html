AN ACT
Amending Titles 18 (Crimes and Offenses) and 42 (Judiciary and
Judicial Procedure) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in falsification and intimidation, further
providing for failure to comply with 42 Pa.C.S. Ch. 97 Subch.
I registration requirements; and, in sentencing, further
providing for registration.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 4915.2(c) of Title 18 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a paragraph and the
section is amended by adding a subsection to read:
§ 4915.2. Failure to comply with 42 Pa.C.S. Ch. 97 Subch. I
registration requirements.
* * *
(a.2) Additional offense defined.--An individual who is
subject to registration under 42 Pa.C.S. § 9799.55(b)(3) commits
an offense if the individual knowingly violates 42 Pa.C.S. §
9799.55(e).

Section 2. Section 9799.55 of Title 42 is amended by adding

(e) Residency.--
(1) A sexually violent predator may not reside within
2,500 feet of a public school, private school, parochial
school or day-care center for the duration of the required
period of registration under subsection (b).
(2) A sexually violent predator who resides within 2,500
feet of a public school, private school, parochial school or
day-care center on the effective date of this paragraph
shall, within six months after the effective date of this
paragraph or upon the expiration of the lease, rental
agreement or similar contractual obligation regarding the
residence, whichever is later, relocate to another residence
that is situated at least 2,500 feet from a public school,
private school, parochial school or day-care center.

or day-care center opens at a location that is within 2,500
feet of the residence of a sexually violent predator, the
sexually violent predator shall, within six months after the
opening of the public school, private school, parochial
