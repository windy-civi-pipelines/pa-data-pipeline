AN ACT
Amending Title 34 (Game) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in Pennsylvania Game Commission, further providing
for Game Commission officers and employees.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 303(b) of Title 34 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:
303. Game Commission officers and employees.
* * *
(b) Status.--[Except]
(1) Except as provided in paragraph (2) and except for
the director, all personnel shall be employed in accordance
with the classification and compensation plans of the
Commonwealth and regulations of the commission.
(2) Each full-time officer whose duty it is to enforce
this title shall be classified in the same manner as members
of the Pennsylvania State Police for purposes of
