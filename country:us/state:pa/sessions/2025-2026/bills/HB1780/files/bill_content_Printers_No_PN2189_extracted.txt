2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1780 PN 2189 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2189 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1780 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY GUZMAN, HILL-EVANS, GIRAL, WEBSTER, SANCHEZ, PIELLI, WAXMAN, RIVERA, FIEDLER AND CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, AUGUST 1, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON CONSUMER PROTECTION, TECHNOLOGY AND UTILITIES, AUGUST 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 12 (Commerce and Trade) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, providing for transparent payment fees.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 12 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a chapter to read:CHAPTER 58TRANSPARENT PAYMENT FEESSec.6401. Scope of chapter.6402. Definitions.6403. Surcharge disclosure.6404. Enforcement.§ 6401. Scope of chapter.This chapter relates to informing consumers of payment surcharges related to the use of credit cards.1234567891011121314151617 § 6402. Definitions.The following words and phrases when used in this chapter shall have the meanings given to them in this section unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:"Credit card surcharge." A fee charged by a merchant to a consumer exclusively for credit card payment.§ 6403. Surcharge disclosure.(a) Disclosure required.--A merchant located in this Commonwealth, or doing business with a consumer located in this Commonwealth, shall disclose any credit card surcharge prior to the completion of a transaction.(b) Limit on surcharge.--The credit card surcharge may not exceed the merchant's actual cost to process the credit card payment.§ 6404. Enforcement.(a) Penalties.--A violation under this chapter shall be subject to penalties as determined by the Bureau of Consumer Protection in the Office of Attorney General.(b) Deposit of penalty fees.--Any penalty fees collected under this section shall be deposited into the General Fund.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 120 days.20250HB1780PN2189 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021
