2025-2026 Regular Session HR 121 PN 0957 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 957 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.121 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY NEILSON, WAXMAN, GIRAL, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, HILL-EVANS AND D. WILLIAMS, MARCH 13, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, MARCH 13, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing March 18, 2025, as "National Transit Worker Appreciation Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Nearly 1 million Pennsylvanians throughout all 67 counties rely on some form of public transportation each day; andWHEREAS, Every year, Pennsylvania's public transit workers such as bus and train operators, shared-ride drivers and maintenance workers facilitate more than 237 million trips that are crucial for connecting people in their communities to employment and educational opportunities, medical appointments and recreation; andWHEREAS, More than 16,800 dedicated public transit employees throughout this Commonwealth work tirelessly to ensure that our public transit systems are safe, clean, reliable and operate smoothly every single day regardless of weather conditions; andWHEREAS, Through their contributions in providing transportation options to Pennsylvanians, public transit workers support economic growth and prosperity in this Commonwealth, 123456789101112131415161718 access to opportunities and community connectivity and the improved physical and mental well-being of their customers; andWHEREAS, In recent years, modes of public transit have been experiencing increasingly dangerous incidents of violence and other disruptions that put public transit employees at risk; andWHEREAS, "National Transit Worker Appreciation Day" not only demonstrates the importance of acknowledging the essential services that public transit employees deliver every day, it also raises awareness about the need for investing in public transportation infrastructure and supporting the workforce behind it; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize March 18, 2025, as "National Transit Worker Appreciation Day" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage all riders and residents across this Commonwealth to join us in offering sincere thanks to public transit workers for keeping Pennsylvania moving.20250HR0121PN0957 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718
