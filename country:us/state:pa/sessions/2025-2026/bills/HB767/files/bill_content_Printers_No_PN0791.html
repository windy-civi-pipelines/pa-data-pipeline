AN ACT
Amending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in registration of vehicles, providing for Gadsden
flag plate.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
§ 1369.5. Gadsden flag plate.
(a) Application and payment.--Upon application of any
person, accompanied by a fee of $26 which shall be in addition
to the annual registration fee, the department shall issue to
the person a special Gadsden flag registration plate. The
special registration plate may be used only on a passenger car
or truck with a registered gross weight of not more than 14,000
pounds or a motorcycle.
(b) Description of plate.--The plate shall include a
depiction of the Gadsden flag with the words "Don't Tread On
Me." The flag shall be yellow and contain the depiction of a
