2025-2026 Regular Session HB 767 PN 0791 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 791 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.767 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KERWIN, ANDERSON, COOK, DIAMOND, GILLEN, GROVE, HAMM, KAUFFMAN, SMITH, STAMBAUGH AND ROWE, MARCH 3, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TRANSPORTATION, MARCH 3, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 75 (Vehicles) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in registration of vehicles, providing for Gadsden flag plate.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 75 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 1369.5. Gadsden flag plate.(a) Application and payment.--Upon application of any person, accompanied by a fee of $26 which shall be in addition to the annual registration fee, the department shall issue to the person a special Gadsden flag registration plate. The special registration plate may be used only on a passenger car or truck with a registered gross weight of not more than 14,000 pounds or a motorcycle.(b) Description of plate.--The plate shall include a depiction of the Gadsden flag with the words "Don't Tread On Me." The flag shall be yellow and contain the depiction of a 123456789101112131415161718 coiled timber rattlesnake.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 120 days.20250HB0767PN0791 - 2 - 12
