2025-2026 Regular Session HR 326 PN 2343 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 2343 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.326 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KHAN, HILL-EVANS, WAXMAN, NEILSON, PROBST, MAYES, K.HARRIS, BRIGGS, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, HOHENSTEIN, GALLAGHER, SANCHEZ, GREEN AND PARKER, SEPTEMBER 25, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, SEPTEMBER 26, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing December 1, 2025, as "Young Involved Philadelphia Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Young Involved Philadelphia, known as YIP, is celebrating its 25th anniversary on December 1, 2025; andWHEREAS, YIP encourages emerging leadership among young individuals in the city of Philadelphia through participation in civic affairs, engagement in local civic groups and reinforcing communication with local leadership in the city; andWHEREAS, YIP fosters relationships and increases engagement within the community; andWHEREAS, In order to empower young Philadelphians, YIP promotes civic education and advocacy, as well as volunteer opportunities and social events; andWHEREAS, YIP's foundation began in 2000 among a small group of college graduates; andWHEREAS, The first open meeting was held in 2001, and the organization was incorporated as a charitable organization in 1234567891011121314151617 2003; andWHEREAS, At this time, YIP includes more than 7,000 members; andWHEREAS, YIP is known as Philadelphia's leading organization of young individuals, generally 22 to 35 years of age, although all are welcome; andWHEREAS, YIP provides a meaningful contribution to the city of Philadelphia and its young people through civic engagement and advocacy; andWHEREAS, As YIP celebrates its 25th anniversary this year, it is important that we recognize and celebrate YIP's accomplishments; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize December 1, 2025, as "Young Involved Philadelphia Day" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0326PN2343 - 2 - 123456789101112131415
