A RESOLUTION
Recognizing December 1, 2025, as "Young Involved Philadelphia
Day" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Young Involved Philadelphia, known as YIP, is
celebrating its 25th anniversary on December 1, 2025; and
WHEREAS, YIP encourages emerging leadership among young
individuals in the city of Philadelphia through participation in
civic affairs, engagement in local civic groups and reinforcing
communication with local leadership in the city; and
WHEREAS, YIP fosters relationships and increases engagement
within the community; and
WHEREAS, In order to empower young Philadelphians, YIP
promotes civic education and advocacy, as well as volunteer
opportunities and social events; and
WHEREAS, YIP's foundation began in 2000 among a small group
of college graduates; and
WHEREAS, The first open meeting was held in 2001, and the
organization was incorporated as a charitable organization in
