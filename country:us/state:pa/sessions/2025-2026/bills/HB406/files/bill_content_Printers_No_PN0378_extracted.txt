2025-2026 Regular Session HB 406 PN 0378 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 378 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.406 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MARKOSEK, HADDOCK, GIRAL, SANCHEZ, MALAGARI, KENYATTA, DONAHUE, HILL-EVANS, HOWARD, CIRESI, SHUSTERMAN, GREEN, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ AND CERRATO, JANUARY 29, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, JANUARY 29, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 18 (Crimes and Offenses) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in riot, disorderly conduct and related offenses, providing for the offense of kidnapping of animals.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Title 18 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:ยง 5539.1. Kidnapping of animals. (a) Offense defined.--A person commits an offense if the person:(1) intentionally, knowingly or recklessly takes, sells or purchases any dog, cat, equine animal, swine or cattle for resale to another, use in a laboratory or for animal fighting; or(2) intentionally, knowingly or recklessly takes or carries away any dog, cat or equine animal without permission for a special or temporary purpose.123456789101112131415161718 (b) Grading.--An offense under subsection (a) shall be graded as follows:(1) An offense under subsection (a)(1) is a felony of the third degree and a second or subsequent offense under subsection (a)(1) is a felony of the second degree.(2) An offense under subsection (a)(2) is a misdemeanor of the third degree and a second or subsequent offense under subsection (a)(2) is a misdemeanor of the second degree.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250HB0406PN0378 - 2 - 123456789
