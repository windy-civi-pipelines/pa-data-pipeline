2025-2026 Regular Session HR 198 PN 1382 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1382 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.198 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY HILL-EVANS, N. NELSON, SANCHEZ, GUENST, POWELL, NEILSON, RABB AND GIRAL, APRIL 17, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS, APRIL 17, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating May 6, 2025, as "AKA Day at the Capitol" in Pennsylvania in recognition of the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc.WHEREAS, The House of Representatives applauds Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., for their strong advocacy on issues important to the future of this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., is the oldest Greek-letter organization established by African-American, college-educated women, founded January 15, 1908, at Howard University in Washington, DC; andWHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., celebrates 117 years of sisterhood and service to all mankind across the nation in small towns, big cities and throughout the world, as the sorority has an international presence; andWHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., is currently comprised of 360,000 initiated members in graduate and undergraduate chapters located in 12 countries, including the United States, the Bahamas, Bermuda, Canada, Dubai, Germany, 123456789101112131415161718 Japan, Liberia, Nigeria, South Korea, South Africa and the United States Virgin Islands; andWHEREAS, Service programs of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., are centered around significant issues that affect society; andWHEREAS, Under the leadership of Ms. Danette Anthony Reed, 31st International President and the CEO of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., the 2022-2026 International Program has focused on "Soaring to Greater Heights of Service and Sisterhood"; andWHEREAS, Under the International Program, the theme "Soaring to Greater Heights of Service and Sisterhood," sorority programming includes initiatives such as Strengthen Our Sisterhood, Empower Our Families, Build Our Economic Wealth, Enhance Our Environment, Advocate for Social Justice and Uplift Our Local Community; andWHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., consists of 10 regions; andWHEREAS, Members residing in eastern Pennsylvania are members of the North Atlantic Region, which includes eastern Pennsylvania, Connecticut, Delaware, the District of Columbia, eastern New York, Maine, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Rhode Island and Vermont; andWHEREAS, Members residing in western Pennsylvania are members of the Great Lakes Region, which includes western Pennsylvania, Ohio, West Virginia, Michigan and western New York; andWHEREAS, Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., chapters throughout this Commonwealth continue to flourish through members' community service involvement, social and political action and scholastic achievement; andWHEREAS, Program events, service projects and educational 20250HR0198PN1382 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 workshops have expanded access to opportunities for communities across the nation, this Commonwealth and abroad; andWHEREAS, "AKA Day at the Capitol" is an advocacy day that provides sorority members with an opportunity to advocate for initiatives paramount to not only Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., but black women and girls and also strengthens the relationships with the elected officials that serve their local communities; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate May 6, 2025, as "AKA Day at the Capitol" in Pennsylvania in recognition of the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc.20250HR0198PN1382 - 3 - 1234567891011
