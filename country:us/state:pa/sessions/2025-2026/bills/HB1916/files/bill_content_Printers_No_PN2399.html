AN ACT
Amending Title 74 (Transportation) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in turnpike, further providing for
electronic toll collection.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 8117(d.1)(4) of Title 74 of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:
§ 8117. Electronic toll collection.
* * *
(d.1) Notice for posting video toll or flat video toll to
customer account.--
* * *
(4) The commission shall provide for an appeal
process[.] that shall provide customers with not less than
six months from the posting date of the video toll or flat
video toll on the customer's account to file an appeal.
* * *
Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
