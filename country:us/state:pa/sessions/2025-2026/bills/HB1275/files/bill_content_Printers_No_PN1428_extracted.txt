2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1275 PN 1428 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1428 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1275 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. MACKENZIE, APRIL 21, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, APRIL 21, 2025 AN ACTSelecting, designating and adopting the Pennsylvania March as the official march of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The march style of music has had a long history in the United States; andWHEREAS, John Philip Sousa's "The Stars and Stripes Forever" is an early example of American march music that is now an iconic part of our collective history; andWHEREAS, Currently, marches are a fixture of high school, college and university marching bands and many states across this nation have their own official march; andWHEREAS, On September 16, 2018, composer Ron DeGrandis' "Pennsylvania March" was premiered in Allentown by The Marine Band of Allentown; andWHEREAS, DeGrandis said he was inspired by this Commonwealth's diverse cities and landscapes; andWHEREAS, DeGrandis described the march as "...a tribute to honor our glorious state. It features the rhythm of the word Penn-syl-vania throughout the music to tie it together. This 123456789101112131415161718 march is in the style of John Philip Sousa. It is played by a band and is an instrumental piece of music"; andWHEREAS, Prior to this composition, the Commonwealth has never had a tune in the traditional American march genre.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Short title.This act shall be known and may be cited as the Official Pennsylvania March Designation.Section 2. Designation of the official march of the Commonwealth.The Pennsylvania March is selected, designated and adopted as the official march of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.Section 3. Publication in the Pennsylvania Bulletin.The composition of the Pennsylvania March shall be published by the Legislative Reference Bureau in the next available issue of the Pennsylvania Bulletin within 30 days of the effective date of this section.Section 4. Effective date.This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB1275PN1428 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920
