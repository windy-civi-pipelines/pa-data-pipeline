AN ACT
Amending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An
act relating to the public school system, including certain
provisions applicable as well to private and parochial
schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the
laws relating thereto," in certification of teachers,
providing for educator preparation admission.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known
as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a
section to read:
Section 1207.5. Educator Preparation Admission.--(a) A
preparing institution shall recruit and admit applicants who
demonstrate potential for professional success in a public
school by ensuring that each applicant admitted to an initial
preparation program culminating in a bachelor's degree or higher
shall have completed at least forty-eight (48) semester credit
hours or the full-time equivalent of prior college or university
coursework with a minimum grade point average of 2.8.

(b) The criteria for admission to an advanced preparation
program shall include at least the following:
(1) An overall minimum grade point average of 2.8.
(2) Faculty and professional recommendations.
(3) A record of competence and effectiveness in professional
work.
(4) Graduation from a regionally accredited college or
university.
(c) A preparing institution may accept up to ten percent
(10%) of the applicants for admission to the initial preparation
program who do not meet overall minimum GPA requirements under
subsection (a). The criteria for admission of applicants under
this subsection shall be developed and documented by the
preparing institution and must include the requirements in 22
Pa. Code 49.12 (relating to eligibility).
(d) As used in this section, the following words and phrases
shall have the meanings given to them in this subsection unless
the context clearly indicates otherwise:
"Advanced preparation program." As defined in 22 Pa. Code
354.1 (relating to definitions).
"Applicant." As defined in 22 Pa. Code 354.1.
"Initial preparation program." As defined in 22 Pa. Code
354.1.
"Preparing institution." As defined in 22 Pa. Code 354.1.
Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
