AN ACT
Amending Title 35 (Health and Safety) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in 911 emergency communication
services, further providing for uniform 911 surcharge and
providing for termination; and making a repeal.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 5306.2(a) of Title 35 of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes is amended to read:
5306.2. Uniform 911 surcharge.
(a) Surcharge imposed.--Each subscriber or consumer shall
pay a surcharge of [$1.65] $1.95 for each 911 communications
service or prepaid wireless device for which that subscriber or
consumer is billed by a provider or seller as provided for under
this chapter. The surcharge shall be collected apart from and in
addition to a fee levied by the provider or seller, in whole or
in part, for the provision of 911 services. The surcharge shall
be subject to the following:
(1) The surcharge shall be uniform, competitively

(2) Except as provided under section 5307.1 (relating to

The Treasurer may retain up to 1% of the remitted surcharge

(3) No subscriber or consumer shall be required to pay

* * *
Section 2. Title 35 is amended by adding a section to read:
5398.1. Termination.
This chapter shall expire January 31, 2029.
Section 3. Repeals are as follows:
(1) The General Assembly declares that the repeal under
paragraph (2) is necessary to effectuate the amendment or
addition of 35 Pa.C.S. 5306.2(a) and 5398.1.
(2) Article I-K of the act of April 9, 1929 (P.L.343,
No.176), known as The Fiscal Code, is repealed.
Section 4. This act shall take effect in 60 days.
