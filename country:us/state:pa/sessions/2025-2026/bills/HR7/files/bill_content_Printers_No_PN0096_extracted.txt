2025-2026 Regular Session HR 7 PN 0096 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 96 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.7 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MARCELL, LABS, STEHR, CONKLIN, PICKETT, VENKAT, COOPER, M. MACKENZIE, CIRESI, NEILSON, MENTZER, FREEMAN AND REICHARD, JANUARY 14, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON AGING AND OLDER ADULT SERVICES, JANUARY 14, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing August 21, 2025, as "National Senior Citizens Day" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Senior citizens are valuable and esteemed members of communities across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania has become a popular destination for individuals seeking retirement, thus increasing the number of senior citizens residing in this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania has more than 2.5 million residents who are 65 years of age and older and an older adult population growth rate more than 20 times higher than the growth rate of its entire population; andWHEREAS, Senior citizens may face unique barriers and challenges in their daily lives, including isolation, chronic illness and limited income; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania offers crucial services to its aging and older adult population through several State agencies, including the Department of Aging; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, "National Senior Citizens Day" encourages communities to appreciate their senior citizens and raises awareness about the unique needs and challenges that they face; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize August 21, 2025, as "National Senior Citizens Day" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives urge all residents of this Commonwealth to visit or call a loved senior citizen in their life to check in and see that the senior citizen's needs are being met and to offer appreciation for the wisdom and knowledge they offer.20250HR0007PN0096 - 2 - 123456789101112
