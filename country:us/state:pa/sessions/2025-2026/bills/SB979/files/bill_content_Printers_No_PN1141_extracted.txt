2025-2026 Regular Session SB 979 PN 1141 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1141 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIASENATE BILL No.979 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BROWN, CULVER, FONTANA, STEFANO AND MILLER, SEPTEMBER 4, 2025 REFERRED TO URBAN AFFAIRS AND HOUSING, SEPTEMBER 4, 2025 AN ACTAmending Title 53 (Municipalities Generally) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes, in consolidated county assessment, further providing for changes in assessed valuation.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. Section 8817(a) of Title 53 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes is amended and the section is amended by adding a subsection to read:ยง 8817. Changes in assessed valuation.(a) General rule.--[In] Subject to subsection (a.1), in addition to other authorization provided in this chapter, the assessors may change the assessed valuation on real property when a parcel of land is subdivided into smaller parcels or when improvements exceeding 20% of the most recent assessed value of real property are made to real property or existing improvements are removed from real property or are destroyed. The recording of a subdivision plan shall not constitute grounds for assessment increases until lots are sold or improvements 123456789101112131415161718 exceeding 20% of the most recent assessed value of real property are installed. The painting of a building or the normal regular repairs to a building shall not be deemed cause for a change in valuation by the assessors under the authority of this section.(a.1) Limitation.--Assessors may not assess real property when improvements are made to real property if:(1) the improvements are equal to or less than 20% of the most recent assessed value of the real property;(2) the real property is the primary residence of the owner of the real property for a period of at least five years from the date improvements were installed; and(3) this is the only time during the real property owner's ownership of the real property when improvements equal to or less than 20% of the most recent assessed value of the real property are made.* * *Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.20250SB0979PN1141 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617
