2025-2026 Regular Session HR 11 PN 0147 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 147 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.11 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY LABS, JANUARY 16, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, JANUARY 16, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of June 2025 as "Lipedema Awareness Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Lipedema is a chronic medical condition characterized by a symmetric buildup of adipose tissue in the legs and arms; andWHEREAS, Lipedema almost exclusively affects women and is known to start or worsen during puberty or other periods of hormonal changes, such as pregnancy and menopause; andWHEREAS, Lipedema may cause muscle pain, tenderness, swelling, easy bruising and fatigue; andWHEREAS, Sometimes the presence of nodular and fibrotic texture beneath the skin can create an uneven, dimpled appearance; andWHEREAS, There are no definitive diagnostic tests for lipedema; andWHEREAS, The causes of the condition are not well understood, making it difficult to diagnose; andWHEREAS, Lipedema is commonly misdiagnosed as lymphedema or 123456789101112131415161718 obesity, though symptoms are resistant to dietary interventions and exercise; andWHEREAS, Lipedema can have a negative impact on an individual's mental health and quality of life, leading to lack of energy, feelings of hopelessness, low self-esteem or eating disorders; andWHEREAS, While there is no known cure for lipedema, there are various care and treatment options available to manage symptoms and improve quality of life; andWHEREAS, Providers commonly advise daily light to moderate exercise in combination with an anti-inflammatory diet for lipedema patients; andWHEREAS, The Lipedema Foundation is a nonprofit that supports collaborative research to address the basic biology, genetics and epidemiology of lipedema, with the goal of improving the treatment landscape for everyone with this condition; andWHEREAS, Increased public awareness of lipedema is needed to improve diagnosis and treatment; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of June 2025 as "Lipedema Awareness Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives encourage continued research and support for those living with lipedema.20250HR0011PN0147 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
