andWHEREAS, Eagles quarterback Jalen Hurts put in one of the best performances of his career, and for his efforts, he was named Super Bowl MVP; andWHEREAS, The fifth-year quarterback looked assured and calm under the bright lights, going 17-for-22 passes for 221 yards, two touchdowns and only one interception and, as a runner, 11 1234567891011121314 carries for 72 rushing yards and one touchdown; andWHEREAS, Saquon Barkley had a great game as he ended up in the record books after surpassing Professional Football Hall of Famer Terrell Davis' 2,476 rushing yards combined between the regular season and postseason; andWHEREAS, Eagles rookie Cooper DeJean became the first player in NFL history to score a touchdown in a Super Bowl on his birthday as the defensive back's first career interception resulted in a return for a touchdown on his 22nd birthday; andWHEREAS, The entire team contributed to the Eagles' dominant victory with running back Saquon Barkley having 25 carries for 57 yards, wide receiver DeVonta Smith having 4 receptions for 69 yards and 1 touchdown, wide receiver A.J. Brown having 3 receptions for 43 yards and 1 touchdown, defensive tackle Milton Williams having 3 tackles and 2 sacks, linebacker Zack Baun having 3 tackles and 1 interception, defensive back Cooper DeJean having 3 tackles and 1 interception and defensive end Josh Sweat having 2 tackles and 2.5 sacks; andWHEREAS, The Eagles had the better roster and outcoached the Chiefs on the biggest stage; andWHEREAS, Philadelphia's defense was the best in the league during the regular season and postseason; andWHEREAS, This postseason, the Eagles rushed for 817 yards, an 20250HR0217PN1526 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 NFL record and a testament to their dominance; andWHEREAS, They scored 145 points in a single postseason, which is also an NFL record; andWHEREAS, The House of Representatives congratulates the Philadelphia Eagles on their many achievements on the way to their Super Bowl victory; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize May 7, 2025, as "Philadelphia Eagles Day at the Capitol" in Pennsylvania and honor the 2024 Philadelphia Eagles as the Super Bowl LIX Champions.20250HR0217PN1526 - 3 - 12345678910
