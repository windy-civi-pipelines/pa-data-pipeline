2025-2026 Regular Session HB 415 PN 0391 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 391 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.415 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY TOMLINSON, KHAN, MARCELL, SANCHEZ, CIRESI, SCHLOSSBERG, SAMUELSON AND GREEN, JANUARY 30, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HUMAN SERVICES, JANUARY 30, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), entitled "An act creating the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources consisting of certain functions of the Department of Environmental Resources and the Department of Community Affairs; renaming the Department of Environmental Resources as the Department of Environmental Protection; defining the role of the Environmental Quality Board in the Department of Environmental Protection; making changes to responsibilities of the State Conservation Commission and the Department of Agriculture; transferring certain powers and duties to the Department of Health; and repealing inconsistent acts," in Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, providing for suicide prevention.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of June 28, 1995 (P.L.89, No.18), known as the Conservation and Natural Resources Act, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 323. Suicide prevention.The department shall prominently post signage in all State parks and State forests in visitor entrances, parking lots and highly trafficked areas providing for information regarding suicide awareness and the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline . 1234567891011121314151617181920212223 Section 2.
