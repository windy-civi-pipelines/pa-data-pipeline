2025-2026 Regular Session HR 212 PN 1524 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1524 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.212 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY BRENNAN, VENKAT, PROBST, MERSKI, HILL-EVANS, WAXMAN, SANCHEZ, CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, GUENST, GIRAL AND DONAHUE, APRIL 28, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON LOCAL GOVERNMENT, APRIL 28, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the week of May 18 through 24, 2025, as "Public Works Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Public works professionals work behind the scenes to make our communities healthier and safer and improve the overall quality of life of residents in this Commonwealth and across the nation; andWHEREAS, Public works professionals are comprised of individuals from State or local agencies and the engineers, consultants, contractors, inspectors and vendors who work for those agencies; andWHEREAS, Public works professionals are the individuals who make our quality of life better and improve the communities in which we live by providing core services such as planning, building, managing and operating public works systems; andWHEREAS, Public works professionals are on call day and night and are often the first individuals to report to the scene of an emergency and among the last individuals to leave; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Streets, sewer systems, water systems, building maintenance, wastewater treatment, solid waste collection, airport operations and many other amenities and services within local communities are maintained behind the scenes by public works professionals; andWHEREAS, These individuals' services are often needed most in times of natural disasters like hurricanes and severe snowstorms; andWHEREAS, Public works professionals also work alongside first responders like firefighters and police officers to clear evacuation routes from dangerous areas or help facilitate access to emergency areas for such individuals; andWHEREAS, This year marks the 65th annual commemoration of "National Public Works Week" sponsored by the American Public Works Association, which continues to promote an understanding of and appreciation for the role public works projects play in sustaining economic growth and vitality; andWHEREAS, The theme for "National Public Works Week" in 2025 is "People, Purpose, Presence"; andWHEREAS, This Commonwealth recognizes the contributions of public works professionals and extends sincere appreciation for their commitment to excellence and efforts to improve and enhance the health, safety, welfare and prosperity of the Keystone State; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the week of May 18 through 24, 2025, as "Public Works Week" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives honor the contributions and accomplishments made by public works professionals across this Commonwealth and the nation and 20250HR0212PN1524 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 encourage all residents to give the same recognition.20250HR0212PN1524 - 3 - 1
