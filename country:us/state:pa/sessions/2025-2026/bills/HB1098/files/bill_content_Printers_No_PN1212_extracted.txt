2025-2026 Regular Session HB 1098 PN 1212 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1212 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.1098 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY ORTITAY, BENHAM, COOK, MADDEN, SANCHEZ, GAYDOS AND RIVERA, APRIL 1, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY, APRIL 1, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of November 22, 1978 (P.L.1166, No.274), entitled "An act establishing the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency, providing for its powers and duties establishing several advisory committees within the commission and providing for their powers and duties," providing for Investigation and Prosecution of Internet Crimes Against Children Program.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of November 22, 1978 (P.L.1166, No.274), referred to as the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency Law, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 7.3. Investigation and Prosecution of Internet Crimes Against Children Program.(a) Establishment.--The Investigation and Prosecution of Internet Crimes Against Children Program is established as a program within the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency.(b) Use.--Money available to the Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency for the Investigation and Prosecution of 1234567891011121314151617181920 Internet Crimes Against Children Program shall be used for the following purposes:(1) to provide grants to law enforcement agencies to establish or support task forces of State or local investigators and prosecutors, which may include forensic specialists and education specialists, to investigate and prosecute violations of 18 Pa.C.S. Ch. 31 (relating to sexual offenses) committed on the Internet against individuals under 18 years of age;(2) to provide grants to task forces which receive funding under 34 U.S.C. Ch. 211 Subch. I (relating to national strategy for child exploitation prevention and interdiction) to supplement Federal funding received under 34 U.S.C. ยง 21116 (relating to ICAC grant program); or(3) to provide grants for the purpose of training members of task forces under paragraphs (1) and (2) in the best practices of detecting, investigating and prosecuting violations of 18 Pa.C.S. Ch. 31 committed on the Internet against individuals under 18 years of age or the purposes of a task force under 34 U.S.C. ยง 21113 (relating to purpose of ICAC task forces).(c) Guidelines.--The Pennsylvania Commission on Crime and Delinquency shall adopt guidelines for the administration of the Investigation and Prosecution of Internet Crimes Against Children Program and a scoring process for the awarding of grants, which shall include consideration of geographical dispersion of task forces under subsection (b).(d) Funding.--Grants received under the Investigation and Prosecution of Internet Crimes Against Children Program shall be in addition to funding under 34 U.S.C. Ch. 211 Subch. I or any 20250HB1098PN1212 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 other Federal or State funding.Section 2. This act shall take effect immediately.20250HB1098PN1212 - 3 - 12
