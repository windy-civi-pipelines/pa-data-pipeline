2025-2026 Regular Session HR 194 PN 1434 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 1434 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.194 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY MIHALEK, SHUSTERMAN, ROWE, SCIALABBA, O'MARA, MAJOR, FLEMING, HOGAN, WARNER, KAIL, LABS, LEADBETER, GLEIM, BOROWSKI, BENHAM, METZGAR, SAPPEY, GIRAL, SANCHEZ, VENKAT, KULIK, GAYDOS, NEILSON, OTTEN, RIVERA, GREEN, KERWIN, WEBSTER, COOPER AND PUGH, APRIL 22, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, APRIL 22, 2025 A RESOLUTIONRecognizing the month of August 2025 as "National Wellness Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, The health and well-being of the residents of Pennsylvania are vital to the prosperity and quality of life in this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Wellness is an active process of awareness and consistent choices toward a healthy and fulfilling life, encompassing physical, mental and spiritual health; andWHEREAS, Nurturing the mind, body and spirit contributes to overall well-being and can prevent the onset of chronic diseases and other health conditions; andWHEREAS, Healthy eating plays a crucial role in maintaining optimal health, providing essential nutrients that support bodily functions and reduce the risk of disease; andWHEREAS, Engaging in regular physical activity, practicing mindfulness, fostering social connections and adhering to a 12345678910111213141516 balanced diet are integral components of a holistic approach to wellness; andWHEREAS, "National Wellness Month" is observed annually in August to promote and encourage healthy habits that can lead to a more balanced and fulfilling life; andWHEREAS, Recognizing the month of August 2025 as "National Wellness Month" in Pennsylvania raises awareness about the importance of comprehensive wellness practices and encourages residents to take proactive steps toward improving their health; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives recognize the month of August 2025 as "National Wellness Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That residents of this Commonwealth be encouraged to focus on nurturing their mind, body and spirit through activities, including healthy eating, regular physical exercise, stress management and engaging in community and social activities; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That employers, health care providers, educators and community organizations be urged to promote resources and programs that support comprehensive wellness initiatives throughout the month of August and beyond.20250HR0194PN1434 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
