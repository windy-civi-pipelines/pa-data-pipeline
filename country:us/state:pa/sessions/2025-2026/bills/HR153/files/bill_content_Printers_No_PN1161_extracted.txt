2025-2026 Regular Session HR 153 PN 1161 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1161 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.153 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY SANCHEZ, MUNROE, VENKAT, MALAGARI, MAYES, GREINER, FREEMAN, NEILSON, GIRAL, HILL-EVANS, KHAN, GUENST, RIVERA, O'MARA, MENTZER AND CEPEDA-FREYTIZ, MARCH 27, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON STATE GOVERNMENT, MARCH 27, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the week of November 16 through 22, 2025, as "County Row Office Employee Appreciation Week" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, In November 1682, William Penn established Bucks County, Chester County and Philadelphia County as the first three counties in Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, These three counties have since expanded into the 67 counties Pennsylvania has today, which collectively employ over 90,000 individuals across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, While Federal and state governments may receive more attention due to the scale of their work, county government employees in Pennsylvania regularly perform critical tasks for the Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, In Pennsylvania, county employees are charged with many vital duties relating to the safety of Pennsylvania residents; andWHEREAS, Many of these employees work in departments run by independent elected officials, known as county row offices, and 1234567891011121314151617 carry out key administrative functions of the county that often go overlooked; andWHEREAS, One example is that every county in Pennsylvania has an elected sheriff to assist with law enforcement, the transportation of defendants and the protection of county buildings; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania counties also have prothonotary offices to file documents and records relating to civil court proceedings, including divorce hearings, landlord and tenant disputes and name changes; andWHEREAS, In criminal court proceedings, county clerk of courts offices help maintain and process records and documents relating to criminal or juvenile court cases; andWHEREAS, Each county's district attorney and their staff also play a key role in criminal court proceedings by addressing crime prosecution and prevention while working alongside various levels of law enforcement; andWHEREAS, County row offices also play a key role in counties' fiscal and budgetary affairs, as county treasurers work with their staffs to collect real estate taxes and other grants and fees; andWHEREAS, Further, county controllers' offices work to maintain counties' fiscal and accounting records while ensuring the compliance of records with current accounting standards; and WHEREAS, Another county row office is that of the coroner, who works with a team of medicolegal investigators to certify causes of deaths in their respective counties to compile death certificates; andWHEREAS, County clerk of orphans' court offices work to file and maintain petitions for guardianship and adoptions and are 20250HR0153PN1161 - 2 - 123456789101112131415161718192021222324252627282930 often run by the same individual as the county register of wills office, which issues marriage licenses and probates wills; andWHEREAS, County row offices in Pennsylvania also provide services like the issuance of marriage, hunting, fishing and dog licenses; andWHEREAS, The work done by county row office public service employees should be recognized for all the vital services they provide for the residents of Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, Pennsylvania should honor its county row office employees for everything they do for our communities while also celebrating the anniversary of when our first counties were founded; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the week of November 16 through 22, 2025, as "County Row Office Employee Appreciation Week" in Pennsylvania.20250HR0153PN1161 - 3 - 123456789101112131415
