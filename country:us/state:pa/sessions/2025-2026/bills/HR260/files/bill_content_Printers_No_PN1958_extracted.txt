2025-2026 Regular Session HR 260 PN 1958 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 1958 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.260 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY KERWIN, ANDERSON, BANTA, CONKLIN, COOK, GREINER, GUENST, HADDOCK, JAMES, KAUFFMAN, LABS, M. MACKENZIE, MARCELL, McNEILL, MENTZER, MERSKI, PICKETT, PIELLI, POWELL, PROBST, RABB, REICHARD, RIGBY, SCHMITT, STAATS, VENKAT AND GILLEN, JUNE 17, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON VETERANS AFFAIRS AND EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS, JUNE 17, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating September 25, 2025, as "Disabled American Veterans Day" in Pennsylvania in recognition of the men and women who have borne the wounds of war, and the unwavering advocacy and support of the Disabled American Veterans.WHEREAS, Founded in 1920 and chartered by Congress in 1932, the Disabled American Veterans (DAV) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to delivering comprehensive services on behalf of our nation's veterans with disabilities and their families, ensuring the fulfillment of basic promises; andWHEREAS, DAV assists with United States Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) claims and appeals, ensuring access to earned benefits; andWHEREAS, The DAV Transportation Network offers critical no-cost rides to VA medical appointments, ensuring access to health care; andWHEREAS, DAV employment and volunteer programs and outreach events foster engagement in rural and metropolitan areas alike, 1234567891011121314151617 ensuring access to local resources and partnerships; andWHEREAS, DAV volunteers and service officers exemplify the commitment to leaving no veteran behind; andWHEREAS, Their life-changing support reaches more than 1 million veterans nationwide each year; andWHEREAS, Veterans with disabilities who reside in this Commonwealth have made profound sacrifices in service to our nation; andWHEREAS, "Disabled American Veterans Day" honors them, raises public awareness of their challenges and highlights DAV's impact; andWHEREAS, "Disabled American Veterans Day" permits us to reflect on the contributions, hardships and sacrifices of veterans with disabilities; andWHEREAS, "Disabled American Veterans Day" permits us to renew our commitment to the critical support we owe them; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate September 25, 2025, as "Disabled American Veterans Day" in Pennsylvania in recognition of the men and women who have borne the wounds of war, and the unwavering advocacy and support of the Disabled American Veterans.20250HR0260PN1958 - 2 - 12345678910111213141516171819202122
