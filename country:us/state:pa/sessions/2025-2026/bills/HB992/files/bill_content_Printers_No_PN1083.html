AN ACT
Amending Title 30 (Fish) of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes, in Pennsylvania Fish and Boat Commission, further
providing for Boating Advisory Board.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Section 306(a) and (c) of Title 30 of the
Pennsylvania Consolidated Statutes are amended to read:
§ 306. Boating Advisory Board.
(a) Composition.--There is hereby continued within the
commission a Boating Advisory Board. The board shall consist of
the Secretary of [Environmental Resources, or his designee, the
executive director of the commission and the assistant executive
director of the commission in charge of watercraft safety,]
Conservation and Natural Resources, or a designee of the
Secretary of Conservation and Natural Resources, and the two at-
large members of the commission as described in section 301(b)
(relating to organization of commission), all of whom shall be
ex officio members, and five volunteer members to be appointed
