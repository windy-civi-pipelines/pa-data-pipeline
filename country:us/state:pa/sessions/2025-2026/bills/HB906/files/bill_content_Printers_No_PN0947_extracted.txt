2025-2026 Regular Session HB 906 PN 0947 Bill Text (HTM) See other bills under thesame topic PRINTER'S NO. 947 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE BILL No.906 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. MACKENZIE, HAMM, ROAE, KAUFFMAN, STEHR, KENYATTA AND ZIMMERMAN, MARCH 13, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION, MARCH 13, 2025 AN ACTAmending the act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), entitled "An act relating to the public school system, including certain provisions applicable as well to private and parochial schools; amending, revising, consolidating and changing the laws relating thereto," in school watch, providing for notice of financial information to resident taxpayers.The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania hereby enacts as follows:Section 1. The act of March 10, 1949 (P.L.30, No.14), known as the Public School Code of 1949, is amended by adding a section to read:Section 604-B. Notice of financial information to resident taxpayers.Beginning with the financial information posted on the department's publicly accessible Internet website under section 603-B(a) pertaining to the 2024-2025 fiscal year and each fiscal year thereafter, a public school entity shall provide a notice, via first class mail, of the financial information posted for the public school entity to each resident taxpayer.Section 2. This act shall take effect in 60 days.1234567891011121314151617181920
