2025-2026 Regular Session HR 353 PN 2492 Bill Text (HTM) PRINTER'S NO. 2492 THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF PENNSYLVANIAHOUSE RESOLUTION No.353 Session of 2025 INTRODUCED BY M. BROWN, VITALI, WATRO, PASHINSKI, OLSOMMER, GALLAGHER, SCHMITT, RIVERA AND FRANKEL, OCTOBER 21, 2025 REFERRED TO COMMITTEE ON TOURISM, RECREATION AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, OCTOBER 21, 2025 A RESOLUTIONDesignating the month of October 2025 as "Italian and Italian American History and Culture Month" in Pennsylvania.WHEREAS, Italians and Italian Americans have played an important role in the history of Pennsylvania; andWHEREAS, During the late 19th and early 20th centuries, many Italians immigrated to Pennsylvania and settled in various regions across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, From the streets of the Italian Market in South Philadelphia to western Pennsylvania, Italians and Italian Americans have made significant contributions culturally, socially and economically to this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, While larger cities had the draw of better opportunities, many Italians made their homes in smaller and more industrialized towns and communities across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Italian-American communities across Pennsylvania represent a rich ethnic identity that is still seen today; and1234567891011121314151617 WHEREAS, Organizations across this Commonwealth honor the historic journey of Italian immigrants and their role in shaping this Commonwealth and the nation; andWHEREAS, From the Italian American Program at the Heinz History Center in Pittsburgh to the various Pennsylvania Sons and Daughters of Italy chapters located across this Commonwealth, organizations and their members play a crucial role in preserving and honoring the history of Italian immigrants across this Commonwealth; andWHEREAS, Since 1989, the month of October has been recognized nationally as "Italian American Heritage Month" to promote awareness of Italian culture, traditions and history; therefore be itRESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the month of October 2025 as "Italian and Italian American History and Culture Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it furtherRESOLVED, That, in celebration of the enduring legacy of Italians and Italian Americans, all Pennsylvanians be encouraged to celebrate and observe the achievements, traditions, history and culture during "Italian and Italian American History and Culture Month" by participating in community events and engaging in meaningful celebrations that highlight and show appreciation for Italian heritage in this Commonwealth.20250HR0353PN2492 - 2 - 1234567891011121314151617181920212223
