A RESOLUTION
Designating the month of October 2025 as "Italian and Italian
American History and Culture Month" in Pennsylvania.
WHEREAS, Italians and Italian Americans have played an
important role in the history of Pennsylvania; and
WHEREAS, During the late 19th and early 20th centuries, many
Italians immigrated to Pennsylvania and settled in various
regions across this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, From the streets of the Italian Market in South
Philadelphia to western Pennsylvania, Italians and Italian
Americans have made significant contributions culturally,
socially and economically to this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, While larger cities had the draw of better
opportunities, many Italians made their homes in smaller and
more industrialized towns and communities across this
Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, Italian-American communities across Pennsylvania
represent a rich ethnic identity that is still seen today; and

WHEREAS, Organizations across this Commonwealth honor the
historic journey of Italian immigrants and their role in shaping
this Commonwealth and the nation; and
WHEREAS, From the Italian American Program at the Heinz
History Center in Pittsburgh to the various Pennsylvania Sons
and Daughters of Italy chapters located across this
Commonwealth, organizations and their members play a crucial
role in preserving and honoring the history of Italian
immigrants across this Commonwealth; and
WHEREAS, Since 1989, the month of October has been recognized
nationally as "Italian American Heritage Month" to promote
awareness of Italian culture, traditions and history; therefore
be it
RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives designate the
month of October 2025 as "Italian and Italian American History
and Culture Month" in Pennsylvania; and be it further
RESOLVED, That, in celebration of the enduring legacy of
Italians and Italian Americans, all Pennsylvanians be encouraged
to celebrate and observe the achievements, traditions, history
and culture during "Italian and Italian American History and
Culture Month" by participating in community events and engaging
in meaningful celebrations that highlight and show appreciation
for Italian heritage in this Commonwealth.
