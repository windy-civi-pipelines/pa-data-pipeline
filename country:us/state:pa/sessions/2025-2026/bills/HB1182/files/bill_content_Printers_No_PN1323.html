AN ACT
Amending Title 23 (Domestic Relations) of the Pennsylvania
Consolidated Statutes, in support matters generally,
providing for support of unborn child.
The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania
hereby enacts as follows:
Section 1. Title 23 of the Pennsylvania Consolidated
Statutes is amended by adding a section to read:
4328. Support of unborn child.
(a) Obligations.--A biological mother of an unborn child may
commence a support action or proceeding under this chapter for
support from the biological father of the unborn child. A court
shall order and enforce unborn child support obligations in
accordance with the biological mother's requested support and
the financial ability of the biological father to pay for the
support. The start date for unborn child support obligations
shall commence on the date of the first prenatal medical visit
of a biological mother by a physician .
(b) Paternity.-- A support action or proceeding under this

(c) Accrual of payments.--A court may order that payments

(d) Applicability.--This section shall not apply to a

other fertility treatment of a biological mother .
(e) Termination of support.--A court may terminate unborn
child support upon the determination by a physician that a
biological mother is no longer pregnant or upon the
determination by a paternity test that the biological father who
is a party in the s upport action or proceeding under this
chapter is not the biological father .
(f) Definitions.--As used in this section, the following
words and phrases shall have the meanings given to them in this
subsection unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:
"A rtificial insemination." The deliberate introduction of
sperm into a biological female's uterine cavity for the purpose
of achieving pregnancy.
"Biological father." A male genetic contributor to the
creation of an unborn child who inherits the contributor's DNA.
"Biological mother." A female genetic contributor to the
creation of an unborn child who inherits the contributor's DNA.
